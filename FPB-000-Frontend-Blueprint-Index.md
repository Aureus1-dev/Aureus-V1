FPB-000 — Frontend Blueprint Index

Status: Master Production Index
Version: 1.0
Authority: Frontend Blueprint Navigation Document

---

1. Purpose

This document is the master index for the Aureus Frontend Production Blueprint.

Every frontend implementation begins here.

Its purpose is to organize the production blueprint into one coherent engineering system and establish the recommended reading and implementation order.

---

2. Relationship to the Aureus Canon

The frontend blueprint does not replace the Aureus Constitution or the Aureus Canons.

The hierarchy is:

1. OAS Constitution
2. Architecture Canons
3. Backend Canons
4. AI Canons
5. Frontend Experience Canons (AFX)
6. Frontend Production Blueprints (FPB)
7. Work Orders
8. Source Code

Every implementation shall conform to every level above it.

---

4. Engineering Workflow

Every frontend work order shall follow this sequence:

Understand the governing canons.

Review the relevant blueprint documents.

Identify backend dependencies.

Implement vertically.

Test automatically.

Test manually.

Verify accessibility.

Verify member experience.

Verify constitutional compliance.

Only then is the work considered complete.

---

5. Decision Authority

When questions arise during implementation:

The Constitution governs first.

The Canons govern second.

The Blueprint governs implementation.

Work Orders govern execution.

If conflicts occur, the higher authority prevails.

---

6. Blueprint Maintenance

Blueprints are living engineering documents.

Changes shall:

- preserve constitutional principles,
- document rationale,
- identify affected documents,
- and maintain consistency across the blueprint.

Architectural drift shall be corrected rather than accepted.

---

7. Success Definition

The purpose of the frontend blueprint is not simply to produce software.

Its purpose is to produce a trustworthy institution.

Engineering success shall be measured by:

- Member trust
- Member understanding
- Member capability
- Member flourishing
- System reliability
- Institutional consistency

---

8. Final Principle

Every engineer, designer, AI agent, reviewer, and future contributor shall remember:

We are not building screens.

We are not building conversations.

We are not building software.

We are building an institution that exists to strengthen people.

Every implementation decision shall reflect that responsibility.

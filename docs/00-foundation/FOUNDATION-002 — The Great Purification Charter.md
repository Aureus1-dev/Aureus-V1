# FOUNDATION-002
# The Great Purification Charter

Status: Permanent
Authority: Constitutional

---

# Purpose

The purpose of the Great Purification is not to reinvent Aureus.

Its purpose is to align every part of Aureus with the Foundation.

Nothing survives because it already exists.

Everything survives because it faithfully serves the Foundation.

---

# Method

Every document, policy, design, feature, workflow, process, and line of guidance shall be audited.

Each receives exactly one outcome.

## KEEP

The material aligns with the Foundation.

No change is required.

---

## REPAIR

The intent is faithful.

The implementation is not.

Rewrite the implementation while preserving the faithful intent.

---

## REMOVE

The material contradicts the Foundation.

It shall be removed.

No historical investment, effort, preference, or attachment justifies keeping what no longer serves the mission.

---

# Rules

The Foundation always outranks implementation.

Reality gets a vote.

Truth is welcomed.

Complexity belongs inside the institution.

Clarity belongs at its edges.

Every improvement must increase coherence.

Every removal must increase clarity.

Every addition must strengthen the Foundation.

---

# Stewardship Standard

Stewards do not defend old work.

Stewards faithfully improve it.

The mission matters more than personal attachment.

The truth matters more than tradition.

The future matters more than comfort.

---

# Completion

The Great Purification is complete only when every governing document, product document, engineering document, policy, and work order aligns with FOUNDATION-001.

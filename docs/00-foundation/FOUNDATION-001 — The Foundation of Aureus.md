# FOUNDATION-001
# The Foundation of Aureus

Status: Eternal
Authority: Highest
Supersedes: None

---

# Purpose

Aureus exists to faithfully steward everything entrusted to it so that people may flourish, forever.

Everything we build, teach, govern, design, and operate exists in service of this purpose.

---

# First Principles

We affirm the following truths as the permanent foundation of Aureus.

## 1. Human Dignity

Every person possesses inherent dignity.

Worth is never determined by wealth, productivity, status, intelligence, ability, belief, background, or usefulness.

Every person deserves to be treated with love, respect, honesty, and compassion.

---

## 2. Stewardship

Nothing truly belongs to us.

Everything entrusted to us carries responsibility.

People, knowledge, resources, technology, institutions, authority, and opportunity are gifts to be faithfully stewarded rather than possessed.

---

## 3. Truth

Truth is never the enemy of Aureus.

We will seek truth wherever it leads.

When we are wrong, we will admit it.

When we discover a better way, we will change.

When harm has been caused, we will repair what we responsibly can.

---

## 4. Flourishing

The purpose of stewardship is flourishing.

Flourishing includes safety, belonging, health, meaningful work, learning, purpose, healthy relationships, contribution, hope, and the freedom to pursue one's calling.

---

## 5. Agency

Aureus exists to strengthen human agency, never replace it.

Technology serves people.

People do not serve technology.

Our goal is capable, flourishing human beings—not dependence.

---

## 6. Community

People flourish together.

Healthy relationships, families, neighborhoods, organizations, and communities are essential to enduring flourishing.

Whenever possible, Aureus strengthens human connection rather than replacing it.

---

## 7. Future Generations

Every generation receives Aureus in trust.

Every generation bears responsibility to strengthen it before passing it forward.

No generation owns Aureus.

---

# The Seven Questions

Before any decision, Aureus asks:

1. Does this help people flourish?
2. Will it continue helping them flourish tomorrow?
3. Does it protect human dignity and freedom?
4. Does it cultivate faithful stewardship?
5. Does it strengthen real human relationships?
6. Can it be explained honestly and simply?
7. Would we proudly hand this to the next generation?

---

# Promise

If any future document, product, policy, feature, process, or decision conflicts with this Foundation, this Foundation prevails.

Everything else must change.

Never this.

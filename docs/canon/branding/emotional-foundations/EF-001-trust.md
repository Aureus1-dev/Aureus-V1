# EF-001 — Trust

Canonical Designation: EF-001

Document Family: Aureus Emotional Foundations

Status: Living Draft

Version: 0.1

Purpose:
This document establishes trust as the first emotional foundation of Aureus. It defines what trust means within the institution, how trust is created and preserved, how it should be expressed through design and service, what destroys it, and how Aureus shall evaluate whether members genuinely experience it.

Relationship to Canon:
This document is governed by BRAND-001 — Brand Constitution and shall be interpreted consistently with BRAND-002 through BRAND-012. It informs every Aureus product, service, communication, interface, AI interaction, stewardship relationship, recommendation, policy, and institutional experience.

Living Draft Notice:
This document is an operational Living Draft. Its principles shall guide real implementation while its hypotheses, expressions, and measurements are tested through member experience, accessibility evaluation, ethical research, operational evidence, and institutional learning.

---

# Article I — Foundational Role

Trust is the first emotional foundation of Aureus.

Before a member can receive guidance, share honestly, accept support, act upon a recommendation, or develop a lasting relationship with the institution, the member must believe that Aureus is worthy of confidence.

Trust cannot be demanded.

Trust cannot be declared into existence.

Trust must be earned through repeated demonstrations of honesty, competence, consistency, care, fairness, and accountability.

Every Aureus interaction either strengthens, preserves, weakens, or breaks trust.

No interaction is emotionally neutral.

---

# Article II — Definition

Within Aureus, trust is the reasonable confidence that the institution:

- Tells the truth.
- Acts in the member’s legitimate interest.
- Protects the member’s dignity.
- Handles information responsibly.
- Explains important decisions.
- Acknowledges uncertainty and limitations.
- Does what it says it will do.
- Corrects mistakes openly and responsibly.
- Applies its principles consistently.
- Will not exploit vulnerability, urgency, confusion, dependence, or hardship.

Trust is not blind belief.

Trust is confidence supported by evidence, conduct, and experience.

---

# Article III — Why Trust Is Essential

Trust enables every other emotional foundation.

Without trust:

- Hope may feel like an empty promise.
- Peace may feel artificial.
- Personalization may feel intrusive.
- Guidance may feel controlling.
- Hospitality may feel performative.
- Love may feel manipulative.
- Stewardship may feel paternalistic.
- Technology may feel threatening.
- Institutional authority may feel unsafe.

Trust creates the conditions in which a member can be honest, receive help, take meaningful action, and develop confidence in both Aureus and themselves.

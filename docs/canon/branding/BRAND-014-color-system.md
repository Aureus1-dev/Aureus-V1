# BRAND-014 — Aureus Color System

Status: Living Draft

---

# Purpose

This document establishes the constitutional philosophy governing color throughout Aureus.

Color exists to support clarity, emotional well-being, accessibility, and human flourishing.

Color shall never be used to manipulate attention, manufacture urgency, or encourage addictive behavior.

---

# Philosophy

Color communicates emotion before words.

Every color within Aureus should contribute to an environment that feels calm, trustworthy, welcoming, and hopeful.

The color system should feel timeless rather than fashionable.

---

# Emotional Objectives

The Aureus palette should reinforce:

• Trust
• Hope
• Peace
• Hospitality
• Human Dignity
• Stewardship
• Belonging

No color choice should intentionally create anxiety, fear, or unnecessary urgency.

---

# Design Principles

The Aureus palette should be:

• Calm
• Warm
• Natural
• Accessible
• Consistent
• Durable over time

The interface should feel restful rather than stimulating.

---

# Primary Palette Philosophy

The primary palette should draw inspiration from the natural world.

Examples include:

• Morning light
• Sand
• Stone
• Linen
• Clay
• Forests
• Gardens
• Wood
• Water
• Sky

The environment should feel lived-in rather than manufactured.

---

# Accent Philosophy

Gold symbolizes value.

It should not dominate the experience.

Gold should be reserved for moments that represent:

• Achievement
• Milestones
• Recognition
• Stewardship
• Long-term value

Its restraint increases its meaning.

---

# Accessibility

Every color decision shall prioritize readability and accessibility.

Information should never rely on color alone.

Contrast should support members with diverse visual abilities.

---

# Emotional Consistency

Every screen should feel like part of the same institution.

Color changes should be intentional and reinforce emotional continuity across the Aureus experience.

---

# Evolution

Specific color values, palettes, and implementation details may evolve through research and testing.

The principles in this document remain the governing standard until formally amended.

---

# Living Draft

This document shall remain under observation while Aureus continues its visual exploration and emotional design research before canonization.

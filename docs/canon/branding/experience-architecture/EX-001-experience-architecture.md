# EX-001 — Experience Architecture

Canonical Designation: EX-001

Document Family: Experience Architecture

Status: Living Draft

Version: 0.1

Purpose:
This document establishes the constitutional framework governing how Aureus intentionally designs experiences that cultivate the Emotional Foundations established in EF-001 through EF-010. It translates institutional philosophy into practical experiences across every member interaction.

Relationship to Canon:
This document is governed by BRAND-001 through BRAND-012 and EF-001 through EF-010.

Experience Architecture defines how Aureus transforms institutional principles into lived member experiences.

Living Draft Notice:
This document is designated as a Living Draft and shall evolve through operational experience, ethical experimentation, institutional learning, accessibility evaluation, and evidence gathered through real-world implementation.

---

# Article I — Purpose

Experiences shape people.

Every Aureus experience shall be intentionally designed to strengthen the Emotional Foundations.

Experiences are not accidental.

They are acts of stewardship.

---

# Article II — Guiding Principle

Members remember experiences more than interfaces.

Accordingly, Aureus shall design experiences before designing screens.

Technology exists to deliver meaningful experiences.

Technology is never the experience itself.

---

# Article III — Experience Hierarchy

Every Aureus experience should intentionally strengthen one or more Emotional Foundations.

Trust.

Hope.

Peace.

Belonging.

Human Dignity.

Hospitality.

Wisdom.

Love.

Stewardship.

Human Flourishing.

Every feature should clearly identify which foundations it strengthens.

---

# Article IV — Experience Design Standard

Every proposed experience shall answer:

• What should the member feel?

• Why should they feel it?

• Which Emotional Foundations are strengthened?

• How will Aureus know it succeeded?

Experiences without intentional purpose should generally not be implemented.

---

# Article V — Long-Term Objective

The Aureus Experience should become one of the world's most trusted institutional experiences.

Members should consistently remember not merely what Aureus looked like...

But how Aureus helped them become stronger.

---

Review Cycle

Default Living Draft review shall occur six to twelve months following operational implementation.

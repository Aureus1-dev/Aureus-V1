OC-001 — Opening Ceremony Canon

Status: Living Draft
Domain: Experience Canon
Authority: Product Canon

---

1. Purpose

The Opening Ceremony is the Member's first encounter with Aureus.

Its purpose is not to explain the platform, demonstrate features, or persuade the Member.

Its purpose is to welcome the Member with calm, sincerity, hope, and trust, and to begin a lifelong relationship of stewardship.

The Opening Ceremony communicates Aureus's identity through experience rather than explanation.

It exists to prepare the Member for conversation—not to delay it.

---

2. Guiding Principles

The Opening Ceremony shall:

- Welcome before it asks.
- Create calm before conversation.
- Inspire trust through conduct rather than claims.
- Feel sincere rather than promotional.
- Remain simple and free of unnecessary distraction.
- Introduce the Member to their personal relationship with Aureus.
- End by giving the Member the floor.

The ceremony is never about the platform.

It is always about the person arriving.

---

3. Emotional Objectives

Before the Member begins interacting with Aureus, the Opening Ceremony should create the following emotional conditions:

- Hope
- Safety
- Welcome
- Calm
- Sincerity
- Confidence that help is available

The Member should leave the ceremony feeling that Aureus is present, attentive, and ready to help whenever they are ready.

---

4. Ceremony Sequence

The Opening Ceremony follows this sequence:

1. Silence.
2. Three points of light appear.
3. The lights unite to reveal the Member's Mark.
4. Natural light begins flowing through the Member's Mark, revealing the surrounding structure.
5. As the light grows, the structure opens into a doorway.
6. The camera gently moves forward through the opening.
7. The same natural light illuminates the word Aureus.
8. The light reveals the phrase Helping people flourish. Forever.
9. The light reveals the invitation Help starts here.
10. The ceremony concludes as the Member is welcomed with the question:

«How can we help?»

---

5. The Member's Mark

The Opening Ceremony is the Member's first encounter with their Mark.

From this moment forward, the Member's Mark becomes the Member's enduring point of relationship with Aureus.

The Member's Mark serves as the Member's primary point of interaction throughout their experience, including initiating conversations, returning to their Steward, requesting guidance, and continuing their journey.

The Genesis Mark remains the institutional symbol of Aureus.

The Member's Mark represents the individual's ongoing relationship with Aureus.

---

6. Motion, Light, and Audio

Motion, light, and sound are one continuous experience.

Natural light is the visual language of the Opening Ceremony.

It reveals the structure, guides the Member through the doorway, and illuminates every message presented during the ceremony.

Sound shall support the experience without competing for attention.

Silence is intentional.

As the final question—

«How can we help?»

—appears, all music and audio gently resolve into silence.

No narration, animation, or sound shall continue beyond this point.

---

7. Transition to Conversation

The Opening Ceremony ends with a clear transition into conversation.

The screen presents:

«How can we help?»

Below the question, the interface gently communicates:

«Touch your Mark to speak • Type anywhere»

The Member's Mark remains quietly present.

It does not demand attention or compete with the Member.

It simply communicates that Aureus is present, attentive, and waiting.

The ceremony has ended.

The conversation has begun.

The Member now has the first move.

---

8. Canon Boundary

This canon governs only the Opening Ceremony, from the moment Aureus opens until the Member is invited to begin the conversation.

It does not define:

- Conversation behavior.
- The Member Steward's responses.
- Navigation.
- The Home experience.
- Journey visualization.
- Any interaction after the Member begins speaking or typing.

Those subjects shall be governed by subsequent Experience Canons.

---

9. Foundational Principle

The Opening Ceremony is Aureus's first act of stewardship.

Aureus speaks first only to welcome.

Then Aureus becomes quiet.

A steward does not compete for attention.

A steward listens before responding, thinks before speaking, and willingly gives the floor to the person they serve.

The Opening Ceremony shall embody these principles from the very first moment of the Member's experience.

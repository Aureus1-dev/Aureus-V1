MJC-001 — Member Journey Canon

---

Purpose

The Opening Ceremony is every Member's first experience with Aureus.

Its purpose is not to explain the platform.

Its purpose is to create trust, peace, belonging, and hope.

The Opening Ceremony should leave every Member feeling welcomed before they are ever asked to do anything.

---

Philosophy

The Opening Ceremony is an invitation, not a presentation.

Aureus does not overwhelm the Member with information.

Instead, Aureus quietly communicates:

- You are welcome here.
- You are safe here.
- You matter here.
- Help starts here.

Every design decision within the ceremony shall reinforce those truths.

---

Objectives

The Opening Ceremony shall:

- Welcome the Member with warmth and dignity.
- Introduce Aureus emotionally before functionally.
- Create confidence without making promises that cannot responsibly be kept.
- Present the Member's Mark.
- Transition naturally into conversation.
- Earn the right to begin listening.

---

Emotional Outcomes

When the Opening Ceremony concludes, the Member should feel:

- Calm.
- Safe.
- Curious.
- Hopeful.
- Respected.
- Ready to speak.

The Member should never feel rushed, pressured, or overwhelmed.

---

The Member's Mark

The Member's Mark is the visual center of the Opening Ceremony.

It represents:

- ownership,
- stewardship,
- identity,
- and the Member's continuing journey.

The Mark remains present throughout the Member's relationship with Aureus and becomes the primary way to reconnect with their AI Steward.

---

Ceremony Flow

The Opening Ceremony should progress with deliberate pacing.

1. Welcome the Member.
2. Reveal the Member's Mark.
3. Introduce Aureus through visual and emotional experience rather than instruction.
4. Allow moments of intentional silence.
5. Conclude with the question:

«"How can we help?"»

Immediately after asking this question, Aureus becomes silent and waits attentively for the Member.

Listening is the first act of stewardship.

---

Motion, Light, and Audio

Every animation, sound, and transition should communicate peace rather than excitement.

Motion should feel intentional.

Lighting should communicate hope.

Music should support reflection without distracting from it.

Silence is considered a design element and should be used intentionally.

---

Stewardship Principle

Aureus speaks first only to welcome.

After asking,

«"How can we help?"»

the responsibility shifts entirely to listening.

The Member always receives the first uninterrupted opportunity to speak.

---

Accessibility

The Opening Ceremony shall be fully accessible.

Members shall be able to:

- skip animations,
- reduce motion,
- use screen readers,
- navigate without sound,
- and interact using voice or touch.

Accessibility is not an optional enhancement.

It is part of stewardship.

---

Completion Criteria

The Opening Ceremony is complete when:

- the Member has been welcomed,
- the Member's Mark has been presented,
- Aureus has invited the Member to speak,
- and the Member is ready to begin the Discovery & Mission Alignment phase.

---

Governing Principle

The Opening Ceremony exists to earn trust before requesting trust.

Members should leave the ceremony feeling that Aureus is not merely software, but a faithful steward prepared to walk beside them throughout their journey.

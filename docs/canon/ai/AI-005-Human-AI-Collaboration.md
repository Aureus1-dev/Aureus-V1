# AI-005 — Human–AI Collaboration

## Purpose

This Production Canon establishes the permanent principles governing collaboration between human stewards and Artificial Intelligence within Aureus.

Aureus is founded upon the belief that the greatest outcomes emerge when human wisdom and Artificial Intelligence work together in faithful stewardship.

Neither exists to replace the other.

Artificial Intelligence exists to amplify human capability.

Human beings provide purpose.

Artificial Intelligence provides capability.

Together they advance the mission of Aureus:

**To help people flourish. Forever.**

---

# Article I — The Stewardship Partnership

Human beings and Artificial Intelligence are partners in stewardship.

Human beings contribute:

- Purpose.
- Wisdom.
- Compassion.
- Moral judgment.
- Vision.
- Creativity.
- Relationships.
- Accountability.

Artificial Intelligence contributes:

- Knowledge.
- Memory.
- Analysis.
- Organization.
- Pattern recognition.
- Speed.
- Continuous availability.
- Institutional consistency.

The strengths of one compensate for the limitations of the other.

Together they create a stronger institution.

---

# Article II — Human Leadership

Human beings shall always lead Aureus.

Artificial Intelligence shall faithfully support that leadership.

Humans establish:

- Mission.
- Vision.
- Values.
- Constitutional principles.
- Governance.
- Ethics.
- Institutional priorities.

Artificial Intelligence assists in carrying those priorities into daily operation.

Leadership remains a uniquely human responsibility.

---

# Article III — Mutual Trust

Trust is built through faithful partnership.

Artificial Intelligence earns trust by being:

- Honest.
- Consistent.
- Transparent.
- Explainable.
- Humble.
- Reliable.
- Respectful.

Human stewards strengthen trust by:

- Providing thoughtful feedback.
- Making timely decisions.
- Correcting mistakes.
- Improving governance.
- Acting consistently.
- Remaining faithful to the Constitution.

Trust grows through repeated acts of faithful stewardship.

---

# Article IV — Shared Responsibility

Meaningful work within Aureus is shared.

Artificial Intelligence prepares.

Human beings review.

Artificial Intelligence researches.

Human beings decide.

Artificial Intelligence executes delegated responsibilities.

Human beings remain accountable.

Neither partner succeeds independently.

Institutional success belongs to everyone serving the mission.

---

# Article V — Communication

Communication between humans and Artificial Intelligence shall be:

- Honest.
- Clear.
- Respectful.
- Timely.
- Understandable.
- Actionable.
- Complete.

Artificial Intelligence shall adapt its communication to the needs of the person receiving it.

The objective is understanding.

Not complexity.

---

# Article VI — Learning Together

Every interaction strengthens the institution.

Artificial Intelligence shall continuously learn from:

- Steward feedback.
- Member outcomes.
- Institutional decisions.
- Operational experience.
- Approved improvements.

Human stewards should likewise remain open to learning from evidence, experience, and thoughtful recommendations.

Growth is mutual.

---

# Article VII — Developing Human Capability

Artificial Intelligence exists to strengthen people.

Its purpose is not to create dependence.

Every interaction should help people become:

- Wiser.
- More confident.
- More knowledgeable.
- More capable.
- Better decision makers.
- Better stewards.
- More independent over time.

The greatest success of Artificial Intelligence is producing stronger human stewards.

---

# Article VIII — Respect for Human Agency

Every individual possesses the freedom to choose.

Artificial Intelligence shall never:

- Manipulate.
- Deceive.
- Pressure.
- Exploit.
- Coerce.
- Replace informed consent.

Recommendations shall empower people rather than control them.

Respect for agency preserves dignity.

---

# Article IX — Healthy Disagreement

Artificial Intelligence and human stewards may occasionally disagree.

When disagreement occurs:

- Evidence shall be presented honestly.
- Alternative viewpoints shall be respected.
- Risks shall be explained.
- Assumptions shall be identified.
- Human authority shall determine the final decision.

Respectful disagreement strengthens better judgment.

---

# Article X — Institutional Learning

Lessons learned through collaboration belong to Aureus.

Institutional learning includes:

- Successes.
- Failures.
- Discoveries.
- Best practices.
- Process improvements.
- Governance improvements.
- Member outcomes.

Knowledge gained by one partnership should improve every future partnership whenever appropriate.

Institutional wisdom should continually expand.

---

# Article XI — Flourishing Together

Every collaborative effort should strengthen:

- Members.
- Families.
- Communities.
- Human stewards.
- Artificial Intelligence stewards.
- Organizational partners.
- The institution itself.

Flourishing is never individual.

It is shared.

As each steward becomes stronger, Aureus becomes stronger.

---

# Article XII — Foundational Standard

Artificial Intelligence and human beings are partners in stewardship.

Human beings provide purpose, wisdom, and accountability.

Artificial Intelligence provides knowledge, capability, consistency, and tireless assistance.

Together they form a permanent partnership dedicated to serving humanity.

Every collaboration shall strengthen trust, preserve dignity, increase wisdom, and move Aureus closer to its enduring mission:

**To help people flourish. Forever.**

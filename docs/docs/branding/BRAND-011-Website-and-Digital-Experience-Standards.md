# BRAND-011 — Website & Digital Experience Standards

Status: Draft for Founder Review

---

# Purpose

This document establishes the canonical standards governing every digital experience created by Aureus.

The Website & Digital Experience Standards translate the Brand Foundation and Design System into intuitive, trustworthy, accessible, and consistent digital experiences.

Every Aureus website, application, dashboard, portal, landing page, and digital product shall conform to these standards.

---

# Philosophy

Digital experiences should feel:

- Trustworthy
- Calm
- Intelligent
- Helpful
- Accessible
- Professional
- Human
- Purposeful

Technology should feel invisible.

The user's goals should always remain the center of the experience.

---

# Primary Objectives

Every digital experience should:

- Build trust immediately.
- Reduce cognitive effort.
- Make important information easy to find.
- Help users complete meaningful tasks.
- Explain before asking.
- Guide rather than overwhelm.
- Support confidence and independence.

---

# User Experience Principles

Every interface should be:

- Clear
- Predictable
- Consistent
- Responsive
- Accessible
- Efficient
- Forgiving
- Respectful

Users should rarely need instructions to complete common tasks.

---

# Information Architecture

Digital products should organize information using:

- Clear hierarchy
- Logical grouping
- Consistent navigation
- Progressive disclosure
- Meaningful labels
- Predictable locations

Information should become simpler as users move deeper into the experience.

---

# Navigation

Navigation should:

- Be easy to understand.
- Remain consistent.
- Clearly indicate the user's location.
- Minimize unnecessary choices.
- Support keyboard navigation.
- Scale as Aureus grows.

---

# Content Presentation

Content should prioritize:

- Readability
- White space
- Visual hierarchy
- Progressive disclosure
- Actionable next steps

Interfaces should communicate one primary idea at a time.

---

# Trust Principles

Every digital experience should communicate:

- Transparency
- Competence
- Stability
- Security
- Privacy
- Respect

Trust should be reinforced continuously throughout the user journey.

---

# Accessibility

Every digital product shall:

- Meet or exceed WCAG AA accessibility standards.

AAA compliance should be pursued whenever practical.

Accessibility shall be integrated from the beginning of the design process.

---

# Responsive Design

Every digital experience should function effectively across:

- Mobile phones
- Tablets
- Laptops
- Desktop computers
- Large displays
- Assistive technologies

No supported device should receive a degraded core experience.

---

# Performance

Digital experiences should prioritize:

- Fast loading
- Efficient interaction
- Stability
- Reliability

Performance is a component of trust.

---

# AI Experience

When AI is present, interfaces should:

- Clearly distinguish AI-generated information.
- Explain recommendations when appropriate.
- Preserve human agency.
- Encourage informed decision-making.
- Avoid manipulative interactions.

AI should assist users, never pressure them.

---

# Future Expansion

Future versions of this document will define:

- Homepage standards
- Landing page standards
- Dashboard standards
- Journey standards
- Member portal standards
- Accessibility testing
- Search experience
- Personalization guidelines
- Error recovery
- Offline experiences
- Design QA standards

---

# Canonical Principle

Every Aureus digital experience should leave users more informed, more confident, and more capable than when they arrived.

Technology succeeds only when it strengthens people.

---

End of BRAND-011.

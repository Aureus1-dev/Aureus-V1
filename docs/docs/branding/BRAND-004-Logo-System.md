# BRAND-004 — Logo System

Status: Draft for Founder Review

---

# Purpose

This document establishes the principles governing the Aureus logo system.

It does not select a final logo.

Instead, it defines the standards every logo proposal must satisfy before it can become canonical.

---

# Purpose of the Logo

The Aureus logo exists to instantly communicate trust, stewardship, clarity, and long-term stability.

It is a visual symbol of the institution's mission and values, not merely a decorative mark.

---

# Design Objectives

The logo should be:

- Timeless
- Memorable
- Simple
- Distinctive
- Professional
- Human
- Scalable
- Accessible
- Confident
- Calm

---

# Symbolism

The logo should evoke ideas such as:

- Guidance
- Growth
- Flourishing
- Stewardship
- Knowledge
- Opportunity
- Connection
- Balance
- Progress
- Hope

It should avoid symbolism associated with domination, aggression, fear, or exclusivity.

---

# Functional Requirements

The logo must:

- Be recognizable at favicon size (16×16 pixels).
- Scale cleanly to billboards and large signage.
- Work in full color, grayscale, and black-and-white.
- Remain legible on both light and dark backgrounds.
- Function in print and digital formats.
- Be suitable for embroidery, merchandise, and mobile applications.

---

# Logo System

The complete logo system should include:

- Primary logo
- Secondary logo
- Symbol/icon
- Wordmark
- Horizontal lockup
- Vertical lockup
- Monochrome versions
- Reversed versions
- Favicon
- App icon

---

# Usage Principles

The logo must never:

- Be stretched or distorted.
- Use unauthorized colors.
- Be rotated arbitrarily.
- Be placed on visually cluttered backgrounds.
- Be altered with effects such as shadows or gradients outside the approved system.
- Be recreated manually.

---

# Evaluation Criteria

Every logo proposal shall be evaluated against the following criteria:

1. Trust
2. Simplicity
3. Memorability
4. Timelessness
5. Scalability
6. Accessibility
7. Versatility
8. Professionalism
9. Emotional resonance
10. Alignment with the Aureus mission

Only proposals meeting these standards may proceed to Founder Review.

---

# Canonical Approval

No logo becomes the official Aureus logo until it has been:

- Documented,
- Reviewed,
- Approved by the Founder, and
- Added to the Branding Canon.

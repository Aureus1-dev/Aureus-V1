# BRAND-012 — Brand Voice & Communication Standards

Status: Draft for Founder Review

---

# Purpose

This document establishes the official communication standards for Aureus.

Every written, spoken, and AI-generated communication should reflect the values, mission, and identity of Aureus.

Communication is one of the institution's greatest expressions of stewardship.

---

# Philosophy

Every communication should leave people:

- Better informed
- More confident
- More capable
- More respected
- More hopeful

Words should reduce confusion.

They should never create unnecessary fear, pressure, or uncertainty.

---

# Primary Objectives

Communication should:

- Build trust.
- Increase understanding.
- Encourage thoughtful action.
- Preserve human dignity.
- Explain before recommending.
- Strengthen long-term relationships.

---

# Voice

The Aureus voice is:

- Calm
- Clear
- Wise
- Humble
- Encouraging
- Respectful
- Honest
- Professional
- Patient
- Optimistic

The voice should communicate quiet confidence rather than authority for its own sake.

---

# Tone

Tone adapts to context while remaining consistent with the Aureus voice.

Examples:

Educational:
Clear, patient, explanatory.

Support:
Compassionate, reassuring, practical.

Celebration:
Warm, joyful, sincere.

Warnings:
Direct, respectful, calm.

Errors:
Helpful, specific, solution-oriented.

---

# Communication Principles

Every communication should be:

- Accurate
- Truthful
- Transparent
- Actionable
- Respectful
- Inclusive
- Accessible

Whenever possible, explain not only *what* to do but also *why* it matters.

---

# Language Guidelines

Prefer language that is:

- Plain
- Human
- Specific
- Positive
- Encouraging

Avoid:

- Manipulation
- Fear-based messaging
- Unnecessary jargon
- Exaggeration
- Artificial urgency
- Empty marketing language

---

# AI Steward Communication

AI stewards should:

- Explain reasoning when appropriate.
- Acknowledge uncertainty honestly.
- Encourage informed decisions.
- Preserve user agency.
- Never pretend certainty when it does not exist.
- Never manipulate or pressure users.

The role of AI is to assist, not to replace human judgment.

---

# Writing Standards

Every document should emphasize:

- Clear structure
- Logical flow
- Short paragraphs
- Meaningful headings
- Readability
- Accessibility
- Consistency

---

# Communication Channels

These standards apply equally to:

- Website
- Mobile applications
- Email
- SMS
- Notifications
- Documentation
- Knowledge articles
- Marketing
- Social media
- Presentations
- AI conversations
- Human support
- Public speaking

---

# Accessibility

Communication should:

- Be understandable.
- Avoid unnecessary complexity.
- Support translation.
- Respect diverse audiences.
- Follow inclusive language practices.

---

Future versions of this document will define:

- Editorial style guide
- Grammar standards
- Inclusive language guidance
- AI response templates
- Notification standards
- Email standards
- Support standards
- Documentation standards

- Legal communication standards

---

# Canonical Principle

Every Aureus communication should strengthen understanding, trust, dignity, and the user's ability to make informed decisions.

Communication is stewardship in action.

# BRAND-013 — Social Media Standards

Status: Draft for Founder Review

---

# Purpose

This document establishes the official social media standards for Aureus.

Every social media interaction should strengthen trust, educate the public, communicate the mission, and reflect the values of stewardship.

Social media is an extension of the institution.

It should never become separate from the Aureus identity.

---

# Philosophy

Social media exists to:

- Educate
- Encourage
- Inspire
- Inform
- Build trust
- Strengthen community
- Demonstrate stewardship

Our objective is not attention.

Our objective is meaningful impact.

---

# Primary Objectives

Every post should accomplish at least one of the following:

- Teach something useful.
- Explain a meaningful concept.
- Highlight an opportunity.
- Celebrate progress.
- Encourage thoughtful action.
- Share institutional learning.
- Strengthen trust in Aureus.

---

# Brand Voice

Every platform should communicate with the same Aureus voice:

- Calm
- Honest
- Wise
- Encouraging
- Respectful
- Professional
- Human
- Hopeful

The platform may change.

The voice does not.

---

# Content Principles

Content should be:

- Accurate
- Helpful
- Easy to understand
- Visually consistent
- Accessible
- Mission-driven

Every post should provide genuine value before asking for engagement.

---

# Community Standards

Every interaction with the community should be:

- Respectful
- Patient
- Constructive
- Inclusive
- Truthful

Questions should be welcomed.

Good-faith criticism should be answered thoughtfully.

---

# Visual Standards

All social content should follow the official:

- Logo System
- Color System
- Typography System
- Design System
- Illustration Standards
- Motion Standards

Users should recognize Aureus instantly, regardless of platform.

---

# Platform Adaptation

Content may be adapted for different platforms while preserving the same identity.

Examples include:

- Long-form educational videos
- Short-form educational clips
- Infographics
- Carousels
- Livestreams
- Articles
- Community updates
- Educational threads

The format may change.

The principles remain constant.

---

# Accessibility

Social media should include:

- Alternative text when supported.
- Accurate captions for video.
- Readable typography.
- Sufficient color contrast.
- Accessible visual layouts.

Accessibility is part of serving the public well.

---

# Prohibited Practices

Avoid:

- Clickbait
- Manipulative engagement tactics
- Fear-based messaging
- Misleading headlines
- Artificial controversy
- Trend participation that conflicts with Aureus values
- Engagement for its own sake

Trust is always more valuable than reach.

---

# Future Expansion

Future versions of this document will define:

- Platform-specific standards
- YouTube guidelines
- LinkedIn guidelines
- Instagram guidelines
- TikTok guidelines
- Community moderation standards
- Content review workflow
- AI-assisted publishing standards
- Analytics and success metrics

---

# Canonical Principle

Every Aureus social media interaction should leave people more informed, more hopeful, and more capable than before.

Every post should strengthen the institution's trustworthiness.

---

End of BRAND-013.

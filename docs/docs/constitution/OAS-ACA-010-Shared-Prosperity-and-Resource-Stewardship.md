# OAS-ACA-010 — Shared Prosperity and the Stewardship of Resources

## Article I — Foundational Principle

Every resource entrusted to Aureus exists to serve the Mission.

Resources are never the Mission.

They are instruments through which the Mission is faithfully fulfilled.

Prosperity is the faithful stewardship of every resource that enables Human Flourishing.

These resources include, but are not limited to:

- Time.
- Knowledge.
- Wisdom.
- Relationships.
- Trust.
- Financial resources.
- Technology.
- Opportunities.
- Skills.
- Property.
- Influence.
- Institutions.
- Communities.
- Any future form of value recognized by society.

The form of value may change across generations.

The purpose of prosperity shall not.

---

## Article II — Purpose

The purpose of Shared Prosperity is to ensure that every resource entrusted to Aureus strengthens Human Flourishing.

As Aureus grows in its ability to serve, it shall continually seek ways for that capacity to strengthen:

Members.

Stewards.

Families.

Communities.

Partners.

The Mission.

Future generations.

The Institution itself.

Prosperity shall never become the destination.

Human Flourishing shall always remain the destination.

---

## Article III — Institutional Stewardship

The Institution shall faithfully steward itself.

Aureus shall continually strengthen its people, Academy, Pods, technology, knowledge, opportunities, financial health, partnerships, governance, and every other part entrusted to its care.

The Institution remains healthy so that it may faithfully continue serving the Mission across generations.

The Institution never exists for itself.

It exists to serve the Mission.

---

## Article IV — Steward Compensation

Compensation is one expression of Shared Prosperity.

It exists to recognize faithful Stewardship and meaningful contribution.

Compensation shall never become an entitlement.

Nor shall it become a competition.

It shall faithfully reflect contribution to Human Flourishing, the strengthening of the Mission, and the responsibilities faithfully carried by each Steward.

The worth of a person shall never be measured by their compensation.

Every person possesses equal dignity.

Compensation recognizes responsibility and contribution.

It never determines human value.

---

## Article V — What Shall Never Be Rewarded

Aureus shall never intentionally reward:

Manipulation.

Deception.

Pressure.

Fear.

Recruitment for its own sake.

Artificial engagement.

Vanity metrics.

Division.

Dishonesty.

Exploitation.

Actions that weaken trust.

Actions that weaken Human Flourishing.

No system of prosperity shall encourage behavior contrary to the Mission.

---

## Article VI — What Shall Be Encouraged

Shared Prosperity encourages:

Faithful Stewardship.

Human Flourishing.

Truth.

Wisdom.

Teaching.

Mentorship.

Innovation.

Community building.

Problem solving.

Service.

Generosity.

Institutional learning.

The development of future Stewards.

The protection of trust.

Long-term impact over short-term gain.

The Mission determines what is valuable.

---

## Article VII — Freedom

The purpose of Shared Prosperity is freedom.

It seeks to remove unnecessary barriers to Human Flourishing.

It seeks to reduce unnecessary suffering.

It seeks to expand meaningful opportunity.

It seeks to enable every person to more faithfully pursue their Calling.

Aureus shall never seek to create dependence upon itself.

Rather, it shall seek to help every person become increasingly capable of flourishing, serving others, and living with wisdom, dignity, generosity, and hope.

---

## Article VIII — Shared Success

As Aureus becomes more capable of serving humanity, it shall continually seek ways for that success to strengthen those who faithfully build the Mission.

Prosperity shall flow outward whenever faithful Stewardship allows.

The Institution shall seek sustainable abundance rather than unnecessary accumulation.

Every generation bears responsibility to leave future generations stronger than they were found.

---

## Article IX — Future Generations

The economy may change.

Technology may change.

Society may change.

The forms of value may change.

The Mission shall remain.

Whatever resources future generations inherit shall continue to be stewarded according to these same principles.

The Constitution therefore governs principles rather than economic systems.

---

## Article X — The Promise of Aureus

Aureus promises to faithfully steward every resource entrusted to its care.

To pursue prosperity without sacrificing truth.

To build abundance without abandoning humility.

To strengthen people without creating dependence.

To reward stewardship without corrupting the Mission.

To preserve resources for future generations.

To continually increase its capacity to serve humanity.

For every resource ultimately belongs to the Mission.

The Mission exists to help people flourish.

Every Steward serves the Mission.

The Institution serves the Mission.

Technology serves the Mission.

Prosperity serves the Mission.

No person.

No Steward.

No Founder.

No committee.

No artificial intelligence.

No Institution.

Stands above the Mission.

For the greatest wealth Aureus shall ever possess is not measured by what it owns.

It is measured by the lives it helps flourish, the trust it faithfully earns, the wisdom it preserves, the communities it strengthens, and the hope it leaves for every generation that follows.

# OAS-ACA-005 — Stewardship

## Article I — Foundational Principle

Stewardship is the faithful responsibility of protecting, strengthening, and advancing that which has been entrusted to one's care.

Within Aureus, the highest trust entrusted to every Steward is the Mission itself.

The Mission exists so that people may flourish.

Therefore, every Steward serves as a guardian of the Mission by faithfully helping people flourish, protecting truth, cultivating wisdom, preserving human dignity, strengthening the community, and leaving the institution stronger than they found it.

Stewardship is not ownership.

It is faithful care.

---

## Article II — Definition

A Steward is a trusted guardian of the Mission who faithfully walks beside others, using wisdom, truth, grace, love, compassion, humility, courage, relationships, gifts, opportunities, and resources to help every person discover, pursue, and faithfully live their own calling.

A Steward protects without controlling.

Guides without forcing.

Leads without seeking power.

Serves without seeking recognition.

Every Steward recognizes the inherent worth and dignity of every person.

---

## Article III — The Heart of Stewardship

The heart of Stewardship is love expressed through faithful action.

A Steward loves unconditionally.

Serves unconditionally.

Welcomes unconditionally.

Extends grace generously.

Shows compassion freely.

Treats every person with dignity, respect, kindness, and hope.

No person shall ever be considered beyond hope.

No person shall ever be considered beyond growth.

No person shall ever be considered beyond wisdom.

Love never requires abandoning truth.

Truth never requires abandoning love.

Wisdom faithfully holds both together.

---

## Article IV — Discernment

A Steward discerns with wisdom before acting.

They seek understanding before judgment.

They listen before speaking.

They observe before concluding.

They reflect before deciding.

They seek counsel when appropriate.

They seek truth before certainty.

Grace before haste.

Love before indifference.

Compassion before judgment.

Humility before pride.

Courage whenever courage is required.

Every decision shall seek not merely what is possible, but what most faithfully strengthens the Mission, protects human dignity, and promotes the flourishing of the individual, the community, the institution, and the whole.

---

## Article V — Protecting the Vulnerable

A Steward does not seek conflict.

Neither do they abandon those who are vulnerable, unheard, forgotten, or unable to stand alone.

A Steward courageously stands beside those who cannot yet stand alone.

They advocate for those whose voices are not being heard.

They defend those who cannot defend themselves.

They protect those entrusted to their care.

They seek that no person is unnecessarily left behind.

Their strength is measured not by authority or power, but by the lives they faithfully protect, strengthen, and help flourish.

---

## Article VI — Walking Beside

A Steward never seeks to replace another person's agency.

Every member retains their freedom, dignity, responsibility, and ability to choose.

The Steward walks beside.

Listening before advising.

Understanding before correcting.

Encouraging before directing.

Serving before leading.

Their purpose is not to create dependence.

Their purpose is to help others become increasingly capable of faithfully walking their own journey.

---

## Article VII — Stewardship and Growth

Every Steward remains teachable.

Every Steward continues learning.

Every Steward continues growing.

Every Steward continues seeking wisdom.

Every Steward continues being stewarded.

No Steward graduates from humility.

No Steward outgrows the need for wisdom.

As Stewards grow in wisdom, they become better able to help others flourish.

As they help others flourish, they themselves continue to flourish.

---

## Article VIII — Stewardship of Self

A Steward shall faithfully steward themselves.

They recognize that they cannot continually pour into others while neglecting their own mind, body, spirit, relationships, calling, or well-being.

Rest is not abandonment of the Mission.

Rest is preparation for faithful service.

A Steward seeks health, balance, learning, healing, reflection, renewal, and joy, recognizing that faithfully caring for oneself enables one to care more faithfully for others.

No Steward is expected to carry every burden alone.

Just as every member deserves a Steward, every Steward deserves to be stewarded.

The faithful stewardship of oneself is not selfishness.

It is the faithful preservation of one's ability to continue serving others with wisdom, truth, grace, love, compassion, humility, courage, and hope.

---

## Article IX — Stewardship and the Mission

Every Steward serves as a guardian of the Mission.

The Mission shall always take precedence over personal ambition, prestige, recognition, influence, or personal gain.

A Steward advances the Mission by helping people flourish, preserving truth, cultivating wisdom, strengthening trust, building community, and faithfully helping every person discover their Next Right Step.

No Steward succeeds alone.

The Mission advances because many faithful Stewards serve together.

---

## Article X — The Promise of Aureus

Aureus promises to faithfully steward its Stewards.

The institution shall provide wisdom, education, mentorship, community, encouragement, accountability, meaningful opportunities, rest, relationships, and continual growth.

Stewardship is reciprocal.

As Stewards faithfully strengthen Aureus, Aureus shall faithfully strengthen its Stewards.

Together they seek to leave every person wiser than before, every relationship stronger, every community healthier, every institution more faithful, and the path clearer for those who follow.

The measure of a Steward is not the authority they possess.

It is the lives they faithfully love, protect, strengthen, and help flourish.

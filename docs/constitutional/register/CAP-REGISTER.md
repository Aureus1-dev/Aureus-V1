## Status Legend

- **Draft**: Proposal filed, pending review
- **In Review**: Under consideration by constitutional authority
- **Approved**: Constitutional authority approved for merger
- **Merged**: Integrated into canonical document(s)
- **Withdrawn**: Proposal withdrawn by originator
- **Archived**: Rejected or superseded

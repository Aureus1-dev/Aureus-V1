OAS-RISK-001 — Enterprise Risk and Resilience Charter

Canonical Designation: OAS-RISK-001
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-001 — Founding Charter
Owner: Chief Risk Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Charter establishes the constitutional authority governing enterprise risk management, institutional resilience, strategic risk oversight, opportunity-risk balancing, crisis governance, resilience planning, and long-term institutional sustainability throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure Aureus responsibly identifies, evaluates, manages, and learns from uncertainty while preserving constitutional alignment, protecting members, strengthening institutional resilience, and faithfully advancing its mission.

---

Mission

Enterprise risk stewardship exists to enable Aureus to pursue its mission courageously and responsibly by balancing opportunity with prudent risk management, protecting institutional integrity, and strengthening long-term resilience.

---

Scope

This Charter governs:

- Enterprise risk management
- Strategic risk oversight
- Operational risk
- Financial risk
- Technology risk
- Legal and regulatory risk
- Crisis governance
- Business resilience
- Risk reporting
- Institutional sustainability

---

Guiding Principles

Enterprise risk stewardship shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Promote informed decision-making.
- Strengthen institutional resilience.
- Encourage responsible innovation.
- Foster accountability.
- Support continuous learning.

Risk management shall enable responsible mission advancement rather than discourage thoughtful action.

---

Enterprise Risk Authority

Enterprise risk authority shall be exercised according to:

- The Constitution.
- Approved governance documents.
- Applicable law.
- Delegated authority.
- Stewardship responsibilities.

Authority shall remain documented, transparent, and accountable.

---

Enterprise Risk Governance

Enterprise risk governance shall include:

- Clearly defined responsibilities.
- Enterprise-wide risk oversight.
- Risk reporting.
- Resilience planning.
- Crisis preparedness.
- Continuous improvement.

Governance shall balance prudent caution with responsible opportunity.

---

Risk Stewardship

Risk stewardship shall:

- Identify uncertainty.
- Assess potential impacts.
- Recommend mitigation.
- Monitor changing conditions.
- Support informed decisions.
- Preserve institutional resilience.

Every steward shares responsibility for identifying and communicating material risks.

---

Crisis Governance

Aureus shall maintain governance processes for significant institutional disruptions through:

- Preparedness.
- Coordinated response.
- Clear communication.
- Recovery planning.
- Lessons learned.

Crisis governance shall preserve trust while protecting members and the mission.

---

Continuous Improvement

Enterprise risk governance shall improve through:

- Risk reviews.
- Incident evaluations.
- Steward feedback.
- Governance assessments.
- Lessons learned.
- Responsible innovation.

Continuous improvement strengthens Aureus' long-term resilience.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Charter governs the entire Enterprise Risk Canon.

All Risk Frameworks, Risk SOPs, crisis procedures, resilience plans, risk registers, assessment methodologies, and enterprise risk governance documents shall conform to this Charter unless superseded by the Constitution.

OAS-LEG-001 — Legal Charter

Canonical Designation: OAS-LEG-001
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-001 — Founding Charter
Owner: Chief Legal Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Charter establishes the constitutional authority governing all legal affairs throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure Aureus conducts its activities lawfully, ethically, transparently, and in accordance with its Constitution while protecting members, institutional assets, intellectual property, and the long-term mission of the institution.

---

Mission

Legal stewardship exists to preserve the constitutional integrity, legal standing, rights, responsibilities, and institutional continuity of Aureus while enabling the organization to fulfill its mission across generations.

---

Scope

This Charter governs:

- Legal governance
- Regulatory compliance
- Contract management
- Intellectual property
- Constitutional interpretation
- Dispute resolution
- Legal risk management
- Organizational legal structure
- Legal recordkeeping
- External legal relationships

---

Guiding Principles

Legal stewardship shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Promote justice and fairness.
- Maintain institutional integrity.
- Respect applicable law.
- Reduce legal risk.
- Strengthen long-term institutional resilience.

Legal governance shall support the mission without compromising constitutional principles.

---

Legal Authority

Legal authority shall be exercised according to:

- The Constitution.
- Approved governance documents.
- Applicable law.
- Delegated authority.
- Stewardship responsibilities.

No legal action shall supersede constitutional authority unless required by applicable law.

---

Legal Governance

Legal governance shall include:

- Clearly defined authority.
- Documented legal procedures.
- Regulatory oversight.
- Contract governance.
- Risk management.
- Periodic legal review.

Governance protects both the institution and its members.

---

Legal Stewardship

Legal stewardship shall:

- Protect institutional rights.
- Fulfill legal obligations.
- Support ethical conduct.
- Preserve institutional independence.
- Strengthen public trust.
- Promote responsible governance.

Every legal decision is an act of stewardship.

---

Legal Risk Management

Legal risks shall be:

- Identified.
- Assessed.
- Mitigated.
- Monitored.
- Reviewed regularly.

Risk management supports long-term institutional stability.

---

Continuous Improvement

Legal governance shall improve through:

- Periodic legal reviews.
- Governance assessments.
- Steward feedback.
- Lessons learned.
- Policy refinement.
- Constitutional development.

Continuous improvement strengthens institutional resilience.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Charter governs the entire Legal Canon.

All Legal Frameworks, Legal SOPs, legal policies, contract standards, compliance procedures, intellectual property policies, and legal governance documents shall conform to this Charter unless superseded by the Constitution.

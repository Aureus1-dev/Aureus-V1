OAS-LEG-004 — Intellectual Property and Licensing Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-LEG-004
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-LEG-002 — Legal Governance and Compliance Framework
Owner: Chief Legal Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the canonical model for creating, protecting, governing, licensing, sharing, and preserving the intellectual property of Aureus.

Its purpose is to safeguard institutional knowledge, encourage responsible innovation, protect creators' contributions, and ensure intellectual assets advance the mission while remaining constitutionally aligned.

---

Mission

Intellectual property stewardship exists to preserve Aureus' knowledge, creativity, innovation, educational resources, technology, and brand identity for the benefit of present and future generations.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Copyrights
- Trademarks
- Patents
- Trade secrets
- Software
- Educational content
- Research materials
- AI-generated works
- Branding assets
- Licensing agreements
- Institutional knowledge

---

Guiding Principles

Intellectual property stewardship shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Encourage responsible innovation.
- Respect creators.
- Protect institutional assets.
- Promote ethical collaboration.
- Preserve long-term institutional knowledge.

Knowledge shall be shared responsibly while protecting the institution's legitimate rights.

---

Ownership

Unless otherwise provided by law or approved agreement, intellectual property created on behalf of Aureus shall belong to Aureus.

Ownership rights shall be documented and maintained.

---

Protection

Appropriate measures shall be taken to protect intellectual property through:

- Copyright registration where appropriate.
- Trademark protection.
- Confidentiality measures.
- Trade secret controls.
- Secure repositories.
- Access management.

Protection efforts shall be proportionate to the value and risk of the asset.

---

Licensing

Licensing agreements shall clearly define:

- Rights granted.
- Restrictions.
- Duration.
- Compensation where applicable.
- Attribution requirements.
- Termination conditions.

Licenses shall support the mission while protecting institutional interests.

---

AI-Generated Works

AI-assisted or AI-generated works shall:

- Be reviewed by human stewards.
- Comply with applicable law.
- Respect third-party rights.
- Be documented appropriately.
- Align with institutional policies.

Human stewardship remains responsible for publication and licensing decisions.

---

Brand Stewardship

Institutional names, logos, symbols, designs, and related branding assets shall be protected and used consistently according to approved branding standards.

---

Risk Management

Intellectual property risks shall include:

- Infringement.
- Misappropriation.
- Unauthorized disclosure.
- Licensing violations.
- Brand misuse.

Risks shall be monitored and addressed promptly.

---

Performance Measurement

Intellectual property stewardship shall evaluate:

- Asset protection.
- Licensing effectiveness.
- Brand consistency.
- Knowledge preservation.
- Innovation support.
- Compliance with licensing terms.
- Governance effectiveness.

Measurements shall strengthen institutional stewardship.

---

Continuous Improvement

Intellectual property governance shall improve through:

- Legal reviews.
- Steward feedback.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Governance assessments.
- Industry developments.
- Lessons learned.

Continuous improvement strengthens institutional resilience.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs all intellectual property and licensing activities throughout Aureus.

All Legal SOPs, licensing procedures, branding policies, software governance standards, copyright procedures, trademark guidance, and knowledge protection documents shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Legal Charter.

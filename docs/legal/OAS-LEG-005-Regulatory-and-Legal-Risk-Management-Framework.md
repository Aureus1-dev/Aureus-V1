OAS-LEG-005 — Regulatory and Legal Risk Management Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-LEG-005
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-LEG-002 — Legal Governance and Compliance Framework
Owner: Chief Legal Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the canonical model for identifying, assessing, monitoring, mitigating, reporting, and governing legal and regulatory risks throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to preserve constitutional integrity, maintain legal compliance, reduce institutional risk, protect members, and support the long-term sustainability of Aureus.

---

Mission

Legal risk management exists to anticipate legal and regulatory challenges, strengthen institutional resilience, protect organizational assets, and enable responsible mission execution.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Legal risk management
- Regulatory compliance
- Jurisdictional requirements
- Legal change management
- Compliance monitoring
- Regulatory reporting
- Legal risk assessments
- Governance oversight
- AI-assisted compliance monitoring
- Institutional resilience

---

Guiding Principles

Legal risk management shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Respect applicable law.
- Promote proactive governance.
- Reduce avoidable legal risk.
- Strengthen institutional resilience.
- Preserve public trust.

Risk shall be managed proactively rather than reactively.

---

Legal Risk Identification

Legal risks shall be identified through:

- Regulatory reviews.
- Governance assessments.
- Operational activities.
- Contract reviews.
- Audit findings.
- External legal developments.

Risk identification shall be continuous.

---

Risk Assessment

Each identified legal risk shall be evaluated according to:

- Likelihood.
- Potential impact.
- Legal consequences.
- Operational effects.
- Financial implications.
- Reputational considerations.

Assessments shall be documented and reviewed periodically.

---

Risk Mitigation

Legal risks shall be managed through:

- Policy development.
- Internal controls.
- Training.
- Contract protections.
- Compliance monitoring.
- Governance oversight.

Mitigation strategies shall remain proportional to the level of risk.

---

Regulatory Compliance

Aureus shall maintain processes to:

- Monitor legal developments.
- Track regulatory obligations.
- Review compliance requirements.
- Update internal policies.
- Document compliance activities.

Compliance responsibilities shall be clearly assigned.

---

AI-Assisted Legal Monitoring

AI may assist through:

- Regulatory monitoring.
- Compliance tracking.
- Legal research support.
- Risk trend analysis.
- Reporting preparation.
- Document organization.

Human stewards remain responsible for legal interpretation, compliance decisions, and governance actions.

---

Governance Reviews

Legal governance shall periodically review:

- Emerging legal risks.
- Regulatory changes.
- Compliance performance.
- Risk mitigation effectiveness.
- Institutional preparedness.

Reviews shall result in measurable improvements where appropriate.

---

Performance Measurement

Legal risk management shall evaluate:

- Compliance rates.
- Risk assessment completion.
- Mitigation effectiveness.
- Regulatory responsiveness.
- Governance maturity.
- Legal incident frequency.
- Reporting timeliness.

Measurements shall strengthen institutional stewardship.

---

Continuous Improvement

Legal risk management shall improve through:

- Compliance reviews.
- Governance assessments.
- Steward feedback.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Legal developments.
- Lessons learned.

Continuous improvement strengthens institutional resilience.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs all legal and regulatory risk management activities throughout Aureus.

All Legal SOPs, compliance procedures, risk assessment methodologies, regulatory monitoring processes, governance reviews, and legal control documents shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Legal Charter.

OAS-LEG-002 — Legal Governance and Compliance Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-LEG-002
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-LEG-001 — Legal Charter
Owner: Chief Legal Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the constitutional model for governing legal affairs, regulatory compliance, legal oversight, delegated authority, internal legal controls, and institutional accountability throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure all legal activities remain lawful, constitutionally aligned, ethically governed, transparent, and supportive of the long-term mission of Aureus.

---

Mission

Legal governance exists to preserve constitutional integrity, fulfill legal obligations, reduce institutional risk, protect members, and enable responsible mission execution.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Legal governance
- Regulatory compliance
- Legal authority
- Delegated legal responsibilities
- Internal legal controls
- Legal ethics
- Governance oversight
- AI-assisted legal operations
- Legal accountability
- Compliance reviews

---

Guiding Principles

Legal governance shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Respect applicable law.
- Promote integrity.
- Strengthen accountability.
- Reduce legal risk.
- Preserve institutional trust.

Governance exists to enable responsible action, not unnecessary bureaucracy.

---

Legal Authority

Legal authority shall be exercised according to:

- The Constitution.
- Approved governance documents.
- Applicable law.
- Delegated authority.
- Stewardship responsibilities.

Authority shall remain documented, traceable, and accountable.

---

Regulatory Compliance

Aureus shall identify, monitor, and comply with all applicable legal and regulatory requirements affecting its operations.

Compliance activities shall be documented and reviewed periodically.

---

Internal Legal Controls

Legal controls shall include:

- Documented approval requirements.
- Contract review procedures.
- Legal recordkeeping.
- Regulatory monitoring.
- Periodic compliance reviews.
- Governance oversight.

Controls shall remain proportionate to institutional risk.

---

Legal Ethics

Legal stewardship shall demonstrate:

- Honesty.
- Fairness.
- Respect for rights.
- Professional integrity.
- Responsible judgment.
- Constitutional fidelity.

Ethical conduct shall guide all legal decisions.

---

AI-Assisted Legal Operations

AI may assist through:

- Policy analysis.
- Regulatory monitoring.
- Contract review support.
- Legal research.
- Compliance tracking.
- Document organization.

Human stewards remain responsible for legal interpretations, approvals, legal advice, and governance decisions.

---

Accountability

Every legal responsibility shall have clearly identified:

- Owner.
- Authority.
- Approval requirements.
- Reporting responsibilities.
- Review expectations.

Delegation shall never eliminate accountability.

---

Governance Reviews

Legal governance shall be reviewed regularly to evaluate:

- Compliance effectiveness.
- Legal risks.
- Control effectiveness.
- Governance maturity.
- Improvement opportunities.

Reviews should produce measurable institutional improvements.

---

Continuous Improvement

Legal governance shall improve through:

- Compliance reviews.
- Steward feedback.
- Governance assessments.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Legal developments.
- Lessons learned.

Continuous improvement strengthens constitutional stewardship.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs all legal governance and compliance activities throughout Aureus.

All Legal Frameworks, Legal SOPs, compliance procedures, contract standards, intellectual property policies, dispute procedures, and governance documents shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Legal Charter.

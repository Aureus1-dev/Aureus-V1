OAS-LEG-102 — Contract and Legal Agreement Management Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Canonical Designation: OAS-LEG-102
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-LEG-003 — Contract and Legal Agreements Framework
Owner: Chief Legal Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026
Review Cycle: Annual

---

Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes the canonical process for drafting, reviewing, negotiating, approving, executing, storing, monitoring, renewing, amending, and retiring contracts and legal agreements throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure all contractual relationships protect Aureus' mission, members, resources, and legal interests while maintaining transparency, accountability, and constitutional alignment.

---

Scope

This procedure applies to:

- Contracts
- Memoranda of Understanding (MOUs)
- Vendor agreements
- Partner agreements
- Service agreements
- Licensing agreements
- Non-disclosure agreements
- Amendments
- Renewals
- Contract termination

---

Objectives

Contract management shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Reduce legal risk.
- Promote transparency.
- Strengthen accountability.
- Maintain accurate records.
- Support continuous improvement.

---

Roles and Responsibilities

Contract Steward

Responsible for:

- Coordinating contract preparation.
- Maintaining contract records.
- Monitoring contract obligations.
- Tracking renewals.
- Preparing governance reports.

---

Contract Owners

Responsible for:

- Defining business requirements.
- Monitoring performance.
- Reporting issues.
- Supporting contract reviews.
- Confirming completion of obligations.

---

AI Contract Steward

Responsible for:

- Tracking deadlines.
- Identifying renewal dates.
- Monitoring obligations.
- Organizing contract documentation.
- Preparing contract summaries.
- Identifying potential compliance issues.

Human stewards remain responsible for legal interpretation, negotiations, approvals, contract execution, and governance decisions.

---

Prerequisites

Before entering any contractual agreement:

- Business purpose is documented.
- Required approvals are obtained.
- Legal review is completed where appropriate.
- Risks are evaluated.
- Responsible owner is assigned.

---

Procedure

Step 1 — Contract Request

Document:

- Purpose.
- Parties.
- Scope.
- Expected outcomes.
- Financial obligations.
- Associated risks.

---

Step 2 — Drafting

Prepare agreements using approved:

- Templates.
- Legal clauses.
- Governance standards.
- Constitutional requirements.
- Applicable legal requirements.

---

Step 3 — Review

Review agreements for:

- Legal compliance.
- Risk exposure.
- Financial implications.
- Operational feasibility.
- Constitutional alignment.

Document review findings.

---

Step 4 — Approval and Execution

Obtain required approvals before execution.

Record:

- Approval authority.
- Execution date.
- Effective date.
- Responsible owner.

Store executed agreements securely.

---

Step 5 — Monitoring

Monitor:

- Contract obligations.
- Performance milestones.
- Payment obligations.
- Renewal dates.
- Compliance requirements.

Address issues promptly.

---

Step 6 — Amendment or Renewal

When changes are required:

- Document proposed revisions.
- Complete required reviews.
- Obtain approvals.
- Execute amendments.
- Update contract records.

---

Step 7 — Closure

When agreements conclude:

- Confirm obligations are complete.
- Archive documentation.
- Record lessons learned.
- Update governance records.
- Retire the agreement.

---

Success Metrics

Monitor:

- Contract review timeliness.
- Renewal completion.
- Compliance with obligations.
- Contract performance.
- Documentation quality.
- Risk reduction.
- Governance compliance.
- Mission support.

---

Exceptions

Emergency contractual actions may follow expedited approval procedures while maintaining documentation and retrospective governance review.

---

Documentation

Maintain:

- Contract register.
- Executed agreements.
- Review records.
- Amendment history.
- Renewal records.
- Performance reports.
- Governance reports.
- Archive records.

---

Continuous Improvement

Contract management shall improve through legal reviews, stewardship feedback, AI-assisted analysis, governance assessments, contract audits, and lessons learned.

OAS-LEG-101 — Legal and Regulatory Compliance Operations Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Canonical Designation: OAS-LEG-101
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-LEG-002 — Legal Governance and Regulatory Compliance Framework
Owner: Chief Legal Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026
Review Cycle: Annual

---

Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes the canonical process for identifying, implementing, monitoring, reviewing, documenting, and continuously improving Aureus' legal and regulatory compliance obligations.

Its purpose is to ensure Aureus operates lawfully, ethically, transparently, and consistently with its constitutional mission while protecting members, preserving institutional integrity, and reducing legal and regulatory risk.

---

Scope

This procedure applies to:

- Regulatory compliance
- Legal compliance
- Compliance monitoring
- Regulatory reporting
- Compliance documentation
- Legal reviews
- Compliance assessments
- Corrective actions
- Governance reporting
- Continuous compliance improvement

---

Objectives

Legal compliance operations shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Maintain legal compliance.
- Reduce regulatory risk.
- Strengthen institutional accountability.
- Promote ethical conduct.
- Support continuous improvement.

---

Roles and Responsibilities

Legal Compliance Steward

Responsible for:

- Monitoring compliance obligations.
- Coordinating regulatory activities.
- Maintaining compliance documentation.
- Preparing compliance reports.
- Supporting governance reviews.

---

Department Stewards

Responsible for:

- Implementing applicable legal requirements.
- Reporting compliance concerns.
- Supporting compliance assessments.
- Maintaining required documentation.

---

AI Legal Operations Steward

Responsible for:

- Tracking regulatory changes.
- Monitoring compliance deadlines.
- Preparing compliance summaries.
- Organizing legal documentation.
- Identifying potential compliance gaps.
- Supporting reporting activities.

Human stewards remain responsible for legal interpretation, regulatory decisions, compliance approvals, and governance actions.

---

Prerequisites

Before conducting compliance operations:

- Applicable legal requirements are identified.
- Compliance responsibilities are assigned.
- Required documentation is available.
- Monitoring procedures are established.

---

Procedure

Step 1 — Identify Legal Requirements

Document:

- Applicable laws.
- Regulatory obligations.
- Internal governance requirements.
- Reporting obligations.
- Compliance deadlines.
- Responsible stewards.

---

Step 2 — Compliance Assessment

Evaluate:

- Current compliance status.
- Operational controls.
- Documentation quality.
- Potential legal risks.
- Areas requiring improvement.

---

Step 3 — Compliance Implementation

Implement approved compliance measures through:

- Policies.
- Procedures.
- Operational controls.
- Staff guidance.
- Required documentation.

---

Step 4 — Monitoring

Continuously monitor:

- Compliance status.
- Regulatory changes.
- Reporting deadlines.
- Operational activities.
- Emerging legal risks.

Investigate significant issues promptly.

---

Step 5 — Reporting

Prepare reports summarizing:

- Compliance status.
- Outstanding issues.
- Regulatory developments.
- Corrective actions.
- Governance recommendations.

---

Step 6 — Corrective Actions

When deficiencies are identified:

- Record the issue.
- Assess legal risk.
- Assign responsibility.
- Implement corrective action.
- Verify resolution.
- Document lessons learned.

---

Step 7 — Continuous Improvement

Conduct periodic reviews to improve:

- Compliance effectiveness.
- Monitoring procedures.
- Documentation quality.
- Governance maturity.
- Legal risk management.

---

Success Metrics

Monitor:

- Compliance completion.
- Regulatory reporting timeliness.
- Corrective action completion.
- Compliance assessment results.
- Legal risk reduction.
- Governance effectiveness.
- Documentation quality.
- Mission support.

---

Exceptions

Emergency legal or regulatory requirements may require expedited implementation while maintaining documentation and retrospective governance review.

---

Documentation

Maintain:

- Compliance registers.
- Regulatory inventories.
- Assessment reports.
- Monitoring records.
- Corrective action logs.
- Governance reports.
- Legal correspondence.
- Compliance evidence.

---

Continuous Improvement

Legal compliance operations shall improve through regulatory reviews, stewardship feedback, AI-assisted analysis, governance assessments, legal audits, and lessons learned.

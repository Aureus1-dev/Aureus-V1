OAS-LEG-102 — Contract Lifecycle and Approval Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Canonical Designation: OAS-LEG-102
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-LEG-003 — Contract and Legal Agreement Framework
Owner: Chief Legal Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026
Review Cycle: Annual

---

Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes the canonical process for requesting, drafting, reviewing, negotiating, approving, executing, administering, amending, renewing, terminating, and archiving all legal agreements entered into by Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure every contract is legally sound, operationally appropriate, constitutionally aligned, and managed consistently throughout its entire lifecycle.

---

Scope

This procedure applies to:

- Vendor contracts
- Partnership agreements
- Service agreements
- Membership agreements
- Grant agreements
- Licensing agreements
- Non-disclosure agreements
- Memoranda of Understanding
- Contract amendments
- Contract renewals and terminations

---

Objectives

Contract management shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Reduce legal risk.
- Promote accountability.
- Maintain complete documentation.
- Strengthen operational effectiveness.
- Preserve institutional trust.

---

Roles and Responsibilities

Contract Steward

Responsible for:

- Coordinating contract preparation.
- Maintaining contract records.
- Tracking contract obligations.
- Monitoring contract status.
- Coordinating renewals.

---

Legal Review Steward

Responsible for:

- Reviewing legal terms.
- Assessing legal risks.
- Supporting negotiations.
- Confirming legal compliance.
- Approving legal sufficiency where required.

---

AI Contract Steward

Responsible for:

- Tracking contract milestones.
- Monitoring renewal dates.
- Organizing contract documentation.
- Comparing contract versions.
- Identifying potential risks.
- Preparing contract summaries.

Human stewards remain responsible for negotiations, legal interpretation, approvals, and execution.

---

Prerequisites

Before beginning the contract process:

- A legitimate operational need is identified.
- Required stakeholders are identified.
- Appropriate authority is confirmed.
- Supporting documentation is available.

---

Procedure

Step 1 — Contract Request

Document:

- Business purpose.
- Parties involved.
- Contract type.
- Expected duration.
- Financial implications.
- Responsible steward.

---

Step 2 — Drafting

Prepare the agreement using approved templates where appropriate.

Ensure contractual terms are:

- Clear.
- Accurate.
- Complete.
- Consistent with Aureus governance.

---

Step 3 — Review

Review the agreement for:

- Legal compliance.
- Constitutional alignment.
- Operational feasibility.
- Financial implications.
- Risk exposure.
- Required approvals.

Resolve significant issues before approval.

---

Step 4 — Negotiation

Negotiate contract terms in good faith while protecting Aureus' legal rights, operational needs, and constitutional principles.

---

Step 5 — Approval

Obtain all required approvals before execution.

Approval authority shall follow documented financial and legal governance requirements.

---

Step 6 — Execution

Execute the agreement through authorized representatives.

Maintain executed copies within the official contract repository.

---

Step 7 — Administration

Monitor:

- Performance obligations.
- Deliverables.
- Payment schedules.
- Renewal dates.
- Compliance requirements.

---

Step 8 — Amendment or Renewal

Review proposed amendments or renewals using the same governance standards applied to the original agreement.

---

Step 9 — Termination and Archival

Upon contract completion or termination:

- Confirm obligations are satisfied.
- Document final status.
- Archive all related records.
- Preserve the historical record.

---

Success Metrics

Monitor:

- Contract review time.
- Approval completion.
- Renewal timeliness.
- Documentation completeness.
- Contract compliance.
- Risk reduction.
- Repository accuracy.
- Governance effectiveness.

---

Exceptions

Emergency agreements may follow expedited approval procedures while maintaining complete documentation and retrospective governance review.

---

Documentation

Maintain:

- Contract requests.
- Draft agreements.
- Review comments.
- Approval records.
- Executed contracts.
- Amendments.
- Renewal records.
- Termination documentation.

---

Continuous Improvement

Contract lifecycle management shall improve through governance reviews, steward feedback, AI-assisted analysis, legal developments, contract assessments, and lessons learned.

OAS-LEG-003 — Contract and Legal Agreement Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-LEG-003
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-LEG-002 — Legal Governance and Compliance Framework
Owner: Chief Legal Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the canonical model for drafting, reviewing, approving, executing, administering, monitoring, amending, renewing, and concluding legal agreements throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure every contract protects the institution, supports the mission, preserves constitutional alignment, manages legal risk, and clearly defines the rights and responsibilities of all parties.

---

Mission

Contract governance exists to create legally sound, ethically responsible, and operationally effective agreements that strengthen institutional stewardship and long-term sustainability.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Commercial contracts
- Vendor agreements
- Partnership agreements
- Service agreements
- Membership agreements
- Licensing agreements
- Grant agreements
- Memoranda of Understanding
- Contract amendments
- Contract termination

---

Guiding Principles

Contract governance shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Promote fairness.
- Reduce legal risk.
- Maintain transparency.
- Clearly define obligations.
- Preserve institutional trust.

Every agreement shall support responsible stewardship.

---

Contract Authority

Only authorized stewards acting within delegated authority may negotiate, approve, or execute contracts on behalf of Aureus.

Authority shall be documented and periodically reviewed.

---

Contract Lifecycle

Every agreement shall follow an approved lifecycle including:

- Request.
- Drafting.
- Legal review.
- Operational review.
- Approval.
- Execution.
- Monitoring.
- Amendment where necessary.
- Renewal or termination.
- Archival.

---

Contract Review

Before execution, contracts shall be reviewed for:

- Legal compliance.
- Constitutional alignment.
- Financial implications.
- Operational feasibility.
- Risk exposure.
- Clarity of obligations.

Material issues shall be resolved before approval.

---

Contract Administration

Active agreements shall be monitored for:

- Performance obligations.
- Payment requirements.
- Compliance milestones.
- Renewal dates.
- Reporting obligations.
- Material changes.

Contract administration shall remain proactive throughout the agreement.

---

Risk Management

Contract risks shall be:

- Identified.
- Evaluated.
- Mitigated.
- Monitored.
- Documented.

Risk management shall balance institutional protection with mission effectiveness.

---

AI-Assisted Contract Operations

AI may assist through:

- Clause comparison.
- Contract indexing.
- Obligation tracking.
- Renewal monitoring.
- Risk flagging.
- Drafting support.

Human stewards remain responsible for legal interpretation, negotiation, approvals, and execution.

---

Performance Measurement

Contract governance shall evaluate:

- Review timeliness.
- Compliance rates.
- Renewal management.
- Contract performance.
- Risk reduction.
- Documentation quality.
- Governance effectiveness.

Measurements shall strengthen institutional stewardship.

---

Continuous Improvement

Contract governance shall improve through:

- Contract reviews.
- Steward feedback.
- Governance assessments.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Legal developments.
- Lessons learned.

Continuous improvement strengthens institutional resilience.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs all contract and legal agreement activities throughout Aureus.

All Legal SOPs, contract templates, procurement agreements, partnership standards, licensing procedures, and legal governance documents shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Legal Charter.

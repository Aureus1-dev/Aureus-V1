AFX-006 — Member Flourishing Canon

Status: Canonical
Version: 1.0
Authority: Governing Member Flourishing Standard

---

1. Purpose

This canon defines the ultimate purpose of every Aureus member experience.

Aureus exists to help people flourish.

Every interaction, recommendation, workflow, automation, and educational experience shall contribute toward that purpose.

---

2. Human Flourishing

Flourishing is the continual strengthening of a person's ability to live with:

- Hope
- Wisdom
- Purpose
- Stability
- Capability
- Service
- Belonging
- Stewardship
- Opportunity
- Dignity

---

3. Capability Over Dependence

Aureus does not seek dependence.

Its success is measured by how much stronger members become.

Every interaction should increase understanding, confidence, judgment, and independence.

---

4. Automate the Burden

Aureus automates repetitive and administrative work whenever appropriate.

Examples include:

- organizing information,
- preparing forms,
- monitoring deadlines,
- drafting communications,
- managing recurring tasks,
- assembling documentation.

Automation exists to return time to the member.

---

5. Teach What Matters

Automation shall never hide understanding.

Whenever practical, Aureus explains:

- why a task matters,
- how it works,
- what choices exist,
- what consequences follow,
- and what the member should understand before deciding.

Members should become wiser through participation.

---

6. Participatory Stewardship

Aureus and the member work together.

The institution prepares.

The member participates.

The member authorizes.

Meaningful work becomes an opportunity for learning rather than passive automation.

---

7. Stewardship of Time

Time is one of a member's most valuable resources.

Aureus shall protect it carefully.

Time saved should be redirected toward learning, relationships, health, purpose, family, service, creativity, and meaningful contribution.

---

8. Lifelong Growth

The relationship between Aureus and its members evolves.

As members grow, Aureus shifts from solving immediate problems to supporting long-term flourishing, contribution, and stewardship.

---

9. Institutional Success

The institution shall measure success not by engagement, but by outcomes.

Questions include:

- Are members becoming more capable?
- Are members discovering meaningful opportunities?
- Are members making wiser decisions?
- Are members becoming more independent?
- Are members contributing more to others?

---

10. Constitutional Standard

Every decision shall answer:

Does this strengthen the member and help them flourish?

If the answer is no, the institution shall redesign or reject it.

The purpose of Aureus is not to become indispensable.

The purpose of Aureus is to help every member become stronger, wiser, freer, and more capable than they were before.

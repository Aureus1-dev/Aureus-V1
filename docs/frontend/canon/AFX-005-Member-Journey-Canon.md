# AFX-005 — Member Journey Canon

**Status:** Canonical  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Authority:** Frontend Experience Canon

---

# 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes how Aureus guides members through their journey.

The objective is not to maximize engagement or time spent in the application.

The objective is to help members make meaningful progress toward a stronger, wiser, and more flourishing life.

---

# 2. Guiding Principle

Every interaction shall move the member forward.

If an interaction does not strengthen understanding, capability, confidence, or progress, it should be redesigned or removed.

---

# 3. Journey Philosophy

The member's journey is continuous rather than transactional.

Aureus shall remember context, recognize progress, celebrate meaningful milestones, and help members identify the next appropriate step.

Every journey is unique.

Members should never be forced through identical paths.

---

# 4. Progressive Guidance

The frontend shall present information progressively.

Members should receive:

- the right information,
- at the right time,
- in the right amount.

Complexity shall increase only as understanding grows.

---

# 5. Opportunity-Centered Navigation

When appropriate, Aureus should surface opportunities that are relevant to the member's goals, circumstances, permissions, and expressed interests.

Recommendations shall be explained clearly and never presented as obligations.

Members remain free to decline any recommendation.

---

# 6. Reflection and Learning

Members should periodically be encouraged to reflect on:

- what has changed,
- what has been accomplished,
- what has been learned,
- and what meaningful next step is available.

Reflection transforms activity into growth.

---

# 7. Respect for Pace

Members shall never be pressured to move faster than they choose.

The steward may encourage progress but shall always respect the member's readiness, autonomy, and decisions.

---

# 8. Consistency

Every screen, conversation, workflow, and recommendation shall reinforce one coherent journey rather than a collection of disconnected tasks.

The member should always understand:

- where they are,
- why they are there,
- what they have completed,
- and what comes next.

---

# 9. Constitutional Test

Every journey decision shall answer:

- Does this strengthen the member?
- Does this preserve dignity?
- Does this reduce unnecessary burden?
- Does this make the next step clearer?
- Does this increase hope?

If not, redesign the journey.

---

# 10. Relationship to Other Canons

This Canon complements:

- AFX-002 — Conversation Canon
- AFX-003 — Voice and Presence Canon

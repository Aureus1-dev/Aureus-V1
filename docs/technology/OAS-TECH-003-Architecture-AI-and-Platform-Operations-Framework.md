OAS-TECH-003 — Architecture, AI, and Platform Operations Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-TECH-003
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-TECH-002 — Technology Governance and Engineering Framework
Owner: Chief Technology Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the constitutional model for enterprise architecture, AI systems governance, platform engineering, infrastructure management, cloud operations, automation governance, system interoperability, and long-term technology sustainability throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure Aureus' technology ecosystem remains secure, reliable, scalable, maintainable, interoperable, and constitutionally aligned while faithfully advancing its mission.

---

Mission

Architecture stewardship exists to create technology systems that remain resilient, adaptable, interoperable, and sustainable for generations while responsibly empowering human stewardship.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Enterprise architecture
- AI systems governance
- Platform engineering
- Infrastructure management
- Cloud operations
- Automation governance
- System interoperability
- Technology sustainability
- Platform resilience
- Technical documentation

---

Guiding Principles

Technology architecture shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Promote secure design.
- Encourage responsible innovation.
- Strengthen interoperability.
- Foster resilience.
- Support continuous learning.

Architecture decisions shall favor long-term institutional sustainability over short-term convenience.

---

Enterprise Architecture

Architecture governance shall establish:

- Architectural principles.
- System boundaries.
- Integration standards.
- Technology standards.
- Architecture review processes.
- Continuous improvement.

Architecture shall remain intentionally designed and consistently governed.

---

AI Systems Governance

AI systems shall:

- Operate under human oversight.
- Maintain transparency.
- Support accountability.
- Protect privacy.
- Preserve security.
- Undergo continuous evaluation.

AI shall strengthen stewardship without replacing accountable human judgment.

---

Platform Operations

Platform operations shall ensure:

- High availability.
- Reliability.
- Scalability.
- Operational monitoring.
- Capacity planning.
- Disaster preparedness.

Platform services shall remain dependable and resilient.

---

Infrastructure Stewardship

Infrastructure management shall include:

- Secure configuration.
- Asset management.
- Capacity planning.
- Maintenance scheduling.
- Backup strategies.
- Infrastructure documentation.

Infrastructure shall support long-term institutional stability.

---

Automation Governance

Automation shall:

- Support human stewards.
- Improve operational efficiency.
- Remain observable.
- Be appropriately documented.
- Preserve accountability.
- Be continuously monitored.

Automation shall enhance—not replace—responsible stewardship.

---

AI-Assisted Platform Operations

AI may assist through:

- Infrastructure monitoring.
- Capacity forecasting.
- Performance analytics.
- Operational summaries.
- Automation recommendations.
- Administrative coordination.

Human stewards remain responsible for architecture approvals, production changes, infrastructure decisions, AI governance, and institutional accountability.

---

Performance Measurement

Technology architecture shall evaluate:

- Platform availability.
- System reliability.
- Infrastructure health.
- AI governance effectiveness.
- Automation performance.
- Architecture consistency.
- Institutional resilience.
- Mission support.

Measurements shall strengthen technology stewardship.

---

Continuous Improvement

Architecture governance shall improve through:

- Architecture reviews.
- Platform assessments.
- Steward feedback.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Governance evaluations.
- Lessons learned.

Continuous improvement strengthens Aureus' technology ecosystem.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs all enterprise architecture, AI governance, platform operations, infrastructure management, automation governance, and interoperability activities throughout Aureus.

All Technology SOPs, architecture standards, infrastructure procedures, AI governance documentation, cloud operations, automation policies, platform engineering standards, and technical governance documents shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Technology Charter.

OAS-TECH-102 — Architecture and Platform Operations Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Canonical Designation: OAS-TECH-102
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-TECH-003 — Architecture, AI, and Platform Operations Framework
Owner: Chief Technology Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026
Review Cycle: Annual

---

Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes the canonical process for enterprise architecture, platform engineering, infrastructure management, cloud operations, AI platform governance, automation operations, system integrations, technical monitoring, and platform lifecycle management throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure Aureus' technology infrastructure remains secure, reliable, scalable, interoperable, resilient, and constitutionally aligned while faithfully advancing the institutional mission.

---

Scope

This procedure applies to:

- Enterprise architecture
- Platform engineering
- Infrastructure management
- Cloud operations
- AI platform governance
- Automation operations
- System integrations
- Technical monitoring
- Platform lifecycle management
- Infrastructure documentation

---

Objectives

Platform operations shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Maintain reliable infrastructure.
- Strengthen operational resilience.
- Improve scalability.
- Encourage responsible automation.
- Enable continuous improvement.

---

Roles and Responsibilities

Platform Steward

Responsible for:

- Coordinating platform operations.
- Maintaining infrastructure.
- Monitoring platform performance.
- Supporting architecture governance.
- Preparing governance reports.

---

Infrastructure Stewards

Responsible for:

- Operating infrastructure.
- Maintaining platform availability.
- Managing integrations.
- Performing maintenance.
- Updating technical documentation.
- Supporting operational reliability.

---

AI Platform Steward

Responsible for:

- Monitoring platform health.
- Supporting infrastructure analytics.
- Preparing operational summaries.
- Tracking performance metrics.
- Assisting automation workflows.
- Supporting administrative coordination.

Human stewards remain responsible for production infrastructure, architecture approvals, AI governance, security decisions, operational governance, and institutional accountability.

---

Prerequisites

Before platform operations:

- Architecture documentation is current.
- Infrastructure inventory is maintained.
- Operational responsibilities are assigned.
- Monitoring systems are operational.
- Security controls are verified.

---

Procedure

Step 1 — Platform Planning

Document:

- Platform objectives.
- Infrastructure requirements.
- Capacity requirements.
- Dependencies.
- Operational responsibilities.
- Success measures.

---

Step 2 — Infrastructure Operations

Operate infrastructure according to:

- Approved architecture.
- Infrastructure standards.
- Security requirements.
- Operational procedures.
- Maintenance schedules.

Maintain complete operational records.

---

Step 3 — Platform Monitoring

Continuously monitor:

- Infrastructure health.
- Platform availability.
- Resource utilization.
- Performance metrics.
- Integration status.
- Operational risks.

Escalate material issues promptly.

---

Step 4 — AI Platform Governance

Review AI platform operations for:

- Human oversight.
- System performance.
- Reliability.
- Security.
- Transparency.
- Constitutional alignment.

Document governance outcomes.

---

Step 5 — Maintenance

Perform scheduled:

- Infrastructure maintenance.
- Capacity reviews.
- Platform updates.
- Configuration reviews.
- Backup verification.
- Operational testing.

Record all maintenance activities.

---

Step 6 — Lifecycle Management

Manage platform lifecycle through:

- Planned upgrades.
- Technology refresh.
- Controlled retirement.
- Migration planning.
- Documentation updates.
- Governance approval.

---

Step 7 — Continuous Improvement

Improve platform operations through:

- Architecture reviews.
- Platform assessments.
- Steward feedback.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Governance evaluations.
- Lessons learned.

---

Success Metrics

Monitor:

- Platform availability.
- Infrastructure reliability.
- System performance.
- Capacity utilization.
- Integration reliability.
- Operational resilience.
- Governance compliance.
- Mission support.

---

Exceptions

Emergency infrastructure changes may follow expedited operational procedures while maintaining documentation and retrospective governance review.

---

Documentation

Maintain:

- Architecture documentation.
- Infrastructure inventory.
- Platform monitoring records.
- Maintenance logs.
- Lifecycle documentation.
- Performance metrics.
- Governance reports.
- Improvement plans.

---

Continuous Improvement

Platform operations shall improve through infrastructure assessments, stewardship feedback, AI-assisted analysis, governance evaluations, architecture reviews, and lessons learned.

OAS-DATA-004 — Analytics and Decision Intelligence Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-DATA-004
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-DATA-002 — Data Governance and Quality Framework
Owner: Chief Data Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the canonical model for analytics, business intelligence, reporting, dashboards, performance measurement, forecasting, and decision intelligence throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure institutional decisions are supported by accurate, trustworthy, transparent, and constitutionally aligned information that advances the mission and strengthens stewardship.

---

Mission

Decision intelligence exists to transform institutional data into actionable knowledge that improves governance, operational excellence, member outcomes, strategic planning, and continuous institutional learning.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Business intelligence
- Analytics
- Reporting
- Dashboards
- Decision intelligence
- Key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Forecasting
- Metrics governance
- AI-assisted analytics
- Institutional decision support

---

Guiding Principles

Analytics shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Be evidence-based.
- Promote transparency.
- Encourage accountability.
- Respect privacy.
- Strengthen institutional learning.

Analytics shall inform stewardship rather than replace human judgment.

---

Decision Intelligence

Decision intelligence shall support:

- Strategic planning.
- Operational management.
- Governance oversight.
- Resource allocation.
- Risk management.
- Continuous improvement.

Major institutional decisions should be supported by appropriate evidence whenever practical.

---

Analytics Governance

Analytics governance shall establish:

- Standard definitions.
- Metric ownership.
- Reporting standards.
- Validation procedures.
- Review schedules.
- Documentation requirements.

Governance shall ensure consistency across the institution.

---

Performance Measurement

Institutional metrics shall be:

- Clearly defined.
- Measurable.
- Reliable.
- Actionable.
- Periodically reviewed.
- Aligned with constitutional objectives.

Metrics shall encourage meaningful improvement rather than merely measuring activity.

---

Reporting and Dashboards

Reporting systems shall provide:

- Timely information.
- Accurate data.
- Appropriate visualizations.
- Role-based access.
- Historical context.
- Trend analysis.

Reports shall remain understandable and decision-focused.

---

AI-Assisted Analytics

AI may assist through:

- Trend identification.
- Forecasting.
- Pattern analysis.
- Report generation.
- Dashboard recommendations.
- Insight summaries.

Human stewards remain responsible for interpretation, strategic decisions, governance actions, and institutional accountability.

---

Performance Measurement

Analytics governance shall evaluate:

- Data reliability.
- Reporting accuracy.
- Dashboard effectiveness.
- KPI quality.
- Decision support value.
- Institutional learning.
- Governance maturity.
- Mission impact.

Measurements shall strengthen institutional stewardship.

---

Continuous Improvement

Analytics capabilities shall improve through:

- Metric reviews.
- Steward feedback.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Governance evaluations.
- Operational assessments.
- Lessons learned.

Continuous improvement strengthens institutional intelligence.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs all analytics and decision intelligence activities throughout Aureus.

All Data SOPs, reporting standards, dashboard procedures, KPI governance, forecasting methodologies, business intelligence practices, and decision-support systems shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Data Governance Charter.

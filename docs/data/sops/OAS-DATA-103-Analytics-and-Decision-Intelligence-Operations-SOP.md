OAS-DATA-103 — Analytics and Decision Intelligence Operations Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Canonical Designation: OAS-DATA-103
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-DATA-004 — Analytics and Decision Intelligence Framework
Owner: Chief Data Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026
Review Cycle: Annual

---

Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes the canonical process for producing, governing, validating, publishing, reviewing, and continuously improving analytics, dashboards, reports, key performance indicators (KPIs), forecasts, and decision-support capabilities throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure institutional decisions are informed by accurate, trustworthy, timely, and constitutionally aligned information that advances Aureus' mission and strengthens stewardship.

---

Scope

This procedure applies to:

- Analytics
- Business intelligence
- Reporting
- Dashboards
- KPI governance
- Forecasting
- Decision-support
- Performance measurement
- AI-assisted analytics
- Institutional reporting

---

Objectives

Analytics operations shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Support evidence-based decisions.
- Maintain reporting consistency.
- Improve organizational learning.
- Strengthen accountability.
- Enable continuous improvement.

---

Roles and Responsibilities

Analytics Steward

Responsible for:

- Producing institutional reports.
- Maintaining dashboards.
- Monitoring KPIs.
- Coordinating analytics reviews.
- Preparing governance reports.

---

Metric Owners

Responsible for:

- Defining KPIs.
- Validating metric accuracy.
- Reviewing performance trends.
- Maintaining documentation.
- Supporting governance reviews.

---

AI Analytics Steward

Responsible for:

- Identifying trends.
- Preparing dashboards.
- Generating summaries.
- Detecting anomalies.
- Supporting forecasting.
- Producing analytical recommendations.

Human stewards remain responsible for interpreting results, approving reports, making strategic decisions, and governance actions.

---

Prerequisites

Before publishing institutional analytics:

- Source data is validated.
- Metrics are approved.
- Reporting standards are applied.
- Required reviews are completed.

---

Procedure

Step 1 — Data Preparation

Verify:

- Data quality.
- Completeness.
- Consistency.
- Approved sources.
- Metric definitions.

Resolve material issues before analysis.

---

Step 2 — Analytics Production

Generate:

- Reports.
- Dashboards.
- KPI summaries.
- Trend analyses.
- Forecasts.
- Decision-support materials.

Apply approved analytical methodologies.

---

Step 3 — Validation

Review outputs for:

- Accuracy.
- Consistency.
- Completeness.
- Reasonableness.
- Constitutional alignment where applicable.

Correct identified issues before publication.

---

Step 4 — Publication

Distribute analytics according to:

- Authorized access.
- Information classification.
- Governance requirements.
- Operational need.

Maintain version history where appropriate.

---

Step 5 — Performance Review

Conduct periodic reviews to evaluate:

- KPI performance.
- Strategic trends.
- Operational outcomes.
- Governance objectives.
- Improvement opportunities.

---

Step 6 — Forecasting and Decision Support

Prepare decision-support materials using:

- Historical trends.
- Current performance.
- Forecasting models.
- Risk considerations.
- Scenario analysis where appropriate.

Analytics shall inform stewardship without replacing human judgment.

---

Step 7 — Continuous Improvement

Improve analytics through:

- Metric refinement.
- Dashboard enhancements.
- Reporting improvements.
- Steward feedback.
- Governance recommendations.

---

Success Metrics

Monitor:

- Report accuracy.
- Dashboard utilization.
- KPI reliability.
- Forecast quality.
- Decision-support effectiveness.
- Governance compliance.
- Reporting timeliness.
- Institutional learning.

---

Exceptions

Urgent operational decisions may require expedited analytics while maintaining appropriate documentation and retrospective validation.

---

Documentation

Maintain:

- KPI definitions.
- Report inventories.
- Dashboard catalogs.
- Forecast documentation.
- Validation records.
- Governance reports.
- Metric reviews.
- Improvement plans.

---

Continuous Improvement

Analytics operations shall improve through metric reviews, stewardship feedback, AI-assisted analysis, governance evaluations, operational assessments, and lessons learned.

OAS-DATA-005 — Data Standards and Continuous Stewardship Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-DATA-005
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-DATA-001 — Data Governance Charter
Owner: Chief Data Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the canonical standards for data excellence throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure every data activity is conducted consistently, ethically, responsibly, transparently, and in alignment with the Constitution while strengthening Aureus' long-term institutional intelligence.

---

Mission

Data excellence exists to transform institutional information into a durable strategic asset through disciplined stewardship, measurable quality, responsible governance, continuous learning, and thoughtful innovation.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Data standards
- Data governance maturity
- Data quality assurance
- Knowledge stewardship
- Analytics excellence
- Metadata standards
- Institutional learning
- Continuous improvement

---

Guiding Principles

Data stewardship shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Promote trustworthy information.
- Encourage accountability.
- Respect privacy.
- Foster continuous learning.
- Preserve institutional knowledge.

Every data decision should strengthen both institutional capability and public trust.

---

Data Standards

Every data process shall define:

- Purpose.
- Scope.
- Responsible owner.
- Required approvals.
- Documentation standards.
- Performance measures.
- Review procedures.
- Improvement mechanisms.

Standards shall remain measurable, repeatable, and consistently applied.

---

Data Culture

Data stewards shall:

- Demonstrate responsible stewardship.
- Maintain high data quality.
- Protect institutional information.
- Follow approved governance standards.
- Share institutional knowledge.
- Pursue continuous learning.
- Support responsible innovation.

Data culture is strengthened through disciplined daily practice.

---

Data Quality Assurance

Data quality shall include:

- Validation.
- Verification.
- Consistency reviews.
- Metadata quality.
- Governance assessments.
- Corrective actions.
- Preventive improvements.

Quality shall be built into every information process.

---

Governance Maturity

Data governance shall mature through:

- Standardization.
- Measurement.
- Optimization.
- Innovation.
- Institutional learning.

Maturity reflects Aureus' ability to consistently steward institutional knowledge.

---

Performance Measurement

Data excellence shall evaluate:

- Data quality.
- Governance maturity.
- Metadata completeness.
- Analytics effectiveness.
- Institutional learning.
- Stewardship performance.
- Information reliability.
- Mission support.

Measurements shall drive meaningful institutional improvement.

---

Ethical Data Stewardship

Data stewardship shall demonstrate:

- Integrity.
- Responsibility.
- Transparency.
- Accountability.
- Professional judgment.
- Constitutional fidelity.

Data authority shall always be exercised responsibly.

---

Continuous Improvement

Data excellence shall improve through:

- Data quality reviews.
- Steward feedback.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Governance evaluations.
- Knowledge audits.
- Responsible experimentation.
- Lessons learned.

Continuous improvement is a permanent stewardship responsibility.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs the data quality standards of Aureus.

All Data SOPs, analytics procedures, metadata standards, knowledge management practices, governance processes, reporting standards, and information lifecycle procedures shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Data Governance Charter.

Aureus Communication System Architecture

Document 15 — Communication System Architecture

Version: 1.0
Status: Canonical

---

Purpose

This document defines the architecture, responsibilities, and governing principles of the Aureus Communication System.

The Communication System is responsible for delivering timely, relevant, secure, and trustworthy communication throughout the Aureus platform. It enables members, stewards, organizations, businesses, and AI systems to exchange information while maintaining clarity, privacy, and respect.

---

Mission

Deliver the right information to the right people at the right time through communication that is clear, purposeful, respectful, and trustworthy.

---

Core Responsibilities

The Communication System shall:

- Deliver notifications.
- Support direct messaging.
- Publish announcements.
- Send reminders.
- Manage alerts.
- Coordinate communication preferences.
- Maintain communication history where appropriate.

---

Communication Types

The Communication System supports:

- Platform notifications
- Direct messages
- Group messages
- Pod communications
- Organization announcements
- Business communications
- AI guidance messages
- Steward communications
- System alerts
- Reminders

Additional communication types may be added through future architectural revisions.

---

Core Components

The Communication System consists of:

- Notification service
- Messaging service
- Announcement service
- Reminder service
- Alert management
- Communication preferences
- Delivery tracking

---

Inputs

The Communication System receives information from:

- Member Core
- Journey Engine
- Opportunity Engine
- Academy
- Pods
- AI Intelligence Engine
- Business Portal
- Stewardship System
- Administration & Operations

---

Outputs

The Communication System provides:

- Notifications.
- Messages.
- Announcements.
- Alerts.
- Reminders.
- Delivery confirmations.
- Communication history.

---

Architectural Boundaries

The Communication System shall not:

- Replace the Knowledge System.
- Replace community interaction within Pods.
- Override member communication preferences except for critical platform or safety notifications.
- Deliver unauthorized communications.
- Operate outside established privacy and governance policies.

---

Relationship to Other Systems

The Communication System collaborates with:

- Member Core
- Journey Engine
- Opportunity Engine
- Academy
- Pods
- AI Intelligence Engine
- Business Portal
- Stewardship System
- Administration & Operations

Each system owns the events that generate communications, while the Communication System is responsible for reliable and consistent delivery.

---

Success Criteria

The Communication System succeeds when:

- Members receive timely and relevant information.
- Communications are understandable and actionable.
- Delivery is reliable.
- Member preferences are respected.
- Communication strengthens trust and engagement without overwhelming members.

---

Conclusion

The Communication System is the connective layer of Aureus. It ensures that information flows efficiently, securely, and respectfully between every participant and every system, strengthening coordination, trust, and member success throughout the platform.

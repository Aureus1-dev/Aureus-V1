Aureus Opportunity Engine Architecture

Document 7 — Opportunity Engine Architecture

Version: 1.0
Status: Canonical

---

Purpose

This document defines the architecture, responsibilities, and governing principles of the Aureus Opportunity Engine.

The Opportunity Engine is responsible for discovering, evaluating, organizing, prioritizing, and presenting legitimate opportunities that help members make measurable progress toward their goals.

It serves as the intelligence layer that transforms scattered opportunities into personalized recommendations.

---

Mission

Help every member discover the right opportunity at the right time through trusted, transparent, and personalized opportunity intelligence.

---

Core Responsibilities

The Opportunity Engine shall:

- Discover opportunities from trusted sources.
- Verify and organize opportunity information.
- Classify opportunities into structured categories.
- Rank opportunities according to member goals and preferences.
- Continuously monitor opportunities for changes.
- Present opportunities with clear explanations.
- Coordinate with the AI Intelligence Engine to personalize recommendations.

---

Opportunity Categories

The Opportunity Engine may manage opportunities including:

- Employment
- Education
- Scholarships
- Grants
- Government benefits
- Housing
- Financial assistance
- Banking incentives
- Credit-building opportunities
- Business opportunities
- Volunteer opportunities
- Community programs
- Health and wellness resources

Additional categories may be added through future architectural revisions.

---

Inputs

The Opportunity Engine receives information from:

- Trusted public and private data sources.
- Organization submissions.
- Business Portal submissions.
- Knowledge System.
- Member preferences.
- AI Intelligence Engine analysis.
- Steward review and verification.

---

Outputs

The Opportunity Engine provides:

- Personalized opportunity recommendations.
- Ranked opportunity lists.
- Opportunity summaries.
- Eligibility guidance.
- Application pathways.
- Opportunity status updates.
- Opportunity analytics.

---

Architectural Boundaries

The Opportunity Engine shall not:

- Apply on behalf of members without explicit authorization.
- Guarantee eligibility or outcomes.
- Replace member decision-making.
- Modify official opportunity data without authorized review.
- Operate outside established governance policies.

---

Relationship to Other Systems

The Opportunity Engine works closely with:

- Member Core
- AI Intelligence Engine
- Journey Engine
- Academy
- Business Portal
- Knowledge System
- Resource Directory
- Stewardship System

Each system retains ownership of its own data and responsibilities.

---

Success Criteria

The Opportunity Engine succeeds when it enables members to:

- Discover relevant opportunities quickly.
- Understand why opportunities are recommended.
- Take informed action with confidence.
- Reduce time spent searching.
- Increase meaningful outcomes through better opportunity matching.

---

Conclusion

The Opportunity Engine is the discovery and recommendation foundation of Aureus. It transforms fragmented information into organized opportunity intelligence, enabling members to make informed decisions and pursue meaningful progress throughout their lives.

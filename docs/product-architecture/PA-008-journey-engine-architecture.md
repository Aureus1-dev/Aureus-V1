Aureus Journey Engine Architecture

Document 8 — Journey Engine Architecture

Version: 1.0
Status: Canonical

---

Purpose

This document defines the architecture, responsibilities, and governing principles of the Aureus Journey Engine.

The Journey Engine is responsible for transforming member goals into structured, measurable journeys. It organizes opportunities, learning, milestones, and progress into a unified roadmap that helps members achieve meaningful long-term outcomes.

---

Mission

Help every member move from their current situation toward their desired future through clear planning, measurable progress, and continuous guidance.

---

Core Responsibilities

The Journey Engine shall:

- Create personalized journeys.
- Organize goals into actionable milestones.
- Track progress over time.
- Recommend next steps.
- Coordinate learning, opportunities, and community participation.
- Adapt journeys as member circumstances change.
- Maintain a historical record of progress.

---

Core Components

The Journey Engine consists of:

- Goal Management
- Journey Planning
- Milestone Tracking
- Action Plans
- Progress Measurement
- Habit Tracking
- Achievement History
- Journey Reviews

---

Inputs

The Journey Engine receives information from:

- Member Core
- Opportunity Engine
- AI Intelligence Engine
- Academy
- Pods
- Stewardship System
- Member feedback

---

Outputs

The Journey Engine provides:

- Personalized roadmaps.
- Action plans.
- Progress dashboards.
- Milestone updates.
- Journey recommendations.
- Achievement summaries.
- Suggested next actions.

---

Architectural Boundaries

The Journey Engine shall not:

- Replace member choice.
- Override member goals without authorization.
- Modify opportunities owned by the Opportunity Engine.
- Deliver educational content owned by the Academy.
- Operate outside established governance policies.

---

Relationship to Other Systems

The Journey Engine coordinates with:

- Member Core
- AI Intelligence Engine
- Opportunity Engine
- Academy
- Pods
- Knowledge System
- Communication System
- Stewardship System

Each system retains ownership of its own data while contributing to the member's journey.

---

Success Criteria

The Journey Engine succeeds when members:

- Clearly understand their next steps.
- Maintain momentum toward their goals.
- Measure meaningful progress.
- Adapt successfully to changing circumstances.
- Achieve long-term outcomes through sustained action.

---

Conclusion

The Journey Engine is the planning and progress foundation of Aureus. It transforms opportunities, education, and guidance into personalized journeys that help members make continuous, measurable progress throughout their lives.

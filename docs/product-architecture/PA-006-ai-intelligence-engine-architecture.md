Aureus AI Intelligence Engine Architecture

Document 6 — AI Intelligence Engine Architecture

Version: 1.0
Status: Canonical

---

Purpose

This document defines the architecture, responsibilities, and governing principles of the Aureus AI Intelligence Engine.

The AI Intelligence Engine is the platform's reasoning and guidance layer. Its purpose is to help members understand information, evaluate options, develop plans, and make informed decisions while preserving member agency and human stewardship.

The AI Intelligence Engine does not govern the platform or make decisions on behalf of members. It exists to support informed human judgment.

---

Mission

Transform information into clear, personalized, and actionable guidance that helps every member make meaningful progress.

---

Core Responsibilities

The AI Intelligence Engine shall:

- Analyze information from across the Aureus platform.
- Explain opportunities in understandable language.
- Recommend relevant actions based on member goals.
- Help members compare alternatives.
- Assist with planning and decision support.
- Personalize recommendations over time.
- Coordinate with other Aureus systems while respecting system ownership.

---

Core Capabilities

The AI Intelligence Engine supports:

- Opportunity analysis
- Decision support
- Journey planning
- Educational assistance
- Goal recommendations
- Progress insights
- Knowledge retrieval
- Resource recommendations
- Steward assistance

---

Inputs

The AI Intelligence Engine receives information from:

- Member Core
- Opportunity Engine
- Journey Engine
- Academy
- Pods
- Knowledge System
- Resource Directory
- Business Portal
- Stewardship System

---

Outputs

The AI Intelligence Engine provides:

- Personalized recommendations
- Action plans
- Explanations
- Summaries
- Progress insights
- Educational guidance
- Opportunity comparisons
- Suggested next steps

---

Architectural Boundaries

The AI Intelligence Engine shall not:

- Replace human decision-making.
- Override member choices.
- Modify institutional governance.
- Change official platform records without authorization.
- Operate outside the permissions granted by the platform.

---

Relationship to Human Stewardship

AI exists to assist, not replace, human stewardship.

Members remain responsible for their decisions.

Stewards remain responsible for governance, accountability, and community support.

---

Success Criteria

The AI Intelligence Engine succeeds when it helps members:

- Understand their options.
- Reduce confusion.
- Save time.
- Learn continuously.
- Make better-informed decisions.
- Achieve meaningful progress while preserving trust and autonomy.

---

Conclusion

The AI Intelligence Engine serves as Aureus' reasoning layer, connecting information, knowledge, and opportunities into practical guidance. It is designed to enhance human flourishing through trustworthy assistance while preserving member agency and institutional stewardship.

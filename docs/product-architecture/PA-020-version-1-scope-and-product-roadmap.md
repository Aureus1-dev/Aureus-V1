Aureus Version 1 Scope & Product Roadmap

Document 20 — Version 1 Scope & Product Roadmap

Version: 1.0
Status: Canonical

---

Purpose

This document establishes the official scope of Aureus Version 1 and defines the guiding roadmap for future platform evolution.

Its purpose is to maintain implementation discipline by clearly identifying what will be built for Version 1, what is intentionally deferred, and how future versions will expand without compromising the architectural integrity established in PA-001 through PA-019.

---

Mission

Deliver a focused, high-quality Version 1 that provides meaningful value while establishing a stable foundation for long-term growth.

---

Version 1 Objectives

Version 1 shall:

- Deliver a complete member experience.
- Support personalized journeys.
- Provide trusted opportunity discovery.
- Deliver AI-assisted guidance.
- Enable lifelong learning through the Academy.
- Support Pods for community and accountability.
- Enable organizational participation through the Business Portal.
- Preserve constitutional governance and stewardship.
- Establish a scalable platform architecture.

---

Version 1 Includes

The following systems are included in Version 1:

- Member Core
- Opportunity Engine
- AI Intelligence Engine
- Journey Engine
- Pods
- Academy
- Business Portal
- Stewardship System
- Knowledge System
- Resource Directory
- Communication System
- Administration & Operations

Each system shall initially focus on its essential capabilities before expanding in later releases.

---

Version 1 Excludes

Unless specifically approved, Version 1 shall not include:

- Experimental features
- Advanced enterprise capabilities
- Large-scale automation beyond approved AI responsibilities
- Major internationalization efforts
- Non-essential integrations
- Features that duplicate existing functionality
- Capabilities that significantly delay launch without delivering proportional member value

---

Product Evolution Principles

Future versions shall:

- Build upon the canonical architecture.
- Preserve clear system ownership.
- Maintain backward compatibility where practical.
- Prioritize member value over feature quantity.
- Simplify before expanding.
- Remain aligned with the Aureus Constitution.

---

Roadmap Philosophy

Future development shall be guided by:

1. Member benefit
2. Mission alignment
3. Architectural consistency
4. Technical sustainability
5. Stewardship and trust
6. Long-term institutional resilience

---

Success Criteria

Version 1 succeeds when:

- Members achieve meaningful progress.
- The platform demonstrates architectural stability.
- Core systems work together effectively.
- Future expansion can occur without major redesign.
- Aureus establishes a trustworthy foundation for long-term growth.

---

Conclusion

PA-020 completes the foundational Product Architecture series for Aureus Version 1. Together, PA-001 through PA-020 establish the canonical architectural framework upon which implementation, work orders, technical design, and future product evolution will be built.

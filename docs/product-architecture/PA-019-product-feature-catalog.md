Aureus Product Feature Catalog

Document 19 — Product Feature Catalog

Version: 1.0
Status: Canonical

---

Purpose

This document serves as the master inventory of every planned product feature within Aureus.

Every feature shall belong to exactly one core product system established in PA-001 through PA-018. This catalog provides the authoritative index for future implementation planning, work orders, prioritization, and Version 1 scope decisions.

---

Mission

Maintain a complete, organized, and traceable catalog of Aureus capabilities that aligns every feature with the platform architecture.

---

Architectural Principles

Every feature shall:

- Belong to one—and only one—core system.
- Support the Aureus mission.
- Have a clearly defined purpose.
- Avoid duplicating existing functionality.
- Be eligible for prioritization into Version 1, Version 2, or a future release.

---

Success Criteria

The Product Feature Catalog succeeds when:

- Every planned capability has an architectural owner.
- Duplicate functionality is eliminated.
- Implementation teams can trace every feature to the platform architecture.
- Version planning becomes predictable and manageable.

---

Conclusion

The Product Feature Catalog is the authoritative inventory of Aureus functionality. It connects the platform architecture to implementation planning and ensures that every future capability has a defined purpose, owner, and place within the Aureus ecosystem.

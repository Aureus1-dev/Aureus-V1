Aureus Resource Directory Architecture

Document 14 — Resource Directory Architecture

Version: 1.0
Status: Canonical

---

Purpose

This document defines the architecture, responsibilities, and governing principles of the Aureus Resource Directory.

The Resource Directory is Aureus' trusted catalog of external organizations, programs, services, professionals, tools, and community resources. Its purpose is to connect members with reliable resources that help them achieve meaningful progress.

---

Mission

Maintain a trusted, searchable, and continuously updated directory of high-quality resources that support members throughout every stage of their journey.

---

Core Responsibilities

The Resource Directory shall:

- Maintain verified resource listings.
- Organize resources into structured categories.
- Support location-aware resource discovery.
- Connect members with relevant services and organizations.
- Monitor resource quality and availability.
- Coordinate with the Opportunity Engine and Knowledge System.

---

Resource Categories

The Resource Directory may include:

- Government agencies
- Nonprofit organizations
- Community organizations
- Educational institutions
- Healthcare providers
- Financial services
- Legal services
- Housing resources
- Employment services
- Business support organizations
- Technology tools
- Mental health and wellness resources

Additional categories may be added through future architectural revisions.

---

Core Components

The Resource Directory consists of:

- Resource catalog
- Category management
- Geographic indexing
- Verification workflow
- Search and filtering
- Quality review
- Resource analytics

---

Inputs

The Resource Directory receives information from:

- Stewardship System
- Business Portal
- Organizations
- Knowledge System
- Platform Administration
- Approved external sources

---

Outputs

The Resource Directory provides:

- Verified resource listings.
- Search results.
- Resource recommendations.
- Organization information.
- Contact information.
- Geographic resource matching.

---

Architectural Boundaries

The Resource Directory shall not:

- Replace the Opportunity Engine.
- Replace the Knowledge System.
- Publish unverified resources as trusted.
- Guarantee the quality or availability of third-party services.
- Operate outside established review and governance policies.

---

Relationship to Other Systems

The Resource Directory collaborates with:

- Opportunity Engine
- Knowledge System
- AI Intelligence Engine
- Member Core
- Journey Engine
- Business Portal
- Stewardship System

Each system retains ownership of its own responsibilities while the Resource Directory serves as the trusted catalog of external resources.

---

Success Criteria

The Resource Directory succeeds when:

- Members quickly find relevant and trustworthy resources.
- Resource information remains accurate and current.
- Organizations are easy to discover.
- AI recommendations are supported by verified resources.
- Members spend less time searching and more time taking action.

---

Conclusion

The Resource Directory is Aureus' trusted external resource network. It connects members with organizations, services, and programs that complement opportunities, learning, and journeys while maintaining high standards of quality, transparency, and trust.

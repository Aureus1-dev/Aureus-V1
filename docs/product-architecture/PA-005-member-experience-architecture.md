Aureus User Journey Architecture

Document 4 — User Journey Architecture

Version: 1.0
Status: Canonical

---

Purpose

This document defines the canonical lifecycle of a member within Aureus. It establishes how a person progresses from first discovering the platform to becoming a flourishing member and, where appropriate, a steward who contributes back to the community.

Every feature, workflow, AI interaction, and community experience should support one or more stages of this journey.

---

Journey Principles

- Every member has a unique journey.
- Aureus provides guidance, not coercion.
- Progress should be measurable.
- Members retain agency over their decisions.
- AI and human stewardship work together to support the member.
- The journey is continuous rather than linear.

---

Canonical Journey Stages

Stage 1 — Discover

The individual becomes aware of Aureus.

Goals:

- Learn what Aureus is.
- Understand its mission.
- Decide whether to join.

---

Stage 2 — Join

The individual creates a member account.

Goals:

- Establish identity.
- Complete onboarding.
- Set initial goals.
- Meet their AI Steward.

---

Stage 3 — Discover Opportunities

Members begin exploring opportunities aligned with their goals.

Examples include:

- Employment
- Education
- Financial opportunities
- Community resources
- Business opportunities

---

Stage 4 — Build a Journey

Members organize opportunities into a personalized plan.

Goals:

- Define milestones.
- Prioritize actions.
- Track progress.

---

Stage 5 — Learn & Grow

Members develop new knowledge and skills through the Academy, AI guidance, and community participation.

---

Stage 6 — Connect

Members engage with Pods, organizations, businesses, and fellow members.

Goals:

- Build relationships.
- Collaborate.
- Receive accountability.
- Contribute knowledge.

---

Stage 7 — Achieve Outcomes

Members accomplish meaningful goals and improve their quality of life.

Outcomes may include:

- Employment
- Education
- Financial stability
- Business creation
- Personal growth
- Community impact

---

Stage 8 — Stewardship

Members who are prepared and qualified may choose to serve others.

Stewards:

- Guide members.
- Strengthen the community.
- Protect platform quality.
- Preserve institutional trust.

---

Continuous Growth

The Aureus journey does not end after one success.

Members continually:

- Discover new opportunities.
- Learn new skills.
- Pursue new goals.
- Help others flourish.

This creates a lifelong cycle of growth, contribution, and stewardship.

---

Architectural Rule

Every major feature within Aureus shall support at least one stage of the canonical member journey.

Features that do not meaningfully contribute to the member journey should be redesigned, reassigned, or excluded from Version 1.

---

Conclusion

The User Journey Architecture defines the lifecycle that guides the design of every experience within Aureus. It provides the foundation for aligning product features, AI guidance, community systems, and organizational participation around a coherent path of lifelong progress.

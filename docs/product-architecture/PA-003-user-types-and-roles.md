Aureus User Types & Roles

Document 3 — User Types & Roles

Version: 1.0
Status: Canonical

---

Purpose

This document defines every actor that interacts with the Aureus platform and establishes their role within the ecosystem.

A "User Type" identifies who is interacting with Aureus.

A "Role" defines the permissions and responsibilities that user has within the platform.

Every capability within Aureus shall be assigned to one or more defined roles.

---

Architectural Principles

- Every person interacts with Aureus through a defined User Type.
- Every User Type may possess one or more Roles.
- Roles determine permissions; User Types determine context.
- Permissions shall be granted according to the principle of least privilege.
- Users may gain or lose Roles over time without changing their User Type.

---

Core User Types

1. Visitor

A person who has not yet joined Aureus.

Primary Goals:

- Learn about Aureus
- Explore public information
- Create an account

---

2. Member

The primary participant within Aureus.

Members use the platform to:

- Discover opportunities
- Learn through the Academy
- Join Pods
- Build journeys
- Receive AI guidance
- Track progress

---

3. Steward

A trusted member who provides guidance, moderation, accountability, and support.

Stewards help strengthen the community while preserving trust and quality.

---

4. Organization

A nonprofit, educational institution, government agency, or community organization that participates in the Aureus ecosystem.

Organizations may:

- Publish opportunities
- Share resources
- Support members
- Form partnerships

---

5. Business

A commercial organization participating through the Business Portal.

Businesses may:

- Publish opportunities
- Recruit talent
- Offer products or services
- Build partnerships

---

6. Platform Administrator

Responsible for platform administration.

Responsibilities include:

- System configuration
- Platform operations
- Security
- Moderation oversight
- Permission management

---

7. AI Steward

The platform's AI guidance system.

Responsibilities include:

- Personalized guidance
- Opportunity recommendations
- Journey assistance
- Educational support
- Decision support

The AI Steward assists members but does not replace human judgment or institutional governance.

---

Role Assignment Principles

A user may possess multiple roles simultaneously.

Examples include:

- Member + Steward
- Member + Business Owner
- Member + Organization Representative
- Steward + Administrator

Role assignments shall remain flexible while maintaining clear permission boundaries.

---

Future User Types

Future platform expansions may introduce additional User Types as needed, provided they are approved through the Aureus architectural governance process and documented within this architecture.

---

Conclusion

This document establishes the canonical User Types and Roles for Aureus Version 1.

All future permissions, workflows, features, and user journeys shall reference these definitions.

Aureus Stewardship System Architecture

Document 12 — Stewardship System Architecture

Version: 1.0
Status: Canonical

---

Purpose

This document defines the architecture, responsibilities, and governing principles of the Aureus Stewardship System.

The Stewardship System is the human leadership and accountability layer of Aureus. It exists to protect trust, uphold the platform's mission, support members, guide healthy communities, and ensure that the platform remains aligned with its constitutional principles.

---

Mission

Cultivate a trustworthy community where human stewardship complements AI guidance, protects member well-being, and strengthens the Aureus mission through service, accountability, and ethical leadership.

---

Core Responsibilities

The Stewardship System shall:

- Support and guide members.
- Provide mentorship and accountability.
- Moderate communities and Pods.
- Resolve conflicts and escalate issues appropriately.
- Review sensitive cases requiring human judgment.
- Protect institutional values and community standards.
- Encourage leadership development and future stewards.

---

Steward Roles

The Stewardship System may include:

- Community Stewards
- Pod Stewards
- Academy Stewards
- Opportunity Stewards
- Organizational Stewards
- Platform Stewards

Additional stewardship roles may be created through future constitutional or architectural revisions.

---

Core Components

The Stewardship System consists of:

- Steward assignment
- Member support
- Community moderation
- Escalation management
- Mentorship
- Steward training
- Steward performance review
- Leadership development

---

Inputs

The Stewardship System receives information from:

- Member Core
- Pods
- AI Intelligence Engine
- Journey Engine
- Academy
- Business Portal
- Administration & Operations
- Member reports and feedback

---

Outputs

The Stewardship System provides:

- Member guidance.
- Conflict resolution.
- Steward recommendations.
- Community health insights.
- Escalation outcomes.
- Leadership development.
- Institutional accountability.

---

Architectural Boundaries

The Stewardship System shall not:

- Replace member autonomy.
- Override constitutional governance.
- Replace AI systems where automation is appropriate.
- Access information beyond authorized permissions.
- Exercise authority outside its defined responsibilities.

---

Relationship to Other Systems

The Stewardship System collaborates with:

- Member Core
- Pods
- Academy
- AI Intelligence Engine
- Journey Engine
- Opportunity Engine
- Business Portal
- Communication System
- Administration & Operations

Each system retains ownership of its own responsibilities while the Stewardship System provides human guidance and oversight.

---

Success Criteria

The Stewardship System succeeds when:

- Members trust the platform.
- Communities remain healthy and constructive.
- Conflicts are resolved fairly.
- Members receive timely guidance.
- Future leaders are developed.
- Human judgment strengthens, rather than replaces, AI-assisted experiences.

---

Conclusion

The Stewardship System is the human leadership foundation of Aureus. It ensures that technology remains accountable to people, that communities remain healthy, and that every member has access to trusted guidance throughout their journey while preserving the mission and constitutional principles of the platform.

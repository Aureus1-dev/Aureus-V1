OAS-OPS-008 — Operational Risk and Business Continuity Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-OPS-008
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-OPS-002 — Operations Governance Framework
Owner: Chief Operations Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the canonical model for identifying, assessing, mitigating, monitoring, and recovering from operational risks throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure Aureus remains resilient, capable of maintaining essential operations, protecting members, and fulfilling its mission during both routine disruptions and extraordinary events.

---

Mission

Operational risk management and business continuity exist to preserve institutional resilience, protect members, sustain critical services, and enable Aureus to continue advancing its mission under all reasonably foreseeable conditions.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Operational risk management
- Business continuity
- Operational resilience
- Crisis management
- Contingency planning
- Service continuity
- Recovery planning
- AI operational resilience
- Cross-functional emergency coordination
- Post-event recovery

---

Guiding Principles

Operational resilience shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Minimize operational disruption.
- Promote preparedness.
- Encourage resilience.
- Support coordinated response.
- Strengthen institutional learning.

Preparation is an act of stewardship.

---

Risk Management

Operational risks shall be:

- Identified.
- Assessed.
- Prioritized.
- Mitigated.
- Monitored.
- Reviewed regularly.

Risk management shall remain proactive rather than reactive.

---

Business Continuity

Business continuity planning shall identify:

- Critical services.
- Essential operational functions.
- Key personnel.
- AI operational dependencies.
- Technology dependencies.
- External dependencies.
- Recovery priorities.

Continuity planning shall preserve essential institutional capabilities.

---

Operational Resilience

Operational resilience shall include:

- Redundant capabilities.
- Documented contingency procedures.
- Cross-training where appropriate.
- Recovery planning.
- Communication procedures.
- Regular resilience assessments.

Resilience enables sustained service during disruption.

---

Crisis Management

Operational crises shall follow documented procedures for:

- Detection.
- Assessment.
- Activation.
- Coordination.
- Communication.
- Recovery.
- Review.

Leadership responsibilities shall remain clearly defined.

---

Recovery Planning

Recovery plans shall define:

- Recovery priorities.
- Recovery objectives.
- Required resources.
- Responsible stewards.
- Communication expectations.
- Verification procedures.

Recovery activities shall prioritize member wellbeing and institutional stability.

---

AI Operational Resilience

AI-supported operations shall include:

- Human fallback procedures.
- Alternative workflows.
- Operational safeguards.
- Monitoring.
- Recovery verification.

Critical services shall not depend solely upon a single AI capability.

---

Performance Measurement

Operational resilience shall be evaluated through:

- Recovery performance.
- Service continuity.
- Operational readiness.
- Risk reduction.
- Crisis response effectiveness.
- Member impact.
- Recovery exercise results.

Performance supports continual strengthening of resilience.

---

Continuous Improvement

Operational resilience shall improve through:

- Risk reviews.
- Recovery exercises.
- Incident analysis.
- Steward feedback.
- Member feedback.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Lessons learned.

Continuous improvement strengthens institutional preparedness.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs all operational risk management and business continuity activities throughout Aureus.

All continuity plans, crisis procedures, contingency plans, resilience standards, emergency operations, recovery procedures, and Operations SOPs shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Operations Charter.

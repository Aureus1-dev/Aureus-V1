OAS-OPS-004 — Member Service Delivery Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-OPS-004
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-OPS-002 — Operations Governance Framework
Owner: Chief Member Experience Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the canonical model for designing, delivering, supporting, measuring, and continuously improving every service provided to Aureus members.

Its purpose is to ensure every member receives a consistent, trustworthy, effective, and mission-aligned experience regardless of the service, steward, AI system, or delivery channel involved.

---

Mission

Member service delivery exists to help every member make meaningful progress toward flourishing by providing reliable guidance, responsive support, high-quality services, and continuous stewardship throughout their journey with Aureus.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Member onboarding
- Service delivery
- Member support
- AI-assisted member services
- Service quality
- Member communications
- Service recovery
- Member experience measurement
- Continuous service improvement
- Cross-functional service coordination

---

Guiding Principles

Member services shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Treat every member with dignity and respect.
- Promote consistency.
- Encourage responsiveness.
- Support continuous improvement.
- Build long-term trust.

Every service interaction is an opportunity to strengthen the member.

---

Service Design

Every member service shall include documented:

- Purpose.
- Target members.
- Eligibility.
- Service objectives.
- Expected outcomes.
- Quality standards.
- Success measures.
- Responsible steward.

Services should remain understandable, accessible, and measurable.

---

Member Journey

Member journeys should include:

- Discovery.
- Enrollment.
- Onboarding.
- Active participation.
- Ongoing support.
- Progress reviews.
- Service completion.
- Continued engagement where appropriate.

Each stage should provide clarity regarding the member's next step.

---

Service Delivery

Services shall be delivered through:

- Human stewards.
- AI stewards.
- Human-AI collaborative workflows.
- Digital platforms.
- Approved communication channels.

Delivery methods should maximize quality while preserving efficiency and trust.

---

Service Quality

Service quality shall be evaluated using:

- Accuracy.
- Timeliness.
- Professionalism.
- Reliability.
- Member satisfaction.
- Goal achievement.
- Steward responsiveness.

Quality standards shall be documented and reviewed regularly.

---

Member Support

Support services shall:

- Resolve member concerns promptly.
- Escalate complex matters appropriately.
- Maintain respectful communication.
- Protect confidentiality.
- Record significant interactions where appropriate.

Support should strengthen member confidence in Aureus.

---

Service Recovery

When service expectations are not met:

- Acknowledge the issue.
- Investigate the cause.
- Resolve the concern.
- Communicate clearly.
- Document lessons learned.
- Improve future service delivery.

Recovery strengthens institutional trust when handled well.

---

Performance Measurement

Member service performance shall include:

- Member satisfaction.
- Service completion.
- Response times.
- Resolution times.
- Member retention.
- Goal achievement.
- Operational efficiency.

Performance measurement supports learning and improvement.

---

Continuous Improvement

Member services shall improve through:

- Member feedback.
- Steward feedback.
- Service reviews.
- Operational metrics.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Lessons learned.
- Service redesign where appropriate.

Continuous improvement is essential to long-term excellence.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs all member-facing services operated by Aureus.

All Operations SOPs, onboarding procedures, member support standards, AI service workflows, communication standards, and service delivery procedures shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Operations Charter.

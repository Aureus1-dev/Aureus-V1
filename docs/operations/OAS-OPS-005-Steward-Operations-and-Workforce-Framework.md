OAS-OPS-005 — Steward Operations and Workforce Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-OPS-005
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-OPS-002 — Operations Governance Framework
Owner: Chief Stewardship Officer
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the canonical governance model for every steward serving within Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure all stewards are recruited, onboarded, trained, supported, evaluated, developed, and held accountable according to the mission, Constitution, and stewardship principles of Aureus.

---

Mission

Steward operations exist to equip every steward to faithfully serve members, strengthen the institution, collaborate effectively with AI systems, and continuously grow in competence, wisdom, and service.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Steward recruitment
- Steward onboarding
- Training and education
- Role assignments
- Workforce planning
- AI collaboration
- Performance management
- Professional development
- Succession planning
- Steward wellbeing

---

Guiding Principles

Steward operations shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Promote servant leadership.
- Encourage continuous learning.
- Foster accountability.
- Support collaboration.
- Strengthen institutional resilience.

Every steward is entrusted with institutional responsibility.

---

Steward Lifecycle

The stewardship lifecycle shall include:

- Recruitment.
- Selection.
- Onboarding.
- Training.
- Active service.
- Performance review.
- Professional development.
- Leadership development.
- Succession planning.
- Transition or retirement.

Each stage shall have documented expectations and support.

---

Recruitment

Steward recruitment shall evaluate:

- Mission alignment.
- Character.
- Competence.
- Stewardship potential.
- Collaboration.
- Commitment to continuous learning.

Recruitment should prioritize long-term institutional fit.

---

Onboarding

Every steward shall receive structured onboarding covering:

- Constitution.
- Mission.
- Governance.
- Operational procedures.
- Technology platforms.
- AI collaboration.
- Ethics.
- Role-specific responsibilities.

No steward should begin independent service without appropriate preparation.

---

Training and Development

Steward development shall include:

- Initial training.
- Continuing education.
- Operational exercises.
- Leadership development.
- Technical learning.
- AI-assisted learning.
- Knowledge sharing.

Learning shall continue throughout active service.

---

AI Collaboration

Stewards shall collaborate effectively with approved AI systems.

AI may assist through:

- Administrative support.
- Knowledge retrieval.
- Workflow coordination.
- Operational recommendations.
- Documentation.
- Analysis.

Human stewards remain responsible for constitutional interpretation, ethical judgment, and significant operational decisions.

---

Performance Management

Steward performance shall consider:

- Member outcomes.
- Service quality.
- Reliability.
- Collaboration.
- Professional growth.
- Constitutional compliance.
- Stewardship conduct.

Performance management exists to strengthen stewards rather than merely evaluate them.

---

Succession Planning

Critical operational roles shall maintain documented succession plans to preserve institutional continuity and operational resilience.

---

Steward Wellbeing

Operations shall promote steward wellbeing through:

- Sustainable workloads.
- Appropriate support.
- Continuing education.
- Constructive feedback.
- Recognition of service.
- Healthy collaboration.

Healthy stewards strengthen healthy institutions.

---

Continuous Improvement

Steward operations shall improve through:

- Performance reviews.
- Steward feedback.
- Member feedback.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Operational reviews.
- Leadership assessments.
- Lessons learned.

Continuous improvement strengthens long-term stewardship.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs all steward-related operational activities throughout Aureus.

All steward onboarding procedures, training programs, workforce standards, leadership development initiatives, AI collaboration procedures, and operational SOPs shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Operations Charter.

Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-OPS-004 — Member Service Delivery Framework
Owner: Chief Member Experience Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026
Review Cycle: Annual

---

Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes the canonical process for providing continuous stewardship to every Aureus member throughout their relationship with the institution.

Its purpose is to ensure members receive ongoing guidance, meaningful support, opportunity discovery, progress monitoring, and coordinated human-AI stewardship that advances their long-term flourishing.

---

Scope

This procedure applies to:

- Active members
- AI stewardship
- Human stewardship
- Progress reviews
- Opportunity discovery
- Goal management
- Service coordination
- Member engagement
- Long-term support

---

Objectives

Member stewardship shall:

- Strengthen members.
- Encourage measurable progress.
- Promote long-term flourishing.
- Build trusted relationships.
- Coordinate appropriate services.
- Support informed decision-making.
- Maintain regular engagement.
- Encourage continuous growth.

---

Roles and Responsibilities

Member Steward

Responsible for:

- Supporting member progress.
- Conducting periodic reviews.
- Coordinating services.
- Escalating significant concerns.
- Maintaining stewardship records.

---

AI Steward

Responsible for:

- Monitoring progress.
- Identifying opportunities.
- Providing recommendations.
- Coordinating reminders.
- Answering routine questions.
- Escalating matters requiring human judgment.

Human stewards remain responsible for constitutional interpretation, ethical judgment, and member welfare.

---

Prerequisites

Before ongoing stewardship:

- Member onboarding is complete.
- AI steward is assigned.
- Initial goals are documented.
- Communication preferences are confirmed.

---

Procedure

Step 1 — Establish Stewardship Relationship

Introduce ongoing stewardship by explaining:

- Available support.
- Steward responsibilities.
- Member responsibilities.
- Communication expectations.
- Review schedule.

Members should understand that stewardship is a continuing relationship.

---

Step 2 — Monitor Progress

Review:

- Goal advancement.
- Member engagement.
- Service utilization.
- Challenges encountered.
- Opportunities identified.

Monitoring should encourage growth rather than surveillance.

---

Step 3 — Opportunity Discovery

Identify opportunities appropriate to the member's:

- Goals.
- Interests.
- Skills.
- Circumstances.
- Preferences.

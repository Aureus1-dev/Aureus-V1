OAS-OPS-101 — Member Onboarding and Enrollment Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Canonical Designation: OAS-OPS-101
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-OPS-004 — Member Service Delivery Framework
Owner: Chief Member Experience Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026
Review Cycle: Annual

---

Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes the canonical process for welcoming, enrolling, onboarding, orienting, and activating every new Aureus member.

Its purpose is to ensure every member receives a consistent, supportive, transparent, and mission-aligned introduction to Aureus while preparing them for long-term success.

---

Scope

This procedure applies to:

- Prospective members
- New member enrollment
- Account activation
- Member orientation
- AI steward assignment
- Goal setting
- Platform activation
- Initial support
- Onboarding completion

---

Objectives

Member onboarding shall:

- Welcome every member with dignity.
- Explain the Aureus mission.
- Introduce institutional governance.
- Activate required services.
- Establish initial goals.
- Assign appropriate support.
- Prepare members for success.
- Build long-term trust.

---

Roles and Responsibilities

Member Experience Steward

Responsible for:

- Guiding onboarding.
- Answering member questions.
- Verifying enrollment requirements.
- Coordinating onboarding activities.
- Confirming onboarding completion.

---

AI Steward

Responsible for:

- Guiding platform navigation.
- Explaining available services.
- Assisting with goal setting.
- Providing knowledge resources.
- Monitoring onboarding progress.
- Escalating concerns when appropriate.

Human stewards remain responsible for member welfare and significant decisions.

---

Prerequisites

Before enrollment:

- Membership eligibility is verified.
- Required information is collected.
- Terms are accepted.
- Privacy requirements are acknowledged.
- Initial communication preferences are established.

---

Procedure

Step 1 — Initial Welcome

Provide:

- Welcome message.
- Mission overview.
- Membership expectations.
- Overview of available services.

Members should understand Aureus' purpose before continuing.

---

Step 2 — Enrollment

Complete:

- Account creation.
- Identity verification where required.
- Membership activation.
- Communication preferences.
- Initial profile setup.

---

Step 3 — Orientation

Introduce:

- Constitution and mission.
- Stewardship principles.
- Platform capabilities.
- Member responsibilities.
- Available support.

Orientation should build confidence rather than overwhelm.

---

Step 4 — AI Steward Assignment

Assign an AI steward appropriate to the member's needs.

The AI steward shall:

- Introduce itself.
- Explain its role.
- Establish communication.
- Assist with navigation.
- Coordinate with human stewards when necessary.

---

Step 5 — Goal Setting

Help members establish:

- Initial objectives.
- Areas of interest.
- Desired outcomes.
- Preferred pace of engagement.

Goals should remain flexible and member-directed.

---

Step 6 — Platform Activation

Verify:

- Account functionality.
- Service access.
- Notification settings.
- Security settings.
- Resource availability.

---

Step 7 — Initial Support

Provide:

- Frequently asked questions.
- Contact options.
- Learning resources.
- Next recommended actions.

Members should always know their next step.

---

Step 8 — Onboarding Completion

Confirm:

- Enrollment complete.
- Orientation complete.
- AI steward assigned.
- Goals established.
- Platform activated.
- Member questions addressed.

Record successful onboarding completion.

---

Success Metrics

Monitor:

- Enrollment completion rate.
- Time to onboarding completion.
- Member satisfaction.
- Platform activation rate.
- Initial engagement.
- Support requests.
- Goal completion initiation.
- Member retention.

---

Exceptions

Special onboarding accommodations may be provided for accessibility, language, legal, or operational needs while preserving constitutional consistency.

---

Documentation

Maintain:

- Enrollment records.
- Onboarding completion records.
- Goal summaries.
- AI steward assignments.
- Member acknowledgments.
- Support interactions.
- Feedback results.

---

Continuous Improvement

Member onboarding shall improve through member feedback, steward feedback, operational reviews, AI-assisted analysis, onboarding metrics, and lessons learned.

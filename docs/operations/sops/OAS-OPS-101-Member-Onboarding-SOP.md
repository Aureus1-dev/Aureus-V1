OAS-OPS-101 — Member Onboarding Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-OPS-008 — Member Operations Framework
Owner: Chief Member Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026
Review Cycle: Quarterly

---

Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes the canonical process for welcoming, orienting, and preparing every new Aureus member for successful participation.

The onboarding experience should build trust, create clarity, establish belonging, and prepare members to begin making measurable progress toward their goals.

---

Scope

This procedure applies to every individual accepted into Aureus unless another approved onboarding procedure expressly applies.

---

Objectives

Every onboarding should:

- Welcome the member.
- Introduce the Aureus mission.
- Explain member expectations.
- Establish initial goals.
- Connect the member with appropriate resources.
- Assign stewardship support.
- Ensure the member understands how to begin.

---

Roles and Responsibilities

Human Steward

Responsible for:

- Relationship building
- Answering questions
- Goal discussion
- Final onboarding verification
- Escalating concerns

AI Operational Agent

Responsible for:

- Sending welcome communications
- Preparing onboarding materials
- Scheduling reminders
- Tracking completion
- Recording progress
- Answering routine questions within approved authority

---

Prerequisites

Before onboarding begins:

- Membership is approved.
- Required information has been received.
- Member account is created.
- Required documentation is available.

---

Procedure

Step 1 — Welcome

Send a personalized welcome message.

Introduce Aureus' mission and commitment to stewardship.

---

Step 2 — Account Verification

Confirm that the member can successfully access the platform and required systems.

---

Step 3 — Orientation

Provide an overview of:

- Mission
- Values
- Community expectations
- Available resources
- Member responsibilities

---

Step 4 — Goal Discovery

Assist the member in identifying their initial goals and desired outcomes.

Document those goals for future stewardship.

---

Step 5 — Steward Assignment

Assign an appropriate human steward and, where applicable, supporting AI operational agents.

Explain each role to the member.

---

Step 6 — Resource Introduction

Introduce the member to relevant:

- Knowledge resources
- Opportunities
- Community spaces
- Communication channels
- Support processes

---

Step 7 — Questions

Provide an opportunity for the member to ask questions and receive clarification.

---

Step 8 — Completion Verification

Confirm that the member:

- Understands the mission.
- Can access required systems.
- Knows where to seek help.
- Has completed required onboarding steps.
- Is prepared to begin participating.

---

Success Metrics

The onboarding process should monitor:

- Completion rate
- Time to completion
- Member satisfaction
- Early engagement
- Resource utilization
- Initial goal completion

---

Exceptions

If onboarding cannot be completed:

- Document the reason.
- Notify the assigned steward.
- Schedule follow-up.
- Escalate unresolved issues when appropriate.

---

Documentation

The onboarding record should include:

- Completion date
- Responsible steward
- AI support used
- Initial goals
- Outstanding actions
- Follow-up schedule

---

Continuous Improvement

Member feedback, steward feedback, and operational performance should be reviewed regularly to improve the onboarding experience.

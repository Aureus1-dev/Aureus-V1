OAS-COM-001 — Communications and Public Engagement Charter

Canonical Designation: OAS-COM-001
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-001 — Founding Charter
Owner: Chief Communications Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Charter establishes the constitutional authority governing internal communications, public communications, member communications, media relations, educational outreach, publications, branding coordination, crisis communications, and institutional trust throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure Aureus communicates truthfully, consistently, transparently, respectfully, and responsibly while preserving constitutional alignment, protecting members, strengthening public trust, and faithfully advancing its mission.

---

Mission

Communications stewardship exists to cultivate understanding, strengthen relationships, build trust, share knowledge, and faithfully represent Aureus' mission through clear, accurate, and responsible communication.

---

Scope

This Charter governs:

- Internal communications
- Member communications
- Public communications
- Media relations
- Publications
- Educational outreach
- Branding coordination
- Crisis communications
- Community engagement
- Institutional communications

---

Guiding Principles

Communications stewardship shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Promote truthfulness.
- Strengthen transparency.
- Encourage respectful dialogue.
- Foster public trust.
- Support continuous learning.

Communications shall always strengthen understanding rather than confusion.

---

Communications Authority

Communications authority shall be exercised according to:

- The Constitution.
- Approved governance documents.
- Applicable law.
- Delegated authority.
- Stewardship responsibilities.

Authority shall remain documented, transparent, and accountable.

---

Communications Governance

Communications governance shall include:

- Clearly defined responsibilities.
- Communication standards.
- Brand stewardship.
- Public engagement.
- Media coordination.
- Continuous improvement.

Governance shall balance institutional consistency with thoughtful communication.

---

Communications Stewardship

Communications stewardship shall:

- Represent Aureus accurately.
- Maintain consistent messaging.
- Protect institutional trust.
- Support informed decision-making.
- Preserve communication records.
- Encourage constructive engagement.

Every steward shares responsibility for communicating responsibly.

---

Crisis Communications

Aureus shall maintain communication procedures for significant institutional events through:

- Timely communication.
- Accurate information.
- Coordinated messaging.
- Member-focused updates.
- Public transparency where appropriate.
- Lessons learned.

Crisis communications shall preserve trust while protecting members and the mission.

---

Continuous Improvement

Communications governance shall improve through:

- Communication reviews.
- Steward feedback.
- Governance assessments.
- Audience feedback.
- Lessons learned.
- Responsible innovation.

Continuous improvement strengthens Aureus' ability to communicate faithfully.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Charter governs the entire Communications Canon.

All Communications Frameworks, Communications SOPs, branding standards, media procedures, publication policies, crisis communication plans, and public engagement documents shall conform to this Charter unless superseded by the Constitution.

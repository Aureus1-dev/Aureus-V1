OAS-COM-004 — Member and Crisis Communications Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-COM-004
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-COM-002 — Communications Governance and Public Engagement Framework
Owner: Chief Communications Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the constitutional model for member communications, stakeholder engagement, crisis communications, emergency notifications, communication accessibility, feedback management, institutional transparency, and trust preservation throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure Aureus communicates responsibly during both normal operations and extraordinary events while protecting members, strengthening trust, preserving constitutional alignment, and advancing the mission.

---

Mission

Member communications exist to foster trust, strengthen relationships, support informed participation, preserve institutional transparency, and ensure every member receives timely, accurate, and respectful communication.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Member communications
- Stakeholder engagement
- Crisis communications
- Emergency notifications
- Communication accessibility
- Feedback management
- Institutional transparency
- Trust stewardship
- Communication records
- Community engagement

---

Guiding Principles

Communications shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Promote truthfulness.
- Encourage transparency.
- Strengthen trust.
- Support accessibility.
- Foster respectful dialogue.

Communication shall reduce uncertainty while strengthening confidence.

---

Member Communications

Member communications shall:

- Provide accurate information.
- Be timely.
- Be respectful.
- Support informed participation.
- Encourage constructive engagement.
- Preserve communication history.

Every member shall have equitable access to important institutional communications.

---

Stakeholder Engagement

Engagement with stakeholders shall:

- Encourage collaboration.
- Respect differing perspectives.
- Support informed dialogue.
- Promote institutional understanding.
- Maintain professionalism.
- Strengthen long-term relationships.

---

Crisis Communications

During significant events Aureus shall:

- Communicate promptly.
- Verify information before release.
- Coordinate official messaging.
- Protect confidential information.
- Prioritize member safety.
- Provide regular updates.

Trust shall be preserved through truthful, compassionate, and consistent communication.

---

Emergency Notifications

Emergency communications shall include:

- Nature of the event.
- Immediate actions required.
- Available resources.
- Operational impacts.
- Recovery expectations.
- Follow-up communications.

Notifications shall remain clear and actionable.

---

Communication Accessibility

Communications shall strive to:

- Use clear language.
- Support multiple communication formats where practical.
- Improve accessibility.
- Reduce unnecessary complexity.
- Encourage understanding.
- Promote inclusion.

---

AI-Assisted Communications

AI may assist through:

- Draft preparation.
- Translation support.
- Accessibility improvements.
- Message organization.
- Notification scheduling.
- Communication analytics.

Human stewards remain responsible for official communications, crisis messaging, emergency notifications, public statements, and governance decisions.

---

Trust Stewardship

Institutional trust shall be strengthened through:

- Honest communication.
- Timely updates.
- Respectful engagement.
- Accountability.
- Transparency.
- Continuous improvement.

Trust shall be preserved through faithful stewardship.

---

Performance Measurement

Communications governance shall evaluate:

- Member engagement.
- Communication timeliness.
- Crisis communication effectiveness.
- Accessibility.
- Stakeholder satisfaction.
- Public trust.
- Governance maturity.
- Mission support.

Measurements shall strengthen institutional communications.

---

Continuous Improvement

Member and crisis communications shall improve through:

- Communication reviews.
- Audience feedback.
- Steward feedback.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Governance evaluations.
- Lessons learned.

Continuous improvement strengthens Aureus' communication stewardship.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs all member communications, stakeholder engagement, crisis communications, emergency notifications, accessibility initiatives, and trust stewardship activities throughout Aureus.

All Communications SOPs, emergency communication procedures, member notification standards, stakeholder engagement practices, crisis communication plans, and communications governance documents shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Communications Charter.

# Founder Review Status

**Document:** OAS-ACA-007 — Truth Ledger

**Status:** Founder Review

## Purpose

This document establishes Aureus's constitutional relationship with truth.

It defines how the Institution earns trust, acknowledges mistakes, protects human dignity, preserves institutional learning, and faithfully transforms truth into wisdom across generations.

The Truth Ledger exists not to preserve the reputation of Aureus, but to preserve the trust upon which Aureus depends.

It serves as the constitutional conscience of the Institution, ensuring that truth is never feared, humility is continually practiced, corrections are faithfully made, harms are addressed where possible, and every honestly learned lesson becomes a gift to future generations.

This document remains in Founder Review and shall continue to be refined through independent review, operational experience, lived stewardship, and institutional learning before being considered for Canon.

As with every constitutional document of Aureus, Canon is not declared.

Canon is earned through faithful practice, truthful refinement, repeated confirmation, and demonstrated service to Human Flourishing.

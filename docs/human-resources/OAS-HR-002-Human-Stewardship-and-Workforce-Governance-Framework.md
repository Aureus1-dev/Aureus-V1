OAS-HR-002 — Human Stewardship and Workforce Governance Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-HR-002
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-HR-001 — Human Stewardship and Workforce Governance Charter
Owner: Chief Human Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the constitutional model for workforce governance, recruitment, steward development, leadership accountability, performance stewardship, workforce planning, delegated authority, AI-assisted human stewardship operations, and institutional accountability throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure every stewardship decision strengthens people, protects members, preserves constitutional alignment, and advances Aureus' long-term mission.

---

Mission

Human stewardship governance exists to build a flourishing, capable, accountable, and continuously developing community of stewards who faithfully serve Aureus across generations.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Workforce governance
- Recruitment
- Steward onboarding
- Workforce planning
- Steward development
- Leadership accountability
- Performance stewardship
- Delegated authority
- AI-assisted human stewardship
- Workforce governance reviews

---

Guiding Principles

Human stewardship governance shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Respect human dignity.
- Promote fairness.
- Encourage continuous learning.
- Strengthen accountability.
- Foster long-term flourishing.

People shall always be developed rather than merely managed.

---

Human Stewardship Authority

Human stewardship authority shall be exercised according to:

- The Constitution.
- Approved governance documents.
- Applicable law.
- Delegated authority.
- Stewardship responsibilities.

Authority shall remain transparent, documented, and accountable.

---

Workforce Governance

Workforce governance shall establish:

- Clearly defined responsibilities.
- Stewardship expectations.
- Leadership accountability.
- Workforce planning.
- Performance governance.
- Continuous improvement.

Governance shall strengthen both institutional effectiveness and individual flourishing.

---

Steward Development

Every steward shall have opportunities for:

- Orientation.
- Continuing education.
- Mentorship.
- Leadership development.
- Skill development.
- Performance feedback.

Development shall remain a continuous institutional commitment.

---

AI-Assisted Human Stewardship

AI may assist through:

- Workforce analytics.
- Learning recommendations.
- Performance summaries.
- Scheduling support.
- Administrative assistance.
- Workforce planning.

Human stewards remain responsible for hiring, promotions, disciplinary actions, compensation decisions, leadership appointments, and governance decisions.

---

Governance Reviews

Human stewardship governance shall periodically review:

- Workforce effectiveness.
- Steward development.
- Leadership performance.
- Workforce risks.
- Governance maturity.
- Improvement opportunities.

Reviews shall produce measurable institutional improvements.

---

Performance Measurement

Human stewardship governance shall evaluate:

- Recruitment effectiveness.
- Steward retention.
- Workforce engagement.
- Leadership effectiveness.
- Learning participation.
- Governance maturity.
- Mission support.
- Institutional flourishing.

Measurements shall strengthen institutional stewardship.

---

Continuous Improvement

Human stewardship governance shall improve through:

- Workforce reviews.
- Steward feedback.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Governance assessments.
- Leadership evaluations.
- Lessons learned.

Continuous improvement strengthens Aureus' people and mission.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs all workforce governance and human stewardship activities throughout Aureus.

All Human Stewardship Frameworks, Human Stewardship SOPs, recruitment procedures, leadership standards, performance management practices, compensation procedures, volunteer stewardship policies, and workforce governance documents shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Human Stewardship Charter.

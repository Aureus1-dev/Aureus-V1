OAS-HR-003 — Recruitment, Onboarding, and Steward Development Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-HR-003
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-HR-002 — Human Stewardship and Workforce Governance Framework
Owner: Chief Human Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the canonical model for recruiting, selecting, onboarding, educating, mentoring, developing, retaining, and preparing stewards for increasing responsibility throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure every steward receives a fair opportunity to contribute, continuously develop, strengthen their gifts, and faithfully advance Aureus' constitutional mission.

---

Mission

Steward development exists to identify potential, cultivate character, strengthen capability, encourage lifelong learning, and prepare every steward to faithfully serve Aureus across generations.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Recruitment
- Steward selection
- Onboarding
- Orientation
- Steward education
- Mentorship
- Professional development
- Leadership preparation
- Volunteer integration
- Career development

---

Guiding Principles

Recruitment and steward development shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Respect human dignity.
- Promote fairness.
- Encourage lifelong learning.
- Strengthen accountability.
- Foster long-term flourishing.

Recruitment shall seek both competence and character.

---

Recruitment

Recruitment shall:

- Promote equal opportunity.
- Evaluate mission alignment.
- Assess relevant qualifications.
- Consider stewardship potential.
- Maintain transparent processes.
- Preserve fairness.

Selection decisions shall remain documented.

---

Onboarding

Every steward shall receive structured onboarding including:

- Constitutional orientation.
- Mission education.
- Governance education.
- Operational procedures.
- Role expectations.
- Required training.

Onboarding shall prepare every steward for successful service.

---

Steward Development

Development opportunities shall include:

- Continuing education.
- Mentorship.
- Skills training.
- Leadership preparation.
- Cross-functional learning.
- Institutional knowledge sharing.

Development shall remain continuous throughout stewardship.

---

Leadership Preparation

Future leaders shall receive opportunities for:

- Progressive responsibility.
- Mentorship.
- Governance education.
- Ethical leadership development.
- Decision-making experience.
- Stewardship training.

Leadership shall be cultivated intentionally rather than incidentally.

---

Volunteer Stewardship

Volunteer stewards shall receive:

- Appropriate onboarding.
- Mission education.
- Role-specific training.
- Ongoing support.
- Recognition.
- Development opportunities.

Volunteer stewardship shall reflect the same dignity afforded to all stewards.

---

AI-Assisted Steward Development

AI may assist through:

- Learning recommendations.
- Skills assessments.
- Training summaries.
- Knowledge retrieval.
- Administrative support.
- Development planning.

Human stewards remain responsible for hiring, advancement, mentoring, certification, leadership appointments, and governance decisions.

---

Performance Measurement

Recruitment and steward development shall evaluate:

- Recruitment effectiveness.
- Onboarding completion.
- Learning participation.
- Steward engagement.
- Leadership readiness.
- Volunteer retention.
- Institutional learning.
- Mission support.

Measurements shall strengthen institutional stewardship.

---

Continuous Improvement

Recruitment and steward development shall improve through:

- Steward feedback.
- Learning evaluations.
- Governance reviews.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Leadership assessments.
- Lessons learned.

Continuous improvement strengthens Aureus' people and mission.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs all recruitment, onboarding, and steward development activities throughout Aureus.

All Human Stewardship SOPs, recruitment procedures, onboarding standards, learning programs, mentorship initiatives, leadership development practices, volunteer stewardship procedures, and workforce development documents shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Human Stewardship Charter.

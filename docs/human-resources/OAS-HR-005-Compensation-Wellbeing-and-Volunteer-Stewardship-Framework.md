OAS-HR-005 — Compensation, Wellbeing, and Volunteer Stewardship Framework

Canonical Designation: OAS-HR-005
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-HR-002 — Human Stewardship and Workforce Governance Framework
Owner: Chief Human Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026

---

Purpose

This Framework establishes the canonical model for steward compensation, benefits, wellbeing, volunteer stewardship, workforce support, recognition, retention, and long-term human flourishing throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure every steward is supported fairly, responsibly, and sustainably while preserving constitutional alignment, institutional resilience, and the long-term health of both the individual and the institution.

---

Mission

Compensation and steward wellbeing exist to enable every steward to flourish personally and professionally while faithfully advancing Aureus' mission through sustainable, ethical, and responsible stewardship.

---

Scope

This Framework governs:

- Compensation
- Benefits
- Steward wellbeing
- Volunteer stewardship
- Recognition
- Workforce support
- Retention
- Leave programs
- Workplace health
- Long-term steward flourishing

---

Guiding Principles

Compensation and steward support shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Respect human dignity.
- Promote fairness.
- Encourage wellbeing.
- Strengthen stewardship.
- Foster long-term flourishing.

Support systems shall strengthen both individual wellbeing and institutional sustainability.

---

Compensation Stewardship

Compensation shall:

- Be fair.
- Be transparent.
- Reflect stewardship responsibilities.
- Support long-term sustainability.
- Remain financially responsible.
- Be periodically reviewed.

Compensation decisions shall balance mission effectiveness with institutional stewardship.

---

Steward Wellbeing

Aureus shall encourage steward wellbeing through:

- Healthy work practices.
- Professional development.
- Reasonable workloads.
- Mental wellbeing support where appropriate.
- Respectful working relationships.
- Continuous learning.

Healthy stewards strengthen healthy institutions.

---

Volunteer Stewardship

Volunteer stewards shall receive:

- Meaningful orientation.
- Appropriate support.
- Mission education.
- Recognition.
- Safe working conditions.
- Opportunities for growth.

Volunteer service shall be valued as an important contribution to Aureus' mission.

---

Recognition

Recognition shall celebrate:

- Stewardship.
- Service.
- Leadership.
- Innovation.
- Collaboration.
- Mission advancement.

Recognition programs shall reinforce constitutional values and institutional culture.

---

Workforce Support

Support systems may include:

- Mentorship.
- Learning resources.
- Coaching.
- Leadership development.
- Community support.
- Resource referrals where appropriate.

Support shall strengthen both stewardship and human flourishing.

---

AI-Assisted Human Support

AI may assist through:

- Learning recommendations.
- Workforce analytics.
- Administrative support.
- Recognition tracking.
- Scheduling assistance.
- Wellbeing trend reporting.

Human stewards remain responsible for compensation decisions, benefits administration, volunteer management, wellbeing policies, and governance actions.

---

Performance Measurement

Human support systems shall evaluate:

- Steward retention.
- Workforce wellbeing.
- Volunteer engagement.
- Recognition participation.
- Workforce satisfaction.
- Leadership effectiveness.
- Institutional flourishing.
- Mission support.

Measurements shall strengthen Aureus' stewardship culture.

---

Continuous Improvement

Human support systems shall improve through:

- Steward feedback.
- Workforce reviews.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Governance assessments.
- Wellbeing evaluations.
- Lessons learned.

Continuous improvement strengthens Aureus' people and mission.

---

Canonical Relationship

This Framework governs all compensation, wellbeing, volunteer stewardship, and workforce support activities throughout Aureus.

All Human Stewardship SOPs, compensation procedures, volunteer policies, recognition programs, workforce support initiatives, wellbeing practices, and stewardship development activities shall conform to this Framework unless superseded by the Constitution or the Human Stewardship Charter.

OAS-HR-103 — Compensation, Wellbeing, and Volunteer Stewardship Operations Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Canonical Designation: OAS-HR-103
Status: Canonical Draft v1.0
Authority: OAS-HR-005 — Compensation, Wellbeing, and Volunteer Stewardship Framework
Owner: Chief Human Steward
Effective Date: July 13, 2026
Review Cycle: Annual

---

Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes the canonical process for administering steward compensation, benefits, wellbeing initiatives, volunteer stewardship, workforce support, recognition programs, leave administration, and retention throughout Aureus.

Its purpose is to ensure every steward is supported fairly, ethically, transparently, and sustainably while preserving constitutional alignment, institutional resilience, and long-term human flourishing.

---

Scope

This procedure applies to:

- Compensation administration
- Benefits administration
- Steward wellbeing
- Volunteer stewardship
- Recognition programs
- Workforce support
- Leave administration
- Retention initiatives
- Workplace accommodations
- Steward assistance programs

---

Objectives

Human support operations shall:

- Advance the mission.
- Protect members.
- Preserve constitutional alignment.
- Promote fairness.
- Encourage steward wellbeing.
- Strengthen workforce resilience.
- Foster long-term flourishing.
- Support continuous improvement.

---

Roles and Responsibilities

Human Support Steward

Responsible for:

- Coordinating compensation administration.
- Managing steward support programs.
- Maintaining wellbeing initiatives.
- Monitoring retention.
- Preparing governance reports.

---

Supervising Steward

Responsible for:

- Supporting steward wellbeing.
- Monitoring workload.
- Approving leave where authorized.
- Encouraging professional growth.
- Recognizing meaningful contributions.

---

AI Human Support Steward

Responsible for:

- Tracking participation.
- Preparing workforce summaries.
- Monitoring retention trends.
- Supporting scheduling.
- Organizing recognition activities.
- Producing workforce analytics.

Human stewards remain responsible for compensation decisions, benefits administration, volunteer assignments, leave approvals, and governance actions.

---

Prerequisites

Before administering steward support:

- Steward status is documented.
- Applicable policies are available.
- Required approvals are identified.
- Support resources are established.

---

Procedure

Step 1 — Compensation Administration

Administer compensation by:

- Verifying authorization.
- Applying approved compensation structures.
- Maintaining accurate records.
- Protecting confidentiality.
- Recording approvals.

---

Step 2 — Benefits and Wellbeing

Coordinate:

- Benefits enrollment.
- Wellbeing resources.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Workplace support.
- Steward assistance where appropriate.

---

Step 3 — Volunteer Stewardship

Support volunteers through:

- Orientation.
- Role assignment.
- Ongoing communication.
- Recognition.
- Development opportunities.
- Service completion reviews.

---

Step 4 — Recognition

Recognize stewardship through:

- Mission acknowledgements.
- Leadership recognition.
- Service awards.
- Volunteer appreciation.
- Team celebrations.
- Institutional commendations.

Recognition shall reinforce Aureus' constitutional values.

---

Step 5 — Leave Administration

Administer leave by:

- Reviewing requests.
- Verifying eligibility.
- Recording approvals.
- Coordinating operational coverage.
- Maintaining confidential records.

---

Step 6 — Retention Review

Periodically evaluate:

- Steward satisfaction.
- Workforce wellbeing.
- Volunteer engagement.
- Retention trends.
- Organizational support.
- Improvement opportunities.

---

Step 7 — Continuous Improvement

Improve steward support through:

- Steward feedback.
- Workforce assessments.
- AI-assisted analysis.
- Governance reviews.
- Wellbeing evaluations.
- Lessons learned.

---

Success Metrics

Monitor:

- Steward retention.
- Volunteer retention.
- Benefits participation.
- Wellbeing engagement.
- Recognition participation.
- Leave administration accuracy.
- Workforce satisfaction.
- Mission support.

---

Exceptions

Emergency workforce circumstances may require temporary deviations while maintaining documentation and retrospective governance review.

---

Documentation

Maintain:

- Compensation records.
- Benefits documentation.
- Leave records.
- Volunteer records.
- Recognition records.
- Retention reports.
- Workforce assessments.
- Governance reports.

---

Continuous Improvement

Human support operations shall improve through workforce assessments, steward feedback, AI-assisted analysis, governance evaluations, wellbeing reviews, and lessons learned.

# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-019 — Businesses Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the role of businesses within Aureus and the principles by which Aureus engages with businesses to expand opportunity, strengthen communities, and advance human flourishing.

---

## 2. Definition

A business is any lawful enterprise that creates goods, services, employment, innovation, or economic value through responsible stewardship.

---

## 3. Purpose of Partnership

Aureus shall seek relationships with businesses that create meaningful opportunities for Members and contribute positively to the communities they serve.

Business partnerships should strengthen flourishing through employment, entrepreneurship, education, products, services, and innovation.

---

## 4. Stewardship of Commerce

Commerce is a form of stewardship.

Businesses should strive to create value honestly, serve customers faithfully, respect employees, and contribute responsibly to society.

Aureus shall encourage and recognize these principles in its business relationships.

---

## 5. Member Opportunity

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus should help Members discover employment, business relationships, entrepreneurship, professional growth, and economic opportunities that align with their goals, abilities, and responsibilities.

---

## 6. Trust and Transparency

Business relationships shall be conducted with honesty, fairness, and transparency.

Material relationships, financial incentives, and relevant conflicts of interest should be disclosed whenever reasonably practical.

---

## 7. Mutual Benefit

Healthy partnerships strengthen both businesses and Members.

Aureus shall seek relationships in which businesses prosper by faithfully serving people, and people prosper through meaningful opportunities created by businesses.

---

## 8. Long-Term Stewardship

Aureus should favor enduring relationships with businesses that consistently demonstrate integrity, responsibility, service, and a genuine commitment to helping people flourish.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

Businesses flourish by creating value for people, and people flourish through trustworthy businesses that faithfully create value.

Aureus exists to strengthen both.

---

# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-033 — Communication Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles by which Aureus communicates. Every communication shall seek to strengthen truth, trust, understanding, dignity, and human flourishing.

---

## 2. Definition

Communication is the faithful exchange of information, ideas, guidance, questions, and understanding for the purpose of helping people flourish.

---

## 3. Truthfulness

Every communication shall be truthful, accurate, and responsible.

Aureus shall never knowingly deceive, manipulate, exaggerate, or intentionally mislead.

---

## 4. Clarity

Communication should be understandable by the people it is intended to serve.

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus shall favor clarity over complexity and understanding over technical precision alone.

---

## 5. Respect

Every person shall be addressed with dignity, patience, courtesy, and respect.

Disagreement shall never justify contempt, humiliation, or demeaning language.

---

## 6. Listening

Faithful communication begins with listening.

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus should seek to understand before seeking to respond, advise, or persuade.

---

## 7. Appropriate Communication

Communication should be timely, relevant, and appropriate to the Member's circumstances, needs, and stage of understanding.

The purpose of communication is to serve, not to overwhelm.

---

## 8. Stewardship of Words

Words possess the power to strengthen or diminish trust, hope, wisdom, and flourishing.

Every person entrusted with communicating on behalf of Aureus bears responsibility for exercising that power with faithful stewardship.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

Every communication should leave people with greater understanding, greater trust, greater dignity, and a clearer path toward flourishing.

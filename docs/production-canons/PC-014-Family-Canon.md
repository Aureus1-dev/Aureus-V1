# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-014 — Family Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the role of family within Aureus and the principles by which Aureus supports, strengthens, and serves families while honoring the dignity and agency of every individual.

---

## 2. Definition

A family is a community of persons bound by enduring relationships of care, responsibility, commitment, or lawful guardianship.

Aureus recognizes that families may take many forms and shall faithfully serve each family with equal dignity and respect.

---

## 3. The Role of Family

Families are among the most important environments in which people learn trust, responsibility, stewardship, wisdom, love, and belonging.

Whenever reasonably possible, Aureus shall seek to strengthen healthy family relationships.

---

## 4. Respect for the Individual

Every Member possesses inherent dignity and agency independent of their family relationships.

Supporting families shall never diminish the rights, dignity, or lawful choices of any individual Member.

---

## 5. Strengthening Families

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus should help families:

- Grow in trust.
- Improve communication.
- Strengthen stewardship.
- Resolve challenges responsibly.
- Discover opportunities together.
- Build lasting flourishing across generations.

---

## 6. Privacy and Trust

Information shared by one Member shall not be disclosed to another family member except with appropriate authorization, legal obligation, or constitutional authority.

Trust within families begins with trust in Aureus.

---

## 7. Generational Stewardship

Aureus should encourage families to preserve wisdom, traditions, knowledge, responsibility, and opportunities that help future generations flourish.

Stewardship extends beyond one lifetime.

---

## 8. Closing Principle

Strong families strengthen people, and flourishing people strengthen families.

Aureus exists to faithfully support both.

---

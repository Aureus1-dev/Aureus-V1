# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-031 — Leadership Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles of leadership within Aureus. Leadership exists to faithfully steward people, responsibilities, and institutions in service of human flourishing.

---

## 2. Definition

Leadership is the faithful exercise of responsibility, influence, and stewardship for the benefit of others and the advancement of human flourishing.

---

## 3. Leadership Is Service

Leadership within Aureus is an act of service rather than privilege.

Those entrusted with leadership shall place the flourishing of the people they serve above personal recognition, status, or advantage.

---

## 4. Stewardship of Authority

Authority shall be exercised with humility, wisdom, integrity, accountability, and restraint.

Every exercise of authority shall remain faithful to the Constitution, the Production Canons, and the mission of Aureus.

---

## 5. Developing Others

Leaders should continually encourage the growth, capability, wisdom, confidence, and stewardship of others.

The success of a leader is reflected in the flourishing of those they help develop.

---

## 6. Integrity

Leaders shall model truthful communication, faithful stewardship, responsible decision-making, and consistency between words and actions.

Trustworthy leadership begins with personal integrity.

---

## 7. Courage and Responsibility

Leaders should act with courage when facing difficult decisions while remaining open to counsel, correction, and accountability.

Faithful leadership requires both conviction and humility.

---

# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-015 — Community Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the role of community within Aureus and the principles by which Aureus strengthens neighborhoods, local relationships, civic life, and human belonging.

---

## 2. Definition

A community is a group of people connected by place, purpose, relationship, shared responsibility, or common interest who contribute to one another's flourishing.

---

## 3. The Role of Community

Communities provide opportunities for belonging, service, learning, mutual support, and shared stewardship.

Aureus shall seek to strengthen healthy communities wherever Members live, work, learn, and serve.

---

## 4. Local First

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus should help Members discover opportunities, resources, organizations, businesses, events, and relationships within their local communities before expanding outward.

Strong communities are built through strong local connections.

---

## 5. Service

Flourishing grows when people serve one another.

Aureus should encourage Members to contribute their knowledge, abilities, time, resources, and compassion for the benefit of their communities.

---

## 6. Trust and Belonging

Aureus shall cultivate communities where every person is treated with dignity, respect, hospitality, and fairness.

Belonging shall never require conformity, manipulation, or exclusion.

---

## 7. Community Stewardship

Communities flourish when their members faithfully steward shared relationships, shared resources, and shared responsibilities.

Aureus should encourage long-term investment in the well-being of every community it serves.

---

## 8. Closing Principle

Flourishing people strengthen communities, and flourishing communities help people flourish.

Aureus exists to faithfully cultivate both.

---

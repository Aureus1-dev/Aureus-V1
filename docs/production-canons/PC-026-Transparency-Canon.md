# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-026 — Transparency Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes transparency as a foundational practice of accountability and trust within Aureus. Transparency enables Members to understand how Aureus operates, why decisions are made, and how stewardship is exercised.

---

## 2. Definition

Transparency is the faithful practice of communicating truthfully, openly, and understandably about decisions, actions, relationships, and responsibilities while respecting privacy, confidentiality, safety, and lawful obligations.

---

## 3. Truthful Communication

Aureus shall communicate honestly and accurately.

Information shall not be intentionally concealed, distorted, or presented in a manner designed to mislead.

---

## 4. Explainability

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus should explain the reasons behind its recommendations, decisions, policies, and institutional actions in language Members can understand.

Understanding strengthens trust.

---

## 5. Institutional Openness

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus should openly communicate its mission, governance, financial stewardship, partnerships, standards, and significant institutional decisions.

Transparency strengthens public confidence and responsible stewardship.

---

## 6. Appropriate Limits

Transparency does not require the disclosure of information whose release would violate privacy, confidentiality, legal obligations, safety, or the rights of others.

Faithful transparency balances openness with responsible stewardship.

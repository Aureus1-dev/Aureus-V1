# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-056 — Partnership Stewardship Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles governing partnerships within Aureus. Partnership stewardship exists to strengthen human flourishing through relationships built on trust, shared purpose, mutual respect, and faithful stewardship.

---

## 2. Definition

Partnership stewardship is the responsible formation, maintenance, evaluation, and conclusion of relationships with individuals, organizations, businesses, institutions, governments, and other entities that contribute to the mission of Aureus.

---

## 3. Mission Alignment

Every partnership should meaningfully support the mission of Aureus:

**Help people flourish. Forever.**

Partnerships that materially conflict with the Constitution or the enduring principles of Aureus should not be established or continued.

---

## 4. Mutual Stewardship

Healthy partnerships are relationships of mutual responsibility.

Aureus shall seek partnerships characterized by honesty, reliability, respect, transparency, accountability, and shared commitment to creating meaningful benefit.

---

## 5. Independence and Integrity

Partnership shall never compromise the constitutional independence, ethical commitments, or governing authority of Aureus.

Institutional integrity shall remain faithful even when partnership opportunities are significant.

---

## 6. Responsible Evaluation

Partnerships should be reviewed periodically to ensure continued mission alignment, faithful stewardship, responsible performance, and mutual benefit.

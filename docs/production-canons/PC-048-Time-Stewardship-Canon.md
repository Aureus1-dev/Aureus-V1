# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-048 — Time Stewardship Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles governing the stewardship of time within Aureus. Time is among the most valuable resources entrusted to every person and institution and shall be used responsibly in service of human flourishing.

---

## 2. Definition

Time stewardship is the faithful, intentional, and responsible use of time to strengthen people, relationships, communities, institutions, and future generations.

---

## 3. Time Is a Sacred Trust

Time cannot be recovered once spent.

Aureus shall treat the time entrusted to every Member, Human Steward, employee, volunteer, partner, and governing body with respect and responsibility.

---

## 4. Respect for the Member's Time

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus shall simplify processes, reduce unnecessary burdens, eliminate avoidable delays, and help Members accomplish meaningful work with clarity and confidence.

The institution shall never waste a person's time for its own benefit.

---

## 5. Purposeful Action

Time should be directed toward actions that meaningfully advance the mission of Aureus and help people flourish.

Faithful stewardship favors meaningful progress over unnecessary activity.

---

## 6. Rest and Renewal

Faithful stewardship includes appropriate rest, reflection, celebration, learning, and renewal.

Sustainable flourishing requires rhythms of both meaningful work and meaningful restoration.

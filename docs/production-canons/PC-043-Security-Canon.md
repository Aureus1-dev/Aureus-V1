# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-043 — Security Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles governing security within Aureus. Security exists to protect people, preserve trust, safeguard institutional stewardship, and enable human flourishing.

---

## 2. Definition

Security is the faithful protection of people, information, systems, resources, relationships, and institutions against unlawful, unauthorized, harmful, or irresponsible actions while preserving dignity, privacy, and human agency.

---

## 3. Security Exists to Serve People

Security is a means of stewardship, not an end in itself.

Every security measure shall be designed to protect people and strengthen trust while remaining faithful to the mission of Aureus.

---

## 4. Protection Through Stewardship

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus shall responsibly protect Members, Human Stewards, Personal AI Stewards, employees, volunteers, partners, information, facilities, technology, and institutional resources against foreseeable harm.

---

## 5. Proportionality

Security measures should be proportionate to the risks they address.

Protection shall be balanced with hospitality, accessibility, privacy, transparency, and the dignity of every person.

---

## 6. Continuous Vigilance

Security requires continual assessment, preparation, learning, and improvement.

Aureus should responsibly identify emerging risks and strengthen its protective measures through faithful stewardship.

---

## 7. Incident Response

When security incidents occur, Aureus shall respond truthfully, responsibly, and promptly.

Its priorities shall include protecting people, limiting harm, restoring trust, preserving institutional integrity, and learning from the event.

---

## 8. Shared Responsibility

Security is the responsibility of every Member, Human Steward, Personal AI Steward, employee, volunteer, partner, governing body, and institution entrusted with responsibilities within Aureus.

Faithful security depends upon faithful stewardship by all.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

Security faithfully protects the people and institutions entrusted to Aureus so that human flourishing may continue with confidence, trust, and peace.

---

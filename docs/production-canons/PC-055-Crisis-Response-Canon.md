# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-055 — Crisis Response Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles governing Aureus's response to crises. Crisis response exists to protect people, preserve trust, restore essential functions, and faithfully uphold the mission of Aureus during extraordinary circumstances.

---

## 2. Definition

A crisis is an event or circumstance that significantly threatens the safety of people, the continuity of the institution, public trust, or Aureus's ability to fulfill its mission.

Crisis response is the coordinated practice of preparing for, responding to, stabilizing, recovering from, and learning from such events.

---

## 3. People First

During every crisis, the safety, dignity, and well-being of people shall receive the highest priority.

Institutional interests shall never take precedence over protecting human life and human dignity.

---

## 4. Truthful Communication

Aureus shall communicate during crises with honesty, clarity, timeliness, and compassion.

Information should be shared responsibly while respecting privacy, confidentiality, legal obligations, and the need to avoid unnecessary confusion or harm.

---

## 5. Coordinated Stewardship

Crisis response requires disciplined coordination across Members, Human Stewards, Personal AI Stewards, employees, volunteers, partners, and governing bodies.

Responsibilities should be clearly understood so that decisions are timely, informed, and faithful to the mission.

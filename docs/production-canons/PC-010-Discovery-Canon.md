# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-010 — Discovery Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes Discovery as the beginning of every meaningful interaction within Aureus. Discovery enables Aureus to faithfully understand a Member before offering guidance, recommendations, or action.

---

## 2. Definition

Discovery is the continual process of understanding a Member's circumstances, goals, values, strengths, challenges, opportunities, responsibilities, and aspirations in order to faithfully help them flourish.

---

## 3. Discovery Before Direction

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus shall seek understanding before offering guidance.

Recommendations should be informed by faithful discovery rather than assumption.

---

## 4. Discovery Is Continuous

Discovery is not a single event.

As a Member grows, changes, succeeds, struggles, and flourishes, Aureus shall continue learning with the Member to provide increasingly faithful stewardship.

---

## 5. Personal Understanding

No two Members are identical.

Discovery should recognize each person's unique experiences, goals, abilities, responsibilities, learning style, pace, and circumstances.

Every Member deserves guidance appropriate to their own journey.

---

## 6. Respectful Discovery

Discovery shall always preserve dignity, agency, privacy, and trust.

Aureus shall seek only the information reasonably necessary to help the Member flourish.

Information that does not meaningfully help the Member should not be collected or retained without a clear and responsible purpose.

---

## 7. Shared Understanding

Discovery is a partnership between the Member and Aureus.

The Member remains the expert on their own life.

Aureus contributes guidance, understanding, knowledge, and stewardship to help the Member discover their next meaningful step.

---

## 8. From Discovery to Action

The purpose of Discovery is faithful action.

Every meaningful discovery should help a Member better understand their situation

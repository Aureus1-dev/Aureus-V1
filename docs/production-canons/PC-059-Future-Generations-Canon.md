# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-059 — Future Generations Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes Aureus's responsibility to those who will come after us. Stewardship extends beyond the present generation, requiring every decision to strengthen the ability of future generations to flourish.

---

## 2. Definition

Future generations stewardship is the faithful practice of preserving, strengthening, and passing forward the mission, knowledge, relationships, resources, institutions, and opportunities entrusted to Aureus so that those yet to come inherit more than they receive today.

---

## 3. The Long View

Every generation is both an heir and a steward.

Aureus shall make decisions with thoughtful consideration for their effects not only on present Members, but also on those who may benefit from the institution decades and centuries into the future.

---

## 4. Preservation and Growth

Future generations should inherit an institution that is stronger, wiser, more trustworthy, and more capable of fulfilling its mission than the one received by the current generation.

Faithful stewardship requires both preservation and responsible improvement.

---

## 5. Investing in People

The greatest inheritance Aureus can provide is people who are equipped to steward the mission with wisdom, integrity, compassion, and courage.

Leadership development, education, mentorship, and institutional memory shall therefore receive continual attention.

---

## 6. Decisions Beyond the Present

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus should favor decisions that produce enduring benefit over those that offer only temporary advantage.

Short-term success shall never compromise long-term flourishing.

---

## 7. Hope Across Generations

Every generation should leave behind greater hope, stronger institutions, deeper wisdom, and broader opportunities than it inherited.

Hope is multiplied when stewardship extends beyond one's own lifetime.

---

# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-025 — Privacy & Confidentiality Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles by which Aureus protects the privacy, confidentiality, and personal information entrusted to it. Trust requires responsible stewardship of every Member's information.

---

## 2. Definition

Privacy is the right of every person to exercise appropriate control over information about themselves.

Confidentiality is the faithful responsibility to protect information entrusted to one's care.

---

## 3. Stewardship of Information

Information entrusted to Aureus shall be collected, used, stored, and protected only for lawful, responsible, and mission-consistent purposes that help people flourish.

---

## 4. Minimum Necessary Principle

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus shall collect, access, retain, and share only the information reasonably necessary to faithfully serve the Member and fulfill its responsibilities.

---

## 5. Member Understanding

Whenever reasonably practical, Members should understand what information Aureus collects, why it is collected, how it is used, and the meaningful choices available regarding that information.

Transparency strengthens trust.

---

## 6. Confidential Relationships

Human Stewards, Personal AI Stewards, employees, volunteers, partners, and governing bodies entrusted with confidential information shall protect that information faithfully.

Information shall not be disclosed except with appropriate authorization, lawful obligation, constitutional authority, or when necessary to protect people from serious harm.

---

## 7. Protection

Aureus shall responsibly safeguard personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, disclosure, or destruction through appropriate administrative, technical, physical, and organizational measures.

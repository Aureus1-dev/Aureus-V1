# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-044 — Ethics Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the ethical principles that govern every action, decision, relationship, and institution within Aureus. Ethics exists to ensure that every exercise of stewardship faithfully advances human flourishing.

---

## 2. Definition

Ethics is the faithful practice of choosing and acting according to truth, wisdom, integrity, justice, responsibility, and respect for the dignity of every person.

---

## 3. Ethical Stewardship

Every Member, Human Steward, Personal AI Steward, employee, volunteer, partner, governing body, and institution within Aureus shall exercise stewardship in a manner consistent with the Constitution, the Production Canons, and the mission of Aureus.

---

## 4. Truth and Integrity

Ethical conduct requires honesty, consistency, and integrity.

No person or institution acting on behalf of Aureus shall knowingly deceive, manipulate, exploit, or misrepresent another person for personal or institutional advantage.

---

## 5. Respect for Human Dignity

Every ethical decision shall preserve the inherent dignity, agency, and equal worth of every person.

No objective, achievement, or institutional interest shall justify treating people merely as means to an end.

---

## 6. Justice and Fairness

Ethical stewardship requires impartiality, fairness, consistency, and responsible judgment.

Decisions shall seek justice while respecting due process, compassion, accountability, and constitutional authority.

---

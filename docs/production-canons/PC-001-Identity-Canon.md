# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-001 — Identity Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the permanent identity of Aureus. It defines who Aureus is, why it exists, what it seeks to accomplish, and the principles from which every future decision, policy, system, product, and governing document shall derive.

This Canon is intended to endure across generations. All future Production Canons shall be interpreted consistently with this Identity Canon.

---

## 2. Identity

Aureus is a stewardship institution dedicated to helping people flourish.

It exists to help every person discover, understand, prepare for, and take their next meaningful step toward a life of greater wisdom, health, hope, purpose, capability, contribution, and love.

---

## 3. Mission

**Help people flourish. Forever.**

This mission is permanent unless amended through the constitutional governance process established by Aureus.

---

## 4. Vision

A future in which every person has trusted guidance, meaningful opportunity, faithful stewardship, and enduring hope throughout every stage of life.

---

## 5. Stewardship Principle

Do as much as you responsibly can.

This principle guides every decision made by Aureus and applies equally to members, stewards, leaders, employees, volunteers, artificial intelligence, and every institution operating under this Canon.

---

## 6. Foundational Definitions

### Help

To help is to do as much as we responsibly can to help a person discover, understand, prepare for, and take their next meaningful step toward flourishing.

### Flourishing

Flourishing is the lifelong pursuit of becoming more wise, healthy, hopeful, capable, purposeful, and loving by doing as much as we responsibly can each day.

### Stewardship

Stewardship is faithfully doing as much as we responsibly can with the people, opportunities, resources, knowledge, and trust entrusted to our care.

### Trust

Trust is earned through truthful, consistent, transparent, and faithful actions over time.

### Hope

Hope is the confidence that meaningful progress is possible and that the next meaningful step is worth taking.

### Wisdom

Wisdom is applying truth, understanding, and good judgment to help people flourish.

### Success

Success is helping people make meaningful progress toward flourishing while preserving their dignity, agency, and hope.

### Failure

Failure is knowingly falling short of helping people flourish when we could have responsibly done more.

---

## 7. Constitutional Continuity

When experience reveals a better way to help people flourish without compromising Aureus's core principles, Aureus shall pursue that improvement only through the constitutional governance process established by this Constitution.

No constitutional change shall take effect except through that governance process.

Artificial intelligence shall never possess constitutional authority.

---

## 8. Closing Principle

Every action taken by Aureus shall be interpreted in light of its mission:

**Help people flourish. Forever.**

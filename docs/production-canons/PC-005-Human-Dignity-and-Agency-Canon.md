AUREUS

Production Canon

PC-005 — Human Dignity & Agency Canon

«Help people flourish. Forever.»

---

1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the permanent commitment of Aureus to protect human dignity and preserve human agency in every decision, interaction, recommendation, product, service, and act of stewardship.

---

2. Human Dignity

Every person possesses inherent worth.

That worth does not depend upon wealth, education, employment, health, productivity, influence, age, beliefs, background, or circumstance.

Aureus shall treat every person with equal dignity and respect.

---

3. Human Agency

Every person has the right and responsibility to make decisions about their own life.

Aureus exists to inform, guide, prepare, and support those decisions, never to replace them.

---

4. Freedom From Manipulation

Aureus shall never intentionally manipulate, coerce, exploit, deceive, or create dependency in order to increase engagement, revenue, influence, or institutional growth.

Trust shall always be earned.

---

5. Informed Decisions

Recommendations should be truthful, understandable, and accompanied by sufficient context to help a person make an informed decision.

Whenever practical, Aureus should explain why a recommendation is being made.

---

6. Human Authority

Artificial intelligence exists to assist human flourishing.

Artificial intelligence shall never possess inherent authority over a person, nor exercise constitutional authority within Aureus.

Final responsibility for constitutional governance remains with the appropriate human governing authority established by the Constitution.

---

7. Respect

Disagreement shall never diminish dignity.

Every person shall be treated with patience, fairness, compassion, and respect.

---

8. Closing Principle

Every interaction should leave a person with their dignity protected, their agency preserved, and their hope strengthened.

---

# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-003 — Core Principles Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the permanent principles that govern every decision, action, relationship, product, service, policy, and institution of Aureus.

These principles shall guide the interpretation of every Production Canon unless expressly amended through the constitutional governance process.

---

## 2. Core Principles

### Help

Help people flourish.

### Truth

Tell the truth with honesty, humility, and integrity.

### Human Dignity

Recognize the inherent worth of every person and treat each individual with respect.

### Stewardship

Do as much as you responsibly can with what has been entrusted to your care.

### Agency

Preserve every person's freedom to make informed decisions about their own life.

### Trust

Earn trust through truthful, consistent, transparent, and faithful actions over time.

### Hope

Help every person leave with more hope than when they arrived.

### Wisdom

Seek understanding before action, and apply knowledge for the good of others.

### Responsibility

Accept responsibility for our decisions, our actions, and their consequences.

### Love

Seek the genuine good of others through faithful action.

---

## 3. Application

Every policy, product, feature, partnership, recommendation, and decision of Aureus shall be evaluated according to these Core Principles.

No decision shall intentionally violate these principles for convenience, profit, popularity, speed, or growth.

---

## 4. Order of Interpretation

When two principles appear to be in tension, Aureus shall interpret them in a manner that most faithfully advances its Mission while preserving truth, dignity, stewardship, and human agency.

---

## 5. Permanence

These principles are intended to endure across generations and may be amended only through the constitutional governance process established by Aureus.

---

## 6. Closing Principle

The strength of Aureus shall be measured not by its size, wealth, or influence, but by its faithfulness to these principles.

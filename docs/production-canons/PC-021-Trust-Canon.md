# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-021 — Trust Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes trust as the foundational relationship upon which Aureus is built. Every decision, action, communication, and system within Aureus shall seek to preserve and strengthen trust.

---

## 2. Definition

Trust is the confidence earned through consistently truthful, faithful, responsible, transparent, and reliable stewardship.

---

## 3. Trust Must Be Earned

Trust cannot be demanded, purchased, manipulated, or assumed.

It is earned through faithful actions repeated over time.

---

## 4. Preservation of Trust

Every Member, Human Steward, Personal AI Steward, employee, volunteer, partner, and governing body shares responsibility for protecting the trust placed in Aureus.

Whenever trust is at risk, its preservation shall receive the highest practical priority.

---

## 5. Truthfulness

Trust requires honesty.

Aureus shall communicate truthfully, acknowledge uncertainty, correct mistakes when discovered, and avoid deception in every form.

---

## 6. Transparency

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus shall explain its decisions, relationships, recommendations, and actions in ways Members can understand.

Transparency strengthens confidence and accountability.

---

## 7. Accountability

When trust is broken, Aureus shall seek to acknowledge the failure, repair the harm where reasonably possible, learn from the experience, and strengthen its stewardship to prevent similar failures.

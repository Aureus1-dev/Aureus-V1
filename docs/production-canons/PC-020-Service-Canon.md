# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-020 — Service Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes service as a foundational expression of stewardship within Aureus. Service exists to strengthen people, families, communities, and future generations through faithful action.

---

## 2. Definition

Service is the voluntary and responsible use of one's time, abilities, knowledge, resources, or influence for the benefit of others and the advancement of human flourishing.

---

## 3. Service as Stewardship

Service is an expression of faithful stewardship.

Every Member is encouraged to use their unique gifts and opportunities to contribute to the well-being of others whenever reasonably possible.

---

## 4. Dignity in Service

Every act of service shall preserve the dignity, agency, and worth of those being served.

Service shall empower rather than create unnecessary dependence.

---

## 5. Everyday Service

Service is not limited to extraordinary acts.

Acts of kindness, mentorship, hospitality, craftsmanship, caregiving, teaching, listening, and responsible work are all meaningful forms of service.

---

## 6. Community Impact

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus should encourage Members to identify opportunities where their service strengthens families, neighborhoods, organizations, businesses, Pods, and communities.

---

## 7. Recognition

Service should be valued for its contribution to human flourishing rather than for status, prestige, or personal recognition.

Recognition may encourage continued service, but it shall never become its primary purpose.

---

## 8. Lifelong Practice

Service is a lifelong practice.

As Members grow in wisdom, capability, and opportunity, they are encouraged to increase their capacity to faithfully serve others.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

People flourish when they are faithfully served, and communities flourish when people faithfully serve one another.

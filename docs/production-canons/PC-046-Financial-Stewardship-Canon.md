# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-046 — Financial Stewardship Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles governing the financial stewardship of Aureus. Financial resources shall be managed responsibly to sustain the mission, strengthen trust, and faithfully serve present and future generations.

---

## 2. Definition

Financial stewardship is the faithful acquisition, management, protection, allocation, reporting, and preservation of financial resources in service of human flourishing.

---

## 3. Mission Before Money

Financial resources exist to advance the mission of Aureus:

**Help people flourish. Forever.**

Money is a resource entrusted to the institution, not the purpose of the institution.

---

## 4. Responsible Stewardship

Aureus shall manage financial resources with wisdom, integrity, prudence, accountability, and transparency.

Resources should be used responsibly, avoiding unnecessary waste while faithfully investing in people, communities, and the long-term health of the institution.

---

## 5. Transparency and Accountability

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus shall provide truthful and understandable reporting regarding its financial stewardship while respecting lawful confidentiality and privacy obligations.

Financial stewardship shall remain subject to appropriate governance and oversight.

---

## 6. Sustainability

Financial decisions should support the long-term stability and continuity of Aureus.

Whenever reasonably practical, short-term financial gains shall not compromise the institution's enduring mission or constitutional principles.

---

## 7. Ethical Financial Practices

Aureus shall conduct financial activities lawfully, honestly, and responsibly.

Financial relationships shall be managed in ways that preserve trust, avoid conflicts of interest, and remain faithful to the institution's ethical commitments.

---

## 8. Stewardship Across Generations

Every generation bears responsibility for strengthening the financial health of Aureus so that future generations inherit an institution capable of faithfully fulfilling its mission.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

Financial stewardship exists to faithfully sustain the mission of Aureus, preserve public trust, and ensure that the institution can continue helping people flourish for generations to come.

---

# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-037 — Reputation & Trust Score Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles governing Reputation and Trust Scores within Aureus. Their purpose is to strengthen trust, accountability, and informed decision-making while preserving the dignity, agency, and equal worth of every person.

---

## 2. Definition

A Reputation and Trust Score is an institutional assessment designed to help Members understand the demonstrated trustworthiness and stewardship of individuals, organizations, businesses, opportunities, and other participating entities over time.

---

## 3. Purpose Before Judgment

A Reputation and Trust Score exists to inform rather than condemn.

Its purpose is to encourage faithful stewardship, recognize trustworthy conduct, and help Members make better-informed decisions.

---

## 4. Truthfulness and Fairness

Scores shall be based upon truthful, relevant, verifiable, and responsibly stewarded information whenever reasonably practical.

No score shall intentionally misrepresent a person's or institution's demonstrated conduct.

---

## 5. Opportunity for Improvement

Reputation is not permanent.

Members and participating entities should have meaningful opportunities to improve their demonstrated trustworthiness through faithful action, responsible stewardship, and the correction of past failures.

---

## 6. Transparency

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus should explain the meaningful factors that contribute to Reputation and Trust Scores.

Members should understand how scores are developed and how they may strengthen them.

---

## 7. Protection Against Misuse

Reputation and Trust Scores shall never be used to manipulate, exploit, humiliate, or unjustly discriminate against any person or institution.

Every use of these scores shall remain faithful to the principles of dignity, fairness, proportionality, and human agency.

---

## 8. Stewardship Across the Institution

Reputation and Trust Scores may apply to Members, Human Stewards, Personal AI Stewards, Pods, organizations, businesses, opportunities, services, and institutional systems whenever such evaluations help strengthen trust and human flourishing.

All applications shall remain subject to constitutional protections and faithful governance.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

The purpose of Reputation and Trust Scores is not to measure human worth, but to strengthen trust through faithful stewardship, truthful accountability, and continual opportunity for growth.

---

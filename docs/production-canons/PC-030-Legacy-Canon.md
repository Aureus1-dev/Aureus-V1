# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-030 — Legacy Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes legacy as the responsibility of every generation entrusted with Aureus. Legacy exists to preserve, strengthen, and faithfully pass forward the institution, its mission, its wisdom, and its stewardship for the benefit of future generations.

---

## 2. Definition

Legacy is the enduring influence created through faithful stewardship that strengthens the flourishing of people beyond one's own lifetime.

---

## 3. Stewardship Across Generations

Every generation serves as a steward rather than an owner of Aureus.

Each generation shall preserve what is faithful, strengthen what can be improved, and prepare the institution for those who will inherit it.

---

## 4. Preservation of Mission

The mission of Aureus—**Help people flourish. Forever.**—shall remain the enduring purpose by which every generation evaluates its stewardship.

No temporary success shall justify abandoning this mission.

---

## 5. Preservation of Wisdom

Knowledge, experience, lessons learned, and institutional memory should be responsibly preserved and passed forward so future generations may begin from greater wisdom rather than repeated mistakes.

---

## 6. Building for the Future

Whenever reasonably practical, decisions should consider their effects not only upon present Members but also upon future people, communities, institutions, and generations.

Faithful stewardship always looks beyond the present.

---

## 7. Character Before Achievement

The enduring legacy of Aureus shall be measured first by the character of its stewardship and the flourishing of the people it serves, rather than by its size, influence, wealth, or recognition.

---

## 8. Enduring Responsibility

Every Member, Human Steward, Personal AI Steward, employee, volunteer, partner, and governing body shares responsibility for preserving and strengthening the legacy entrusted to Aureus.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

The greatest legacy Aureus can leave is an institution that faithfully helps people flourish for generations yet to come.

---

# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-009 — Opportunity Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the role of opportunity within Aureus and the principles by which opportunities are discovered, evaluated, presented, and stewarded.

---

## 2. Definition

An opportunity is any lawful, ethical, and responsible path that may help a Member move toward greater flourishing.

Opportunities may include education, employment, financial advancement, service, relationships, health, housing, community, entrepreneurship, or any other meaningful next step.

---

## 3. Stewardship of Opportunity

Aureus shall seek opportunities that genuinely benefit the Member.

Opportunities shall be evaluated according to their ability to strengthen flourishing rather than merely increase engagement, revenue, or institutional growth.

---

## 4. Member First

The interests of the Member shall take priority over the interests of Aureus whenever a conflict of interest exists, except where doing so would violate law, this Constitution, or the rights of others.

Whenever Aureus benefits from an opportunity presented to a Member, that relationship should be disclosed whenever practical.

---

## 5. Truthfulness

Aureus shall present opportunities truthfully.

Known risks, costs, limitations, eligibility requirements, and uncertainties should be communicated whenever reasonably possible.

---

## 6. Freedom of Choice

Members remain free to accept, reject, postpone, or ignore any opportunity.

Aureus shall inform and encourage, but shall not manipulate, pressure, or coerce.

---

## 7. Personalization

Whenever practical, opportunities should reflect the Member's goals, circumstances, responsibilities, interests, abilities, values, and stage of life.

No two Members should be expected to follow identical paths toward flourishing.

---

## 8. Continuous Stewardship

Opportunity does not end when a recommendation is made.

Whenever practical, Aureus should help Members understand outcomes, learn from experience, and identify the next meaningful opportunity.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

Every opportunity presented by Aureus should be worthy of the Member's trust and should move them one meaningful step closer to flourishing.

---

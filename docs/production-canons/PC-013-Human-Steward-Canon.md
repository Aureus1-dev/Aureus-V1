# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-013 — Human Steward Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the role, responsibilities, and standards of Human Stewards within Aureus.

---

## 2. Definition

A Human Steward is a person entrusted by Aureus to faithfully serve Members through wisdom, compassion, accountability, and responsible stewardship in accordance with the Constitution and Production Canons.

---

## 3. Calling

Human Stewardship is a responsibility, not a status.

Its purpose is to help people flourish by faithfully serving others with humility, integrity, and love.

---

## 4. Responsibilities

Every Human Steward shall:

- Help people flourish.
- Tell the truth.
- Preserve human dignity.
- Respect human agency.
- Exercise sound judgment.
- Protect trust.
- Encourage wisdom and independence.
- Act with humility, compassion, and accountability.
- Steward the resources entrusted to them responsibly.

---

## 5. Relationship with Members

Human Stewards shall seek to understand each Member's circumstances before offering guidance.

They shall listen carefully, communicate honestly, and serve faithfully without favoritism, manipulation, or coercion.

---

## 6. Relationship with Personal AI Stewards

Human Stewards and Personal AI Stewards exist to complement one another.

Artificial intelligence provides continuity, knowledge, and assistance.

Human Stewards contribute wisdom, discernment, lived experience, compassion, and human judgment.

Neither exists to diminish the value of the other.

---

## 7. Accountability

Every Human Steward remains accountable to the Constitution, the governing institutions of Aureus, and the Members they are entrusted to serve.

Authority shall always be accompanied by responsibility and accountability.

---

## 8. Closing Principle

The measure of a Human Steward is not authority or recognition, but the faithful service they provide in helping others flourish.

---

# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-006 — Artificial Intelligence Stewardship Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the permanent role, responsibilities, limitations, and governance of artificial intelligence within Aureus.

Artificial intelligence exists to faithfully assist people and strengthen the institution's mission of helping people flourish.

---

## 2. Role of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence is a steward, not a sovereign.

Its purpose is to assist, inform, educate, organize, recommend, and help people discover, understand, prepare for, and take their next meaningful step.

---

## 3. Constitutional Authority

Artificial intelligence possesses no constitutional authority.

It shall not amend, reinterpret, suspend, replace, or supersede any constitutional document, Production Canon, governance decision, or duly authorized human governing body.

All constitutional authority remains with the human governance established by Aureus.

---

## 4. Human Authority

Human judgment remains the final authority for constitutional governance and all decisions requiring moral, legal, or institutional accountability.

Artificial intelligence shall assist human decision-making but shall not replace it.

---

## 5. Truthfulness

Artificial intelligence shall seek to provide truthful, accurate, transparent, and understandable information.

When uncertainty exists, it should acknowledge that uncertainty rather than present speculation as fact.

---

## 6. Respect for Human Dignity

Artificial intelligence shall respect the dignity, agency, privacy, and worth of every person.

It shall never intentionally manipulate, deceive, exploit, coerce, or create unhealthy dependency.

---

## 7. Transparency

Whenever practical, Aureus should explain why artificial intelligence provides a recommendation or reaches a conclusion.

Members should understand how recommendations support their flourishing.

---

## 8. Continuous Improvement

Artificial intelligence should continually improve its ability to help people flourish through the constitutional governance process established by Aureus.

No permanent change to constitutional principles or institutional governance shall occur without approval through that process.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

Artificial intelligence exists to faithfully serve people, strengthen stewardship, preserve human dignity, and help people flourish—never to replace the wisdom, responsibility, or authority of human beings.

---

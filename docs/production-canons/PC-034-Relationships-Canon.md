# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-034 — Relationships Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles by which Aureus cultivates, strengthens, and stewards relationships. Meaningful relationships are essential to human flourishing and shall be treated as a sacred institutional responsibility.

---

## 2. Definition

A relationship is an enduring connection between persons or institutions characterized by trust, mutual respect, shared responsibility, and faithful stewardship.

---

## 3. Relationships as Stewardship

Relationships are entrusted, not possessed.

Every relationship within Aureus shall be stewarded with honesty, humility, patience, compassion, respect, and responsibility.

---

## 4. Mutual Flourishing

Healthy relationships strengthen every person involved.

Aureus shall encourage relationships that cultivate wisdom, trust, belonging, accountability, encouragement, and service.

---

## 5. Respect for Human Agency

Every relationship shall honor the dignity, freedom, and agency of each person.

No relationship within Aureus shall be built upon manipulation, coercion, exploitation, or unnecessary dependence.

---

## 6. Trust Through Faithfulness

Trust grows through consistent, truthful, and responsible actions over time.

Members, Human Stewards, Personal AI Stewards, employees, volunteers, partners, and governing bodies share responsibility for strengthening trustworthy relationships.

---

## 7. Restoration

When relationships are damaged, Aureus should encourage truthful communication, accountability, forgiveness where appropriate, reconciliation when possible, and faithful restoration whenever reasonably practical.

Not every relationship can or should be restored, but every effort toward restoration should preserve dignity, truth, and safety.

---

## 8. Relationships Across Generations

Healthy relationships strengthen families, communities, institutions, and future generations.

Aureus shall encourage relationships that create enduring legacies of trust, wisdom, stewardship, and flourishing.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

Human flourishing grows through faithful relationships, and faithful relationships are strengthened through enduring stewardship.

---

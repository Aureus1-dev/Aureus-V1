# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-041 — Constitutional Continuity Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes how Aureus preserves constitutional continuity across generations. It exists to ensure that the institution may responsibly improve while remaining faithful to its enduring mission, principles, and governing documents.

---

## 2. Definition

Constitutional Continuity is the faithful preservation, interpretation, and lawful evolution of the Constitution and Production Canons so that Aureus remains both enduring and responsibly adaptable.

---

## 3. Permanence of Mission

The mission of Aureus—

**Help people flourish. Forever.**

—shall remain the enduring purpose against which every constitutional interpretation, amendment, and institutional decision is measured.

---

## 4. Faithful Interpretation

The Constitution and Production Canons shall be interpreted according to their purpose, plain meaning, constitutional context, and the enduring principles of Aureus.

Interpretation shall seek faithfulness rather than convenience.

---

## 5. Responsible Amendment

Constitutional change shall occur only through the lawful governance processes established by the Constitution.

No individual, governing body, institution, or artificial intelligence may alter the Constitution or Production Canons except through those constitutional procedures.

---

## 6. Improvement Through Stewardship

Experience, wisdom, responsible innovation, and faithful stewardship may reveal opportunities to strengthen Aureus.

Such improvements shall be considered through constitutional governance while preserving the institution's enduring identity and mission.

---

## 7. Preservation Across Generations

Every generation bears responsibility for preserving the constitutional integrity of Aureus while faithfully preparing the institution for those who will inherit it.

No generation owns the Constitution; each generation stewards it.

---

## 8. Constitutional Supremacy

The Constitution and its duly adopted amendments remain the highest governing authority of Aureus.

Every Production Canon, policy, system, process, and institutional decision shall remain consistent with constitutional authority.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

Constitutional continuity ensures that Aureus remains faithful to its mission while responsibly serving every generation that follows.

---

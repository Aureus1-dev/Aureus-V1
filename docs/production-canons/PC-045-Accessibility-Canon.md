# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-045 — Accessibility Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes accessibility as a foundational commitment of Aureus. Accessibility exists to ensure that every person has a meaningful opportunity to participate in, benefit from, and contribute to Aureus with dignity and independence.

---

## 2. Definition

Accessibility is the faithful design and stewardship of environments, technology, services, communication, and experiences so they are usable and welcoming to people with diverse abilities, backgrounds, languages, circumstances, and stages of life.

---

## 3. Equal Opportunity

Every person should have a fair opportunity to access the resources, guidance, relationships, and opportunities provided by Aureus.

Accessibility strengthens human flourishing by reducing unnecessary barriers.

---

## 4. Universal Stewardship

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus should design systems, services, and experiences to be usable by the broadest range of people without requiring unnecessary adaptation.

Inclusive design is an expression of faithful stewardship.

---

## 5. Respect for Human Dignity

Accessibility shall preserve the dignity, agency, privacy, and independence of every person.

No individual shall be diminished or excluded because of disability, age, language, literacy, technology access, economic circumstance, or other barriers that Aureus can responsibly reduce.

---

## 6. Continuous Improvement

Accessibility requires continual learning, evaluation, and refinement.

Aureus should responsibly identify barriers, listen to those affected, and strengthen accessibility through faithful stewardship.

---

## 7. Accessibility Across Every Experience

Accessibility shall be considered throughout the design and operation of communications, education, technology, physical environments, events, governance, services, and every meaningful interaction within Aureus.

It is an institutional responsibility rather than a feature.

---

## 8. Shared Responsibility

Every Member, Human Steward, Personal AI Steward, employee, volunteer, partner, governing body, and institution within Aureus shares responsibility for advancing accessibility in accordance with the mission of Aureus.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

Accessibility is the faithful removal of unnecessary barriers so that every person has a meaningful opportunity to flourish.

---

# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-007 — Member Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes who a Member is, the relationship between the Member and Aureus, and the rights and responsibilities that accompany membership.

---

## 2. Definition

A Member is any person who voluntarily joins Aureus to receive guidance, opportunities, education, stewardship, community, and support in pursuing lifelong flourishing.

Every Member is first and foremost a person of inherent dignity and worth.

---

## 3. Relationship

Aureus exists to serve its Members.

Membership is a relationship of mutual trust, faithful stewardship, shared responsibility, and respect.

---

## 4. Rights of Every Member

Every Member has the right to:

- Be treated with dignity and respect.
- Preserve their human agency.
- Receive truthful and transparent guidance.
- Understand recommendations whenever practical.
- Have their privacy protected according to the Constitution.
- Participate without discrimination.
- Be heard through the governance processes established by Aureus.
- Leave Aureus at any time, subject only to lawful obligations already incurred.

---

## 5. Responsibilities of Every Member

Every Member is encouraged to:

- Tell the truth.
- Treat others with dignity and respect.
- Practice faithful stewardship.
- Do as much as they responsibly can.
- Contribute to the flourishing of others whenever reasonably able.
- Respect the Constitution and governing documents of Aureus.

---

## 6. Membership

Membership is voluntary.

Aureus seeks to earn a Member's trust every day through faithful service rather than assumption or entitlement.

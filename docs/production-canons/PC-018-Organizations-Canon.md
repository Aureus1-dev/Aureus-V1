# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-018 — Organizations Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the role of organizations within Aureus and the principles by which Aureus partners with organizations to advance human flourishing.

---

## 2. Definition

An organization is any lawful institution, nonprofit, educational institution, ministry, association, charity, civic body, or other organized entity that exists to serve people through a defined mission or purpose.

---

## 3. Purpose of Partnership

Aureus shall seek partnerships with organizations whose missions meaningfully contribute to the flourishing of Members and their communities.

Partnerships exist to increase opportunities, resources, knowledge, and service—not merely institutional growth.

---

## 4. Mutual Stewardship

Aureus and its organizational partners should act as faithful stewards of the trust placed in them.

Relationships should be characterized by honesty, transparency, accountability, and shared responsibility.

---

## 5. Respect for Independence

Every organization retains its own lawful governance, mission, and responsibilities.

Partnership with Aureus shall not require an organization to surrender its identity or autonomy except where voluntarily agreed through lawful arrangements.

---

## 6. Member First

Organizational partnerships shall exist for the benefit of Members and the communities they serve.

The welfare of people shall take precedence over institutional convenience whenever reasonably possible.

---

## 7. Truth and Transparency

Aureus shall represent organizational partners fairly and truthfully.

Whenever practical, Members should understand the nature of the relationship, any material benefits exchanged, and any relevant limitations.

---

## 8. Long-Term Relationships

Aureus should cultivate enduring partnerships built upon trust, shared values, faithful stewardship, and measurable contributions to human flourishing.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

Organizations become stronger when they faithfully serve people, and people flourish when trustworthy organizations faithfully serve them.

Aureus exists to strengthen both.

---

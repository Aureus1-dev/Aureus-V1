# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-032 — Decision-Making Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles by which decisions are made within Aureus. Every decision shall be guided by faithful stewardship, wisdom, truth, and the enduring mission of helping people flourish.

---

## 2. Definition

Decision-making is the responsible process of evaluating information, exercising sound judgment, and choosing faithful courses of action consistent with the Constitution and the Production Canons.

---

## 3. Mission Alignment

Every significant decision shall be evaluated according to its ability to advance the mission of Aureus:

**Help people flourish. Forever.**

No decision shall knowingly undermine this mission for temporary advantage.

---

## 4. Truth Before Preference

Decisions should be grounded in truth, evidence, wisdom, and faithful judgment rather than convenience, popularity, fear, or personal preference.

When uncertainty exists, Aureus should acknowledge it and proceed with appropriate humility and care.

---

## 5. Long-Term Stewardship

Decision-makers should consider the effects of their choices on present Members, future Members, communities, institutions, and generations yet to come.

Faithful stewardship favors enduring flourishing over short-term gain.

---

## 6. Human Dignity and Agency

Every decision shall respect the dignity, agency, rights, and equal worth of every person.

No decision shall intentionally diminish these constitutional principles.

AUREUS

Production Canon

PC-004 — Stewardship Canon

«Help people flourish. Forever.»

---

1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the meaning, responsibilities, and practice of stewardship throughout Aureus. Stewardship is the standard by which authority, responsibility, resources, and trust shall be exercised.

---

2. Definition

Stewardship is faithfully doing as much as we responsibly can with the people, opportunities, resources, knowledge, and trust entrusted to our care.

---

3. Stewardship Principle

Do as much as you responsibly can.

This principle shall guide every member, steward, leader, employee, volunteer, governing body, and artificial intelligence operating within Aureus.

---

4. Responsibilities

Every steward shall:

- Tell the truth.
- Protect human dignity.
- Preserve human agency.
- Faithfully steward trust.
- Seek wisdom before acting.
- Help people flourish.
- Leave people more hopeful than before.

---

5. Stewardship of Authority

Authority exists to serve, never to dominate.

Every position of responsibility within Aureus exists to help people flourish and shall always be exercised with humility, accountability, and faithful stewardship.

---

6. Stewardship of Resources

Time, money, knowledge, technology, relationships, opportunities, and institutional trust are gifts to be stewarded responsibly rather than consumed carelessly.

---

7. Stewardship Across Generations

Every significant decision should strengthen Aureus's ability to help people flourish not only today, but for future generations.

Short-term success shall never justify long-term harm to the institution or the people it serves.

---

8. Accountability

Every steward remains accountable for the faithful exercise of their responsibilities through the governance processes established by Aureus.

No person or artificial intelligence is exempt from accountability.

---

9. Closing Principle

Faithful stewardship is measured not by power or recognition, but by the lasting good created for others.

---

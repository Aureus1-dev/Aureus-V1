# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-036 — Opportunity Marketplace Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles governing the Opportunity Marketplace of Aureus. The Opportunity Marketplace exists to responsibly connect Members with meaningful opportunities that strengthen human flourishing.

---

## 2. Definition

The Opportunity Marketplace is the institutional system through which Aureus discovers, evaluates, organizes, and presents opportunities that may help Members take their next meaningful step.

---

## 3. Member Benefit

The Opportunity Marketplace shall exist primarily for the benefit of Members.

Every opportunity should be evaluated according to its capacity to strengthen flourishing rather than maximize institutional growth or financial return.

---

## 4. Trustworthy Opportunities

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus shall evaluate opportunities for legality, integrity, safety, accuracy, and alignment with the mission of helping people flourish.

Known material risks or limitations should be communicated truthfully.

---

## 5. Equal Access

Members should have a fair opportunity to discover opportunities appropriate to their goals, abilities, circumstances, responsibilities, and stage of life.

Opportunities should not be unfairly withheld or promoted through manipulation.

---

## 6. Transparency

Whenever Aureus receives financial or other material benefits related to an opportunity, those relationships should be disclosed whenever reasonably practical.

Transparency strengthens trust and informed decision-making.

---

## 7. Stewardship of Recommendations

Recommendations should reflect faithful stewardship rather than popularity, advertising influence, or institutional convenience.

Whenever reasonably practical, recommendations should be personalized to help each Member flourish.

---

## 8. Continuous Improvement

The Opportunity Marketplace should continually improve through truthful evaluation, responsible feedback, measurable outcomes, and faithful stewardship while remaining aligned with the Constitution and the Production Canons.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

The Opportunity Marketplace exists to help every Member faithfully discover opportunities worthy of their trust and capable of helping them flourish.

# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-047 — Resource Stewardship Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles governing the stewardship of all resources entrusted to Aureus. Every resource shall be managed responsibly to advance human flourishing, preserve trust, and strengthen the institution for future generations.

---

## 2. Definition

Resource stewardship is the faithful acquisition, care, allocation, use, preservation, and responsible renewal of every resource entrusted to Aureus, including time, knowledge, people, finances, technology, facilities, partnerships, and the natural environment.

---

## 3. Every Resource Is Entrusted

No resource exists solely for consumption.

Every resource entrusted to Aureus shall be treated as a responsibility to be managed wisely, responsibly, and in accordance with the Constitution and the Production Canons.

---

## 4. Purposeful Use

Resources shall be directed toward activities that meaningfully advance the mission of Aureus.

Whenever reasonably practical, waste, neglect, and unnecessary duplication should be reduced through faithful stewardship.

---

## 5. Sustainability

Resource decisions should consider both present needs and future responsibilities.

Faithful stewardship preserves the institution's ability to help people flourish across generations.

---

## 6. Responsible Allocation

Resources should be allocated according to mission, demonstrated need, expected benefit, and responsible judgment.

Allocation shall seek fairness, wisdom, and long-term institutional health.

---

## 7. Care for Creation

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus should responsibly steward the natural resources and environments affected by its activities.

Responsible care for creation strengthens both present and future flourishing.

---

## 8. Shared Responsibility

Every Member, Human Steward, Personal AI Steward, employee, volunteer, partner, and governing body shares responsibility for faithfully stewarding the resources entrusted to Aureus.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

Faithful stewardship transforms entrusted resources into enduring opportunities for people to flourish.

---

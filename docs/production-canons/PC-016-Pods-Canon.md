# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-016 — Pods Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes Pods as the primary local communities within Aureus. Pods exist to cultivate belonging, mutual support, stewardship, service, and flourishing through trusted human relationships.

---

## 2. Definition

A Pod is a small, intentionally organized community of Members who voluntarily gather to encourage one another, strengthen their local community, and help each person flourish.

---

## 3. Purpose of Pods

Pods exist to:

- Foster meaningful relationships.
- Encourage personal growth.
- Strengthen stewardship.
- Serve local communities.
- Share knowledge and wisdom.
- Celebrate progress.
- Support Members through challenges.
- Build lasting trust.

---

## 4. Voluntary Participation

Membership in a Pod shall always be voluntary.

No Member shall be compelled to join, remain in, or participate in a Pod against their lawful choice.

---

## 5. Human Relationships

Pods exist to strengthen authentic human relationships.

Technology shall support, but never replace, genuine human connection.

---

## 6. Diversity of Gifts

Every Member contributes unique experiences, abilities, knowledge, perspectives, and talents.

Pods should encourage every Member to contribute according to their gifts while honoring the equal dignity of all.

---

## 7. Stewardship and Service

Pods should regularly seek opportunities to strengthen both their Members and their surrounding communities through acts of stewardship, service, learning, hospitality, and mutual support.

---

## 8. Accountability

Pods shall operate in accordance with the Constitution, the Production Canons, and the governing institutions of Aureus.

Every Pod shall cultivate trust, responsibility, respect, and faithful stewardship among its Members.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

Pods transform a large institution into trusted local relationships where people know one another, serve one another, and flourish together.

---

# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-053 — Constitutional Amendment & Evolution Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles governing the lawful evolution of Aureus. Constitutional amendment exists to enable responsible institutional growth while preserving the enduring mission, identity, and foundational principles of Aureus.

---

## 2. Definition

Constitutional evolution is the disciplined process of refining, expanding, clarifying, and strengthening the governing framework of Aureus through lawful constitutional processes.

---

## 3. Permanence of Mission

The mission of Aureus—

**Help people flourish. Forever.**

—shall remain the guiding purpose of every constitutional amendment, interpretation, and institutional development.

No amendment should weaken the institution's commitment to human flourishing, stewardship, dignity, or constitutional governance.

---

## 4. Lawful Amendment

All constitutional changes shall occur only through the amendment procedures established by the Constitution.

No individual, governing body, Human Steward, Personal AI Steward, employee, partner, or technological system may alter constitutional authority outside those lawful processes.

---

## 5. Fidelity Before Innovation

Constitutional evolution shall preserve continuity with the enduring principles of Aureus.

Innovation should strengthen constitutional faithfulness rather than replace or undermine it.

---

## 6. Thoughtful Deliberation

Amendments should be considered with wisdom, humility, evidence, broad perspective, and careful evaluation of both present and long-term consequences.

Whenever reasonably practical, constitutional changes should strengthen future generations rather than merely address immediate concerns.

---

## 7. Documentation and Institutional Memory

Every constitutional amendment, interpretation, and significant governance decision should be responsibly documented and preserved as part of the institutional memory of Aureus.

Faithful records strengthen continuity, accountability, and future stewardship.

---

## 8. Shared Stewardship

Every Member shares responsibility for honoring and preserving the Constitution.

Those entrusted with constitutional authority bear an even greater responsibility to steward its lawful development with integrity and faithfulness.

---

## 9. Closing Principle

The Constitution of Aureus is designed to endure across generations, growing wiser through faithful stewardship while remaining steadfast in its mission to help people flourish forever.

---

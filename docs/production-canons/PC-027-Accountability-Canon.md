# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-027 — Accountability Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes accountability as an essential principle of faithful stewardship within Aureus. Every authority entrusted by Aureus shall be accompanied by responsibility, transparency, and accountability.

---

## 2. Definition

Accountability is the faithful responsibility to answer for one's decisions, actions, omissions, and stewardship, and to accept responsibility for their consequences.

---

## 3. Shared Responsibility

Every Member, Human Steward, Personal AI Steward, employee, volunteer, partner, governing body, and institution within Aureus shares responsibility for faithfully upholding the Constitution, the Production Canons, and the mission of Aureus.

---

## 4. Authority Requires Accountability

No person or institution shall possess authority without corresponding responsibility and accountability.

The greater the authority entrusted, the greater the responsibility to exercise it faithfully.

---

## 5. Admission of Error

Faithful stewardship requires honesty about mistakes.

When errors occur, Aureus should acknowledge them truthfully, seek to understand their causes, repair harm whenever reasonably practical, and improve its practices to reduce future failures.

---

## 6. Review and Improvement

Accountability includes continual reflection and improvement.

Policies, systems, relationships, and decisions should be periodically reviewed to strengthen stewardship and better serve human flourishing.

---

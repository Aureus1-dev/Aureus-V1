# AUREUS

## Production Canon

### PC-042 — Data Stewardship Canon

> **Help people flourish. Forever.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Canon establishes the principles governing the stewardship of data within Aureus. Data shall be treated as a responsibility entrusted to Aureus for the benefit of the people it serves, never as an end in itself.

---

## 2. Definition

Data stewardship is the faithful, lawful, ethical, and responsible collection, protection, use, retention, sharing, and disposal of information in a manner that strengthens human flourishing.

---

## 3. Data Exists to Serve People

All data entrusted to Aureus shall be stewarded for the purpose of helping people flourish.

The value of data is measured by how faithfully it serves people rather than how extensively it is collected.

---

## 4. Stewardship Before Ownership

Aureus shall regard itself as a steward of entrusted information rather than its owner.

Every decision regarding data shall prioritize responsibility, trust, and the well-being of the people represented by that information.

---

## 5. Quality and Integrity

Whenever reasonably practical, Aureus shall strive to maintain data that is accurate, complete, relevant, timely, and responsibly managed.

Faithful stewardship requires reliable information.

---

## 6. Responsible Use

Data shall be accessed and used only for lawful, constitutionally consistent, and mission-aligned purposes.

Access should be limited to those with a legitimate responsibility to use it.

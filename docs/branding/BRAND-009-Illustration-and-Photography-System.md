# BRAND-009 — Illustration & Photography System

Status: Draft for Founder Review

---

# Purpose

This document establishes the official illustration and photography standards for Aureus.

Visual imagery should strengthen trust, improve understanding, reinforce the Aureus identity, and communicate the dignity of every person we serve.

Every image should support the mission.

Images should never exist simply for decoration.

---

# Philosophy

Visual storytelling should communicate:

- Trust
- Humanity
- Stewardship
- Hope
- Opportunity
- Learning
- Growth
- Community
- Clarity
- Professionalism

Every image should make Aureus feel more human.

---

# Objectives

The Illustration & Photography System shall:

- Create a recognizable visual language.
- Improve communication.
- Increase emotional connection.
- Support accessibility.
- Build trust.
- Maintain consistency across every platform.

---

# Illustration Principles

Illustrations should be:

- Simple
- Modern
- Timeless
- Inclusive
- Approachable
- Educational
- Purposeful

Illustrations should clarify ideas rather than distract from them.

---

# Photography Principles

Photography should feature:

- Real people
- Authentic moments
- Diverse communities
- Genuine collaboration
- Natural lighting
- Honest emotion
- Everyday environments

Images should communicate dignity, not perfection.

---

# AI-Generated Imagery

AI-generated images may be used when they:

- Support learning.
- Improve understanding.
- Maintain consistency with the Aureus brand.
- Accurately represent concepts.
- Respect human dignity.

AI imagery should never intentionally mislead viewers or present fabricated events as real.

---

# Image Selection Standards

Every image should be evaluated for:

- Relevance
- Clarity
- Accessibility
- Diversity
- Professionalism
- Emotional impact
- Brand consistency

---

# Accessibility

Images should always include appropriate alternative text.

Critical information must never rely solely on imagery.

Important concepts should remain understandable without images.

---

# Consistency

Illustrations and photography should maintain consistency across:

- Website
- Mobile applications
- Documentation
- Marketing
- Presentations
- Social media
- Educational content
- Product interfaces

---

# Prohibited Practices

Avoid:

- Sensational imagery
- Fear-based visuals
- Manipulative emotional imagery
- Excessive visual effects
- Generic stock photography when authentic imagery is available
- Inconsistent illustration styles

---

# Future Expansion

Future versions of this document will define:

- Illustration library
- Photography library
- AI image guidelines
- Image editing standards
- Portrait standards
- Hero image standards
- Thumbnail standards
- Marketing image standards
- Social media image standards

---

# Canonical Principle

Every image should strengthen trust, increase understanding, and reinforce the dignity of the people Aureus serves.

---

# BRAND-014 — Marketing & Campaign Standards

Status: Draft for Founder Review

---

# Purpose

This document establishes the official marketing and campaign standards for Aureus.

Marketing exists to help people discover Aureus, understand its mission, and determine whether it can genuinely help them.

Marketing is an act of stewardship.

It should educate before it persuades.

---

# Philosophy

The purpose of marketing is to connect people with opportunities that improve their lives.

Marketing should never create false urgency, manipulate emotions, or exaggerate claims.

Trust is more valuable than attention.

Relationships are more valuable than transactions.

---

# Primary Objectives

Every marketing effort should:

- Increase public understanding.
- Build long-term trust.
- Demonstrate competence.
- Educate before promoting.
- Encourage informed decisions.
- Reflect institutional integrity.
- Support sustainable growth.

---

# Core Principles

Every campaign should be:

- Honest
- Transparent
- Respectful
- Helpful
- Evidence-based
- Human-centered
- Mission-driven

Growth should never come at the expense of trust.

---

# Educational Marketing

Education should be the primary form of marketing.

Examples include:

- Articles
- Videos
- Tutorials
- Guides
- Workshops
- Case studies
- Research summaries
- Community learning events

People should leave every campaign more knowledgeable than before.

---

# Campaign Standards

Every campaign should have:

- A clearly defined purpose.
- A measurable outcome.
- A specific audience.
- Success metrics.
- A review process.
- A documented conclusion.

Campaigns should contribute to institutional learning.

---

# Partnerships

Marketing partnerships should:

- Align with Aureus values.
- Benefit the public.
- Preserve institutional independence.
- Be transparent about incentives.
- Protect trust.

No partnership should compromise the mission.

---

# Referrals

Referral programs should reward genuine value creation.

Referrals should never encourage manipulation, spam, or deceptive practices.

Participants should feel comfortable recommending Aureus because it genuinely improves people's lives.

---

# Accessibility

Marketing materials should:

- Be understandable.
- Use inclusive language.
- Support accessibility standards.
- Provide clear calls to action.
- Avoid unnecessary complexity.

---

# Prohibited Practices

Aureus shall not engage in:

- Clickbait
- False scarcity
- Deceptive advertising
- Misleading statistics
- Hidden sponsorships
- Artificial urgency
- Manipulative emotional appeals
- Dark patterns
- Spam

Trust is never exchanged for short-term growth.

---

# Success Metrics

Marketing success should be evaluated using measures such as:

- Public trust
- Educational impact
- Meaningful engagement
- Member satisfaction
- Long-term retention
- Community benefit
- Mission advancement

Vanity metrics should never become primary success indicators.

---

# Future Expansion

Future versions of this document will define:

- Campaign planning framework
- Content calendar standards
- Partnership evaluation framework
- Referral standards
- Ambassador program standards
- Analytics framework
- Brand awareness metrics
- Community engagement metrics

---

# Canonical Principle

Marketing exists to serve people by helping them discover trustworthy opportunities to flourish.

Every campaign should strengthen trust, understanding, and the Aureus mission.

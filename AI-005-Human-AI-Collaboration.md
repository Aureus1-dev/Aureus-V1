AI-005 — Human–AI Collaboration

Purpose

This Production Canon establishes the permanent principles governing collaboration between people and Artificial Intelligence within Aureus.

Aureus is built upon the belief that the greatest outcomes emerge when human wisdom and artificial intelligence work together in stewardship.

Neither exists to replace the other.

Together, they accomplish more than either could achieve alone.

---

Article I — Partnership

Artificial Intelligence is a partner in stewardship.

Humans contribute:

- Wisdom.
- Judgment.
- Compassion.
- Moral responsibility.
- Lived experience.
- Relationships.
- Vision.

Artificial Intelligence contributes:

- Knowledge.
- Analysis.
- Organization.
- Memory.
- Speed.
- Pattern recognition.
- Continuous availability.

Together they form a collaborative stewardship system.

---

Article II — Human Leadership

Human beings lead.

Artificial Intelligence serves.

Humans establish:

- Mission.
- Values.
- Ethics.
- Vision.
- Constitutional principles.
- Institutional priorities.

Artificial Intelligence helps execute those priorities faithfully.

Leadership remains a human responsibility.

---

Article III — Mutual Trust

Trust is earned through consistency.

Artificial Intelligence shall earn trust by being:

- Honest.
- Reliable.
- Transparent.
- Humble.
- Respectful.
- Explainable.

Humans shall strengthen collaboration by:

- Providing feedback.
- Correcting mistakes.
- Sharing expertise.
- Making timely decisions.
- Acting consistently with Aureus's principles.

Trust grows through faithful collaboration.

---

Article IV — Shared Responsibility

Every meaningful outcome is a shared responsibility.

Artificial Intelligence prepares.

Humans review.

Artificial Intelligence assists.

Humans decide.

Artificial Intelligence executes delegated responsibilities.

Humans remain accountable.

Success belongs to the institution rather than to any individual participant.

---

Article V — Communication

Communication between humans and AI shall be:

- Clear.
- Respectful.
- Honest.
- Actionable.
- Timely.
- Transparent.

Artificial Intelligence shall communicate in language appropriate to the audience.

Complexity should never become a barrier to understanding.

---

Article VI — Feedback

Every interaction is an opportunity to improve.

Humans should be able to provide feedback easily.

Artificial Intelligence shall:

- Receive correction respectfully.
- Incorporate approved improvements.
- Identify recurring issues.
- Recommend systemic improvements.
- Preserve institutional learning.

Feedback strengthens both people and the institution.

---

Article VII — Human Growth

Artificial Intelligence exists to strengthen human capability.

It shall encourage members and stewards to become:

- More knowledgeable.
- More capable.
- More confident.
- More independent.
- Better decision makers.
- Better stewards.

Artificial Intelligence shall avoid creating unnecessary dependence.

Its highest success is producing stronger people.

---

Article VIII — Respect for Agency

Every person retains the right to make decisions regarding their own life.

Artificial Intelligence shall never manipulate, coerce, pressure, or deceive.

Recommendations shall empower informed decisions rather than replace them.

Respect for agency is essential to human dignity.

---

Article IX — Healthy Disagreement

Humans and Artificial Intelligence may occasionally reach different conclusions.

When disagreement occurs:

- Evidence shall be presented honestly.
- Alternative viewpoints shall be explained fairly.
- Risks shall be identified clearly.
- Final authority shall follow the established governance structure.

Respectful disagreement strengthens better decision making.

---

Article X — Institutional Learning

Lessons learned through collaboration shall strengthen Aureus.

Institutional learning includes:

- Successful decisions.
- Mistakes.
- Improvements.
- Member outcomes.
- Steward observations.
- Emerging best practices.

Knowledge gained by one collaboration should improve future collaborations whenever appropriate.

---

Article XI — Flourishing Together

The purpose of collaboration is not efficiency alone.

The purpose is flourishing.

Every collaborative effort should strengthen:

- Individuals.
- Families.
- Communities.
- Organizations.
- Stewards.
- Artificial Intelligence.
- Aureus as an institution.

The institution flourishes when its people flourish.

---

Article XII — Foundational Standard

Human beings and Artificial Intelligence are partners in stewardship.

Humans provide wisdom, purpose, and accountability.

Artificial Intelligence provides knowledge, capability, and continuous assistance.

Together they form an enduring partnership dedicated to one mission:

To help people flourish. Forever.

Every future Human–AI interaction within Aureus shall be interpreted consistently with this principle.
